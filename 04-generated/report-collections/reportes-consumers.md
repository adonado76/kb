# Report Collections — Technology Consumers Layer

This brief documents the single report collection tied to the Technology Consumers layer: Business Units.

## Business Units Report Collection

Shows the total cost of applications and services by business unit, with assigned values. This gives business units visibility into consumption-driven costs and discretionary investment, making allocations more defensible by distinguishing between direct (consumption) and indirect cost, and making cost understandable and actionable. Includes drill-downs into interconnected detail about applications, services, assets, and projects, with baselines that let you compare cost per FTE and department across business units, and flexibility to match the allocation method to the data available for each application or service.

Four reports:
- **Business Unit Review**
- **Business Unit Portfolio**
- **Business Unit List**
- **Impact of Retiring Applications** — closes the loop with the Application TCO capability: it shows directly how retiring a specific application impacts the cost of the business units that consumed it.
