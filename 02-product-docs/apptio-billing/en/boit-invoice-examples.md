# Billing Standard invoice examples

Within
Billing Standard
, it is possible to send service consumers either a Summary of Detailed invoice by email.

The Summary invoice provides a high level view of charge and budget and then a list of changes within that invoice. Whereas the detailed invoice shows a detail of all services charged.

The links below let you download PDF copies of the email that is sent. They are not PDFs
exported from the system, and so not optimized for PDF paging.

- Summary invoice
