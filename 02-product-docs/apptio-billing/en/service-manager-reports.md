# Service and product manager reports

Service and product managers are interested in understanding the recovery based on unit rates.

Unit Rate Analysis report

The
Unit Rate Analysis
report provides a service/product manager information on recovery based on unit rates.

1. On the Application menu, click Billing Standard
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the
   Report collection
   menu, click
   IT Service Provider
   .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click the Unit Rate Analysis report.

![](../resources/images/boit_images/boit_multi-topic_applications.jpg)

The chart compares the unit rates charged over time with costs. Are the differences what you planned or expected? The
Unit Details
table gives cost, charge, budget, and forecast information for each unit.
