# Business relationship manager reports

Business relationship managers are interested in understanding business consumption and identifying alternate solutions.

**Understand business consumption**

To understand business consumption, use the
Usage Summary
report.

1. On the Application menu, click Billing Standard
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the
   Report collection
   menu, click
   Service Charges
   .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click
   Usage Summary
   .

![](../resources/images/boit_images/boit_multi-topic_applications.jpg)

Look at business units as a whole, or filter the report for a specific business unit.

- Compare activity to plan using the
  Service Overview
  .
- Spot dramatic shifts in consumption using the
  13 Period Trend
  .
- Compare consumption with the expected demand using the
  All Services
  table.
- Review service charge trends using the
  Service Charges
  chart and table.
- Review budget variances using the
  Budget Variance
  tree map. Expand budget areas to understand what is driving the overage.
- Identify the top outliers by sorting the
  Budget Variance
  table.

**Identify alternate solutions**

To identify alternate solutions, use the
Invoice Overview
report.

1. On the Application menu, click Billing Standard
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the
   Report collection
   menu, click
   Service Charges
   .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click
   Invoice Overview
   .

![](../resources/images/boit_images/boit_business-relationship-manager_alternate-solutions.jpg)

The bottom table provides details by service type. Use the values in the
Effective Price
column to identity services that are cost effective.
