# Generating and distributing bills

Once billing results pass validation, the next step is to generate bill views and
distribute them.

1. **Prepare bill views**
   - Run invoice style reports that show:
     - Consumer.
     - Offering or service.
     - Units, rate, and total charge.
     - Period and basic attributes such as environment or region if needed.
2. **Adjust presentation level**
   - Decide what level of detail each audience needs:
     - High level summary by service for Senior Leaders.
     - Detailed line items for Service or Product Owners.
     - Focused views for specific consumers.
3. **Publish within the front end**
   - Make the relevant Billing reports visible to:
     - Service or Product Owners and Analysts.
     - Consumers and Stakeholders as needed.
4. **Export for offline use**
   - Export selected reports to:
     - Spreadsheet or CSV for local analysis.
     - PDF for archive or distribution where appropriate.

1. **Communicate availability**
   - Notify recipients when bills are ready:
     - Period covered.
     - Where to find reports.
     - Who to contact with questions.

For many organizations, this distribution step is also where a first round of questions
appears. A standard process to answer those questions is part of running the cycle.
