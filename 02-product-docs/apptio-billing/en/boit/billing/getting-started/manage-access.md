# Managing access over time

Access rarely stays static. People change roles, new services are added, and new
Billing capabilities are rolled out.

When updating permissions:

- Verify that new users have the right front-end permissions for the Billing report
  collections they need.
- For Admins and Analysts taking on configuration responsibilities, ensure they have TBM
  Studio access to the relevant project.
- Periodically audit who has access to sensitive Billing reports, especially those
  containing legal entity, tax, or transfer-pricing information.

Changes to roles or responsibilities should trigger a quick review of Billing access
alongside Costing and other Technology reporting tools.
