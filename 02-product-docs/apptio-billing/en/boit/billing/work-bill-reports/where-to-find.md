# Where to find Billing reports

Billing reports are where most people experience the outcome of the Billing model. This
section focuses on how to find those reports, what the main report groups look like in each
edition, and how they are typically used.

## Where to find Billing reports

How Billing reports appear in the front end depends on the edition in use.

## Billing Essentials

Note: Applies to Billing Essentials only.

For Billing Essentials:

- Reports appear as a **“Billing” report collection** in the same project that runs
  Costing Essentials.
- Users reach them the same way they reach other Costing Essentials reports, using the
  existing front-end entry point.

Within that collection, reports are usually grouped by purpose, for example:

- Overview / invoice style reports.
- Detail and drill-down reports.
- QA or exception reports.
- Journal or export reports.

## Billing Standard

Note: Applies to Billing Standard only.

For Billing Standard:

- Reports are accessed through a **separate Billing endpoint** in addition to Costing
  Standard.
- Inside that endpoint, reports are typically grouped by domain or topic, for example:
  - Overview / dashboard.
  - Services and consumers.
  - Units and rates.
  - Cloud.
  - Applications.
  - Infrastructure (servers, storage, end user devices).
  - Projects.
  - Variance and pricing.

Transfer pricing and journals.
