# Training for Senior Leaders & Stakeholders

This is about decision support, not mechanics.

Topics for a short session:

- Which Billing dashboards and summaries are considered “official.”
- How often they are updated.
- Example questions Billing can answer:
  - Which business units are the largest consumers of a key service.
  - How unit rates have moved over time for strategic services.
  - How cloud or project spend is trending.

Encourage leaders to use Billing as a starting point and direct deeper questions to Admins,
Analysts, or Service Owners.
