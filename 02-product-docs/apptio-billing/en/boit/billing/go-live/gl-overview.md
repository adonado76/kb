# What “go-live” means for Billing

For Billing, go-live usually means all of the following are true for at least one billing
period:

- Data flows into Costing and Billing consistently.
- Models and allocations run successfully in Production.
- Bills and supporting reports match expectations.
- Journal exports (if used) reconcile and can be posted.
- Admins and Analysts can run the monthly process without help from the implementation
  team.
- Stakeholders know where to look and who to ask when something looks off.

You are not “live” just because components are installed. You are live when you can run a
full cycle and defend the numbers.
