# Review service charges

Service Charges reports provide a wealth of information you can use to review and analyze the services consumed by your business unit. To access the Service Charges reports:

1. On the **Application** menu, click **Billing Standard**
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the **Report collection** menu, click **Service
   Charges** .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, select a report in the collection. Report options include:
   - Usage Summary report
   - Invoice report
   - Invoice Overview report
   - Detailed Invoice report
   - Applications report
   - Cloud report
   - Projects report
   - Service Library report

Configure the reports

You can configure many Service Charges reports by using filters and
adding columns. Filters are displayed across the top of the reports in blue boxes. Click the down
arrow to select filter values. Use Shift-Click to select more than one value. Use the**Global Filters** in the upper-right corner to filter an entire report to a specific
business unit.

On the **Detailed Invoice** tab, click the checkboxes to add columns to the
tables.

**Usage Summary report**

The **Usage Summary** report provides a quick overview of charges across all
business units or for a single business unit, and for a specific department. Click items in the
chart for details about a specific service.

Questions answered by this report:

- What are the business unit's charges for the current month?
- How many units of a service did the business unit consume?
- What is the fastest increasing/decreasing services by charges?
- What is the fastest increasing/decreasing services by Units consumed?

**Invoice report**

The Invoice report contains the same information that is included in invoices mailed out to users.

This report has the following tables:

Added and Removed Services

Price Changes

Unit Rate Changes

Consumption Changes

Allocation Changes

Questions answered by this report:

- What are my monthly and quarterly IT charges?
- How do my actual IT charges compare to the planned spend?
- What services have been added or removed in the previous month and what is the impact to my monthly bill?
- What services have had price changes and what is the impact on my monthly bill?
- What services have had changes to the their effective price and what was the impact to my monthly bill?
- What changes in service consumption have occurred and how have they impacted the business unit's bill?
- What allocation changes have occurred that impact the business unit's bill?

**Added and Removed Services**

This table shows the impact of service offerings for charges which either began this month or ended this month.

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Change | Indicates that the service is Added or Removed . |
| Units Added or Removed | Number of units added or removed and their unit price. |
| Impact Add(Remove) | Unit price multiplied by the number of units added or removed in the previous month. |

**Price Changes**

This table shows the impact of changes in the effective price of service offerings with prices defined in the Service Library, calculated with the previous month's units.

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Current Effective Price | Unit price for this month. |
| Previous Effective Price | Unit price for the previous month. |
| Impact Incr(Decr) | Change in unit price from previous to current month, multiplied by the units consumed in the previous month. |

**Unit Rate Changes**

This table shows the impact of changes in the effective price of service offerings with units, calculated with the previous month's units.

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Current Effective Price | Unit price for this month. |
| Previous Effective Price | Unit price for the previous month. |
| Impact Incr(Decr) | Change in unit price from previous to current month, multiplied by the units consumed in the previous month. |

**Consumption Changes**

This table shows the impact of changes in the units of service offerings, calculated with the previous month's effective price.

| Column | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Current | Current consumption of the service in units. |
| Change | Change from the consumption in the previous month, in units. |
| Impact Incr(Decr) | Change in unit price from previous to current month, multiplied by the units consumed in the previous month. |

**Invoice Overview report**

Use the **Invoice Overview** report to review service charges for all
business units and to look at details for each service type.

Questions answered by this report:

- What are the business unit's charges for the current month, quarter, and YTD?
- What are the top five services consumed during the current month?
- What are the variances with my budget/plan?
- How many units of a service are the business units consuming?
- Which services had an increase or decrease in consumption and by how much month-over-month?

**Detailed Invoice report**

Use the **Detailed Invoice** report to answer questions about charges. Filter
the data, add columns, and pivot the data on specific dimensions.

Click **Top Use Cases** to read about how your company typically evaluates

Questions answered by this report:

- What services have been added or removed this month?
- What service prices have changed this month that impact the business unit's bill?
- What service unit rate charges have changed this month that impact the business unit's bill?
- What consumption changes have occurred for services that impact the business unit's bill?
- What allocation changes have occurred that impact the business unit's bill?

**Applications report**

Use the **Applications** report to review service charges by application.

�

Questions answered by this report:

- What SaaS applications are the Business Units charged for?
- What are the current Month, quarter-to-date, and year-to-date charges for SaaS applications?
- Where is the function that the SaaS application supports?
- How many Units of the SaaS application were used?
- What is the purpose of the SaaS application?

**Cloud report**

Use the **Cloud** report to review charges for cloud services.

Questions answered by this report.

- What cloud infrastructure is the business using?
- What are the monthly charges for the infrastructure?
- How many units of each service is the business using?
- What program does the infrastructure support?
- What detailed services is the business unit leveraging?

**Projects report**

Use the **Projects** report to review service charges by project. Filter the
report by project type, project life cycle, business initiative, and spend type.

Questions answered by this report.

- What project charges have been assigned to the business units?
- What are the current month, quarter-to-date, and year-to-date charges?
- What is the assigned business initiative that the project(s) support?
- What program does the project support?
- What is the project for: RTB, GTB or TTB?

**Service Library report**

Use the **Service Library** report to review the per unit charges for each
service. Filter the data and add columns to the table using the controls running across the top of
the report. A checkbox adds a column to the table.
