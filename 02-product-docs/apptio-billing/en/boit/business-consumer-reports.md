# Business consumer reports

Business consumers are interested in:

- High-level summaries of charges.
- Reviewing monthly charges for the services your business unit has used.
- If your charges have changed, determining why they changed.
- Comparing current charges to the amount budgeted for those charges.

**View the monthly invoice**

For a high-level summary of your monthly charges, view your IT bill. To view the bill:

1. On the **Application** menu, click **Billing Standard**
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. In the Business Owner section, click  **Monthly Invoice**  .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click the  **Usage Summary**  report.

Use the top portion of the report to:

1. Review the charges for the month, compare them to last month's charges, and to compare them to
   the budget.
2. Review the charges for the current quarter.
3. Review variance in the budget.

**Determine why charges changed**

If there are significant changes in the charges, use the following sections of the report to
determine why those changes occurred:

- Added and Removed Services
- Price Changes
- Unit Rate Changes
- Consumption Changes
- Allocation Changes

**Review monthly charges for each service**

To review the monthly charges for each service, open the Invoice Overview report. The following
image shows a report for the Finance business unit.

1. On the **Application** menu, click **Billing Standard**
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the  **Report collection**  menu, click  **Service Charges** .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click the  **Invoice Overview**  report.
4. Use the  **Global Filters**  in the upper-right corner to select your
   specific business unit.

![img](../../../../03-media/apptio-billing/resources/images/boit_images/boit_business-consumer-reports_review-charges.jpg)

Use the charts at the top of the report to get a quick summary of the charges for the top five
services and for the main service types.

Use the  **Detail by Service Type**  table to see service category charges
broken down by service name. You can dig further by doing the following.

- Add the Service Offering column by checking the  **Service Offering** 
  option.   
  ![img](../../../../03-media/apptio-billing/resources/images/boit_images/boit_business-consumer-reports_service-offering.jpg)
- Initially, the table is sorted by descending order on the  **Charge** 
  column. You can sort the table by any of the columns by clicking the column header.
- To see a detailed report about a service, click the name of the service in the **Service Name**  column.
- To see charge and unit trends for a service over a 12-month period, click the charge in the
   **Charge**  column.

**Retrieve detailed information on charges**

For detailed information on charges, view the  **Detailed Invoice**  report:

1. On the **Application** menu, click **Billing Standard**
   (see [Billing Standard menu](getting_started/boit-menu.html)).
2. On the  **Report collection**  menu, click  **Service Charges** .
3. On the bar at the top of the page, click the  **Detailed Invoice**  report.
4. Use the controls at the top of the report to add columns, apply filters, and select a time
   period. For further analysis, you can export the table to Excel by right-clicking in the blank area
   below the table and clicking  **Open in Excel**  .
5. See  [Customize Top Use Cases
   content](top-use-cases.html "(Opens in a new tab or window)")  for additional information.
