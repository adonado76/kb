# Configure Billing Standard

To configure Billing Standard, you must have an existing Cost Transparency project. The
configuration tasks are listed below.

- [Open the Cost Transparency project](../cost-transparency/configuration/moduleconfig.html)
- [Install Billing Standard
  components](boit-components.html)
- [Add data to the Service Library table](service-library-table.html)
- [Add business unit data](business-unit-data.html)
- [Add the consumption data for Price x Quantity calculations](add-consumption-data.html "Use consumption data when you want to charge business units based on their consumption of services rather than cost allocations based on factors such as head count. If no additional consumption data is known, use a list of the Service IDs, which may be provided via appending the raw data for the Service Library. Do not append the table for the Service Library itself, however appending the data appended to the Service Library is allowed.")
- [Add services and business unit budget data](services-bu-data.html)
- [Define Cost model allocations](define-cost-model1.html)
- [Define Billable Units model allocations](define-billable-model.html)
- [Define Charge Price x Quantity relationship](define-charge-price.html)
- [Customize the Top Use Cases content](top-use-cases.html)

For browser support information, see [Apptio application browser support](https://community.apptio.com/viewdocument/supported-browsers-screen-resolut "(Opens in a new tab or window)")  .
