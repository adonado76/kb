# Accessing your applications with Apptio Experience (Apex)

Apptio Experience ( Apex) provides a modern, clean interface with
consistent user interface patterns that brings product consistency to the platform and allows users
to access their applications through a unified product navigation.

![apex](../../../03-media/apptio-benchmarking/resources/images/apex_images/apex4.jpg)

Why Apex?

Apex gives users the following advantages:

- brings all products into a consistent user experience
- reinforces the common aspect of the Apptio platform
- offers shared platform capabilities, like comments & collaboration, bookmarks and
  notifications

Apex impacts the navigation and information architecture of products. There
are no functional changes to the capabilities within products.

Apex design and navigation

The Apex design makes it easy for users to navigate their products and
find the data they are looking for.

![apex](../../../03-media/apptio-benchmarking/resources/images/apex_images/apex2.jpg)

- You can navigate Apex easily using the collapsible left panel navigation
  . Open the left navigation panel to navigate across products, then close it to focus on the current
  screen.
- Move quickly between products using the application switcher in the top
  left corner of the screen.
- Launch contextual product help or manage your preferences using the
  toolbar at the top of the screen.
