# Overview of the Benchmarking application

IBM Apptio Benchmarking is a SaaS application that places your actual costs side-by-side with
trusted peer benchmarks. It does not require use of Costing, but is strongly recommended to leverage
the integration features and Benchmark-enabled reports.

At a high level:

- **Primary purpose**  
  Enable apples-to-apples peer comparisons while leveraging the
  TBM taxonomy so you can evaluate cost levels, cost structure, and efficiency.
- **Core scope today** 
  - Infrastructure cost and efficiency metrics at Resource Tower and Sub-Tower levels
  - IT operating expense (OpEx) metrics mapped to Cost Pools and Towers
  - Industry spend and labor metrics at an enterprise level
- **How it works with Costing (if integrated)** 
  - Enabled features in IBM Apptio Benchmarking:
    - Costing provides actuals modeled into Cost Pools, Resource Towers, Resource Sub-Towers, and
      related dimensions
    - Benchmarking aligns that model to external benchmark datasets
    - Reports and dashboards then show your actuals versus peer metrics, with drill-through back to
      Costing for line-item analysis
  - Enabled features in IBM Apptio Costing:
    - A set of benchmark-enabled reports included in Costing that compare your actuals with selected
      benchmark profiles so that you can provide insight into benchmark differences and drill down to
      accounts and transactions.
