# How to use the Troubleshooting & FAQ section

Troubleshooting and FAQ section is for when things look wrong, missing, or confusing, and for
when someone senior hits you with “I don’t buy that number.”

Audience:

- TBMAs / ITFM analysts
- Admins
- Anyone who has to explain or defend Benchmarking output

## How to use the Troubleshooting & FAQ

Use this section when:

- A view is blank or partially blank
- A metric looks wildly off
- Peer groups behave strangely
- Benchmarking doesn’t reflect recent Costing changes
- You need a short, re-usable explanation for stakeholders

Each section gives you:

- Symptom
- Likely causes
- What to check
- A short answer you can adapt for email or slides

If the root cause is configuration, you’ll usually end up back in [Configuration Guide](../home.html#benchmarking__config_guide).

If the root cause is definitions, you’ll end up in [Data & Methodology Reference](../home.html#benchmarking__data-methodology).
