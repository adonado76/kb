# Output

| IT Resource Sub Tower | Definitions | Examples | Unit of Measure (standard) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Central Print | Central print services; often provided to support customer billing or customer documentation support processes. | - Centralized printers (including print to non-paper media) - Document output to fiche/DVD or other permanent storage medium - Print management software - Excludes print to local desktop or departmental printers. | Images (million) |
| Post Processing | Centralized post print processing support (e.g. fold, stuff, apply postage, bundle); often provided to support customer billing or customer documentation support processes. | - Specialized equipment to handle collation, binding, automatic enveloping, print dispatch. - Post processing software | Images (million) |

**Output Unit of Measure**

| IT Resource Sub Tower | Unit of Measure (standard) | Guidelines |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Output | Millions of Images per Month | The output tower includes centralized printing. The unit of measure is the millions of images processed per month and is used to compute the annual cost of output per million images. An image is a side of a page (so a doubled side page counts as 2 images). |
