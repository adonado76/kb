# Industry Sectors for Industry Metrics

This list describes the Industry Sectors supported for the Industry Benchmark Metrics.

**Automotive**

- **Automobiles**
  : Consumer, commercial, manufacturing, sales, service, and parts

**Banking and Finance**

- **Banking**
  : Commercial, Federal Reserve, Central Reserve, national, state, commercial, private, industrial, diversified, trade financing, and mortgage finance
- **Financial services**
  : Fund management, brokerage, capital market, merger and acquisitions, advice, clearinghouses, credit agencies, credit card, check validation, consumer finance, trusts, trust management, escrow, loan, patent, business finance, leasing, credit unions, accounts, audit, payroll, tax, consulting, and trading

**Biotech, Pharma and Medical Equipment**

- **Products**
  : Generic pharma, vaccines, non-prescription, vitamins, supplements, medicinal biotechnology, veterinary, biotechnology, genetics, drug delivery, and life science
- **Services**
  : Labs, manufacturing, and R & D
- **Equipment**
  : Healthcare, dental care, cryogenic, medical, hospital facilities, testing, diagnostic, monitoring, rehabilitation, home healthcare, aid devices, prosthetics, crutches, and veterinary

**Central and Government Banks**

- Central reserve depository, Federal Reserve banks, Foreign Trade and International banks, public-finance institutions, Taxation and Monetary Policy

**Chemicals**

- Products: Industrial organic, industrial inorganic, diversified, fertilizer, agricultural, industrial gases, explosives, dyes, paints, sealants, ink, plastics, films, foam, synthetics, polymers, and silicones

**Commercial Banking**

- Commercial banks

**Construction and Engineering**

- Commercial, contractors, builders, heavy, infrastructure, power, rail, residential, and demolition
- Concrete, glass, masonry, stonework, plastering, roofing, siding, sheet metal, and insulation
- **Construction materials**
  : Brick, tile, glass, concrete, stone, gravel, granite, limestone, marble, sandstone, slate, and clay

**Consumer Products**

- Consumer electronics and household durables
- Security, fire, heat, radar, smoke, and home automation
- Home furnishing, appliances, furniture, décor, accessories, floor, garden, climate control, air conditioners, humidifiers, heaters, and personal appliances
- **Housewares**
  : Ornaments, decorations, cookware, and utensils
- **Leisure equipment**
  : Musical instruments, sporting and recreational, hobbies, toys, games, outdoor, fishing, playground, swimming pool, and photography
- **Apparel and luxury goods**
  : Accessories, clothing, uniforms, luggage, handbags, linens and bedding, travel kits, jewelry, and timepieces
- **Products**
  : Household, tobacco, personal care, office, dental, family, feminine hygiene, food and beverage processing, optical, skin, hair, and first aid
- **Hardware, tools and equipment**
  : Locks, power tools, blades, sinks, baths, plumbing, pipes, nuts, bolts, and washers

**Defense Contractors**

- Organizations financed or whose primary segment is national government/defense agencies in the following industries: Aerospace and defense, Commercial services and supplies, Construction and engineering, Electrical & electronic equipment, Instruments and components, Internet software and services, IT services, logistics, and machinery

**Distribution, Transportation and Logistics**

- **Wholesale distribution**
  : All industries
- **Transportation**
  : Airlines, aircraft, freight and logistics, courier, railroad, charter, postal, sightseeing, rescue, marine, ports and services, docks and piers, cruise, tankers, trucking, limousine, taxi, storage, controllers, highways, and tracks

**Education**

- **Schools**
  : Elementary, secondary, vocational, specialty, and professional
- **Services**
  : Exam preparation, tutoring, online, and childcare

**Global Banking**

- Global banks with an organization revenue of $20B+ and varying combinations of retail banking, wealth/asset management, equities, markets, and business banking

**Government and Non-Profit National, State, Local**

- Government services, governmental organizations, NGO, and non-profit

**Healthcare Payer**

- Healthcare insurance

**Healthcare Provider**

- **Facilities**
  : Hospitals, healthcare, assisted living, nursing home, retirement community, mental care, and animal hospitals
- **Services**
  : Healthcare, dental, nursing, optometry, specialty, home, chiropractic, referral, childbirth, rehabilitation, physical therapy, occupational therapy, medical practice organizations, ambulance, medical laboratory, primary care, practice management, and veterinary

**Higher Education**

- Colleges, universities, and junior colleges

**Hospitality and Travel**

- Hotels, cruise, resorts, travel sites, tours, restaurants, and lodging

**Information Technology**

- Internet software and services, software products, cloud services, and IT outsourcing services

**Insurance**

- **Insurance**
  : Life, property, casualty, homeowner, renter, motor vehicle, accident, disability, fire, marine, liability, commercial, financial, title, surety, health, and medical
- Adjusters, processors, reinsurance, and brokers
- Benefits management

**Insurance Benefits**

- Benefits management

**Investment Banking**

- Asset management and custody banks
- Investment banking and brokerage

**Manufacturing**

- **Transportation, farm, construction, aerospace, defense, machinery equipment**
  : Railroad, recreational vehicles, airplanes, engines, parts, fuel tanks, fuselage, landing, propulsion, farm machinery, heavy trucks, ship, industrial (across all industries), and machinery
- Office furnishings and equipment
- **Electric equipment**
  : Electric components and equipment, battery, lighting, wiring, heavy electric, generators, motors, and fuel cells

**High Tech Manufacturing**

- **Electronic equipment**
  : Computer, network, storage, peripherals, instruments, components, office electronics, all industries, and semiconductors
- **Precision equipment**
  : Satellites, rockets, weapons, and military

**Life Insurance**

- Life Insurance

**Media**

- Broadcast media production for radio, TV, cable, satellite, internet, print, and entertainment production
- Writing, editing, and translation services
- Movie, video, and music production and distribution
- **Entertainment**
  : Casinos, lotteries, amusement parks, recreation, sports, golf, skiing, museum, and gardens

**Metals and Natural Resources**

- **Ores and metals**
  : Copper, lead, aluminum, nickel, radium, tin, titanium, uranium, zinc, coal, gold, silver, steel, iron, and diversified
- Lumber, logging, paper, wood, mills, furnaces, and products
- Agricultural and animal services, and mining

**Non-Profit**

- Tax exempt and or charitable organizations with primary activities in the following sectors: Business associations, Civic and social associations, Commercial nonphysical research, Commercial physical research, Educational, religious, and charitable trusts, Employment agencies, Health and allied services, Individual and family services, Labor organizations, Membership organizations, Noncommercial research organizations, Political organizations, Professional organizations, Public relations services, Refuse & water systems, Religious organizations, Services, Social services

**Oil and gas**

- Drilling, rig, field, exploration, production, refining, extraction, manufacturing, production, marketing, products, storage, transportation, and pipelines

**Professional Services**

- **Consumer**
  : Residential, sanitary, storage, retail, building, and security
- **Commercial**
  : Advertising, building, design, public relations, marketing, management services, real estate, and human resources
- **R&D**
  : IT consulting, research, maintenance, management consulting, and planning
- **Organizations**
  : Membership organizations, social, labor, environmental, conservation, wildlife, religious, voluntary, and political (does not include non-profit organizations)

**Property and Casualty Insurance**

- Property and Casualty Insurance

**Real Estate**

- REITs, Real estate development, real estate services, real estate operating companies

**Retail**

- **Stores**
  : Brick and mortar, internet, catalog, food and drug, supermarket, auto rental, services, markets, and personal care

**Retail and Consumer Credit**

- Consumer finance, regional banks, thrifts and mortgage finance

**State, Local and Education Government Agencies**

- State/Province and local level government agencies

**Telecommunications**

- **Equipment**
  : Communications, towers, telephone and telecommunications, ISDN, PBX, teleconferencing, voicemails, and wireless
- **Services**
  : Carriers, broadband, ATM, DSL, ISDN, fiber optics, VPN, digital, conferencing, satellite, mobile data, long distance, and local

**US Federal Government**

US national/federal level government agencies

**Utilities**

- **Power generation**
  : Fossil fuels, nuclear, hydro electric, solar, and wind
- **Transmission**
  : Electric and gas
- **Utilities and distribution**
  : Electric, gas, water, and waste
