# The Customer Value of Apptio Benchmarking

With Benchmarking you can:

**Establish and Maintain Trust in IT Actual Costs**

By leveraging best practices using the Apptio TBM Unified Model (ATUM), your organization’s IT
costs are assigned and allocated to industry approved standard definitions. You slice your GL into
Cost Pools and view their costs in terms of IT Resource Towers. Benchmarking ’s peer benchmark
metrics for these TBM Taxonomy Cost Pools and IT Resource Towers provide a standard way to evaluate
your costs. You can delve into cost drivers down to individual line items to get a clear
understanding of their metrics. Specifically you can:

- Articulate the value of IT and justify the cost of IT delivery
- Improve dialog and alignment with business users based on standardized actuals and peer benchmarks

**Identify Areas of Optimization**

Evaluate your organization’s actuals in comparison with the peer benchmark. This enables you to rationalize costs and determine areas of optimization.

**Set Informed Fiscal Targets**

After identifying your focus areas, set metric-specific targets and track progress over time.

**Get up-to-date Peer Comparisons On Demand**

Benchmarking builds on the “liveness” of your organization’s actuals in Cost Transparency.
Once your actuals are on-boarded, incremental updates are automatic. On-boarding actuals in Cost
Transparency is a one-time implementation cost. Additionally, Apptio refreshes the benchmark peer
data on an annual basis by the end of the 2nd quarter of the calendar year. You can access the peer
comparison with current actuals and peer benchmark metrics whenever you wish.
