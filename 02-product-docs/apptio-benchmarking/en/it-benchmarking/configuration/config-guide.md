# Benchmarking Configuration Guide

Configuring Benchmarking is a two step process, illustrated in the following video series.

**Step 1: Interactive Benchmarking Configuration**

Input your actuals into Interactive Benchmarking:

- Automatically, by pulling in your actuals from your Cost Transparency (CT) project:

  [Video: Automatically fetch actuals from Cost Transparency (8 min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/about-fetching-actuals-from-cost-tr "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- Or manually:

  [Video: Manually input or adjust actuals (5 min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/video-configure-interactive-benchm "(Opens in a new tab or window)")  This video
  also explains how you can override actual values pulled in from your CT project.

If your CT project is in non-US currency (as of April 2017, EUR or GBP only), the following videos provide guidance on conversion within Interactive Benchmarking:

- [Video: Use with non-US currencies (Intro)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/video-configure-interactive-benchm-1 "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- [Video: Use with non-US currencies (Details)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/configure-interactive-benchmarking "(Opens in a new tab or window)")

**Step 2: Cost Transparency Benchmarking Configuration**

Download the selected benchmarks for Industry, IT OpEx, Infrastructure metrics from Interactive Benchmarking as a CSV.

Upload the Benchmark .CSV file into Cost Transparency:

- [Video: Upload and append the benchmark data into Cost Transparency (3
  min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/configure-cost-transparency-benchma-1 "(Opens in a new tab or window)")

Map your Cost Pools to Benchmarks in Cost Transparency:

- [Video: Map cost pools (2 min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/configure-cost-transparency-benchma-2 "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- [Map the cost pools](map-cost-pools.html)

Map your Towers, Sub-Towers & Units of Measure to Benchmarks in Cost Transparency:

- [Video: Map towers (1 min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/configure-cost-transparency-benchma "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- [Map the towers](map-towers.html)
- [Configure Cost Transparency Benchmarking: Map Sub-Towers (2 min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/configure-cost-transparency-benchma-4 "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- [Map the sub-towers](map-sub-towers.html)
- [Video: Configure Cost Transparency Benchmarking: Map Units of Measure (2
  min.)](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/video-configure-cost-transparency "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
- [Map the units of measure](map-uom.html)
- [Choose the benchmarks displayed on reports](report-benchmarks.html)
