# Architecture and data flow

At a high level, Interactive Benchmarking combines four ingredients:

1. **External benchmark datasets** 
   - Industry IT economics.
   - IT OpEx distributions.
   - Infrastructure unit cost and efficiency.
2. **Your organizational profile** 
   - Revenue, headcount, industry, region, size band.
3. **Your IT org’s cost data** 
   - Annual IT cost by Cost Pool, Resource Tower, and Sub-tower.
4. **Your IT org’s volume and capacity data** 
   - Servers, TB of storage, network ports or devices, FTEs, and similar
