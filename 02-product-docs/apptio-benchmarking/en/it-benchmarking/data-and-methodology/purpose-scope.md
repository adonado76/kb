# ***

Data and Methodology section is for the “how exactly is that calculated” crowd.

Audience

- TBMAs and ITFM analysts who must defend the numbers
- Finance and audit folks who do not take charts on faith
- Execs who ask “what is in this metric, precisely” instead of nodding politely

## Purpose and scope

The section explains:

- Where benchmark data comes from
- How it is normalized to TBM
- How peer groups and distributions are built
- How key metric families are calculated
- What the main limitations and caveats are

It does not cover how to fix wiring in the product. For configuration, see [Configuration Guide](../home.html#benchmarking__config_guide). For “something looks
wrong” triage, see [Troubleshooting &
FAQ](../home.html#benchmarking__faq_tr).
