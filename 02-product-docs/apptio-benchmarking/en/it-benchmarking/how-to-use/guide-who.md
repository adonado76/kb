# Who this guide is for

## Primary Audience

These people configure, run, and explain Benchmarking:

- TBM Analysts (TBMAs)
- IT Finance / ITFM Analysts (ITFMAs)
- Admins

## Secondary Audience

These people typically consume reports and make decisions:

- CIO, CTO, CFO, VP Finance, VP Infrastructure
- Product, Service, and Resource Tower Owners
- Other senior stakeholders who log in to read Benchmarking outputs
