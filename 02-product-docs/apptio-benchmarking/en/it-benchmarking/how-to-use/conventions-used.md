# Conventions used in this guide

To keep things consistent:

- **Costing** means the IBM Apptio product formerly known as Cost Transparency.
- **IBM Apptio Benchmarking**and **“Interactive Benchmarking”** mean the overall
  benchmarking product.
- **Benchmark-enabled Costing reports”**means reports that include benchmark values alongside
  your actuals in the IBM Apptio Costing product.
- **TBM** means the Technology Business Management discipline and standard taxonomy for
  classifying Technology spend.
- **ATUM** means Apptio’s implementation of the TBM taxonomy and model. When we talk about cost
  structures, Cost Pools, Resource Towers, and Resource Sub-Towers, references to “TBM” and “ATUM” are
  used interchangeably to mean the standardized TBM model that underpins Costing and
  Benchmarking.
