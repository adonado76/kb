# D&amp;A in Industry benchmarks

**Q. Do I include IT spending outside the control of IT?**

Your organization may have spending on IT outside the control of the main IT department. This might include spending allocated to Business Process Outsourcing, operational technology, or "Shadow IT".

In your benchmark, do not include any spending which is controlled by other areas of your organization.

**Q. Which cost pools should I exclude from the infrastructure benchmarking?**

Do not include any of the following cost pool expenses:

- Facilities and Power
- Telecom
- Internal Services
- Other

**Q. Where do I include expenses belonging to the Telecom cost pool?**

Include any expenses relating to the Telecom cost pool in
Network – Transport
.

**Q. Are there any infrastructure benchmarking metrics for the
Facilities and power
,
Internal services
or
Other
cost pools?**

No.

**Q. How are public cloud costs accounted for in OpEx and infrastructure benchmarking metrics?**

In OpEx benchmarking, public cloud costs are included in the Outside services cost pool.

In Industry benchmarking, public cloud costs are included in
IT spend
.

**Q. Do I include contractors?**

Yes.

**Q. Where do I allocate business data or information subscriptions?**

Allocate these to the
Other
cost pool.

**Q. What can I do if I would like to look at different KPIs for a specific area, for example, to divide by devices rather than users?**

All available KPIs are surfaced in the product.
