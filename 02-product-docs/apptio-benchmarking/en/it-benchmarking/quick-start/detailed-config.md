# When to move on to detailed configuration

You are ready to go deeper once you can:

- Open Interactive Benchmarking reliably.
- See at least one Industry and one IT OpEx view with sensible values.
- Explain the peer group and time period you are using.
- Defend, at a basic level, what is included in the metrics you show.

At that point:

- Use the [Configuration Guide](../home.html#benchmarking__config_guide) section
  to update/refine:
  1. TBM/ATUM version selection.
  2. Infrastructure cost and volume alignment.
  3. Peer group selections.
- Use the [Using Benchmarking
  Reports](../home.html#benchmarking__benchmark_reports) section to prepare better stories and embed benchmark output into regular reporting
  and Resource Tower discussions.

Do not over-engineer this stage. Get to a usable baseline, then iterate with better mapping and
governance rather than trying to get everything perfect on day one.
