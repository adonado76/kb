# Accessing Benchmarking

To open IBM Apptio Benchmarking, follow one of the two options listed below:

1. Sign into IBM Apptio Benchmarking directly by navigating to this web address: [https://itbmx.apptio.com](https://itbmx.apptio.com "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
2. Access IBM Apptio Benchmarking via Access Administration by:
   1. Navigating to Access Administration in your web browser [https://frontdoor.apptio.com](https://frontdoor.apptio.com "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
   2. Click on the **Interactive Benchmarking** button

      ![Accessing Benchmarking](../../resources/images/it%20benchmarking_images/accessing-benchmarking.png)

After opening, you can see

- A landing page with entry points such as **Interactive Benchmarking**.
- Tiles to benchmark-enabled **Costing** reports, if configured.
- A path to **Settings** or configuration for Benchmarking, visible to users with admin
  rights.

If you do not see the Interactive Benchmarking button or experience any difficulties accessing
the app

- Confirm with your IBM Apptio contact the product is provisioned to you.
- Confirm your user role includes access to Interactive Benchmarking. At a minimum, the role you
  are assigned in Frontdoor should include the *ViewInteractiveBenchmarksAndCostData* to access
  Interactive Benchmarking. To complete all setup actions, the role should include the
  *ViewInteractiveBenchmarksAndCostData*, *ConfigureInteractiveBenchmarking*, and
  *RetrieveInteractiveBenchmarkingCostData* permissions.
- Confirm you are in the correct environment (for main).
