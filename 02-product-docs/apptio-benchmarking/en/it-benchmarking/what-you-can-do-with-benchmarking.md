# What you can do with Benchmarking

IBM Apptio Benchmarking provides self-service peer comparisons of your technology spend over
time. It turns your IT costs into context: where you are ahead of peers, where you lag, and where to
focus improvement.

- **Validate spending levels**  
  Compare your actuals to self-selected benchmarks to
  see where you sit relative to peers at Cost Pool, Resource Tower, and Sub-Tower levels.
- **Track progress**
    
  Set targets on key metrics and monitor movement over time instead of treating
  benchmarking as a one-time event.
- **Identify improvement areas**
    
  Use credible benchmark data as an objective reference point to prioritize optimization,
  sourcing strategy changes, and investment shifts.
- **Defend and justify budgets**
    
  Support budget asks or protection of run-the-business spend with external context, not
  just internal history.
- **Inform resource and portfolio planning**  
  Use benchmarked distributions of spend
  to guide how much you invest in specific Resource Towers, Services, or Products.

For TBMAs and ITFMAs with modeled IT costs, Benchmarking is both a quality check on the cost
model and a signal generator for where to investigate further.

For executives, it is a way to understand whether the organization is spending in line with peers
and operating efficiently.
