# ***

Operations and Governance section is about how to run IBM Apptio Benchmarking as part of your TBM
/ ITFM program, not how to click buttons.  
Audience:

- TBM / ITFM leaders
- TBMAs and admins who keep the system alive
- Sponsors, tower owners, and Finance who rely on Benchmarking in recurring governance

## Why Benchmarking needs governance

If you do not govern Benchmarking, it devolves into:

- Random screenshots thrown into decks
- Different teams using different peer groups and years
- “We’re above median” arguments that never go anywhere

With even light governance, it becomes:

- A standard input to planning and steering
- A repeatable way to set and defend targets
- A shared lens for tower performance and optimization

You need three things:

1. Clear **ownership**
2. Simple **cadence**
3. Basic **rules of use and change**
