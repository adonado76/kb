# Export a Report

To extract raw data from a Report, users can Export the data to a CSV file by navigating to the
"Export" option from the dot menu:

![](../../../../03-media/cloudability-premium/images/export-menu.png)

Clicking the "Export" menu item will initiate the Report generation. It may take several minutes
to prepare the Report for download, depending on the size of the data set being exported. The
following modal will be displayed on the bottom right side of the page to confirm that the Report is
being generated:

![](../../../../03-media/cloudability-premium/images/export-modal.png)

Reports that are being generated can be found in the menu on the top of the page, next to the
View selector:

![](../../../../03-media/cloudability-premium/images/generate-report.png)
