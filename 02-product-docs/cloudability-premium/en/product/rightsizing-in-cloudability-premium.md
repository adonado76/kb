# Rightsizing in Cloudability Premium

## Public Cloud Optimization

As businesses grow, the complexity of applications and the cloud infrastructure requires constant
vigilance from your teams to meet evolving demands. This requires continuously determining the exact
compute, storage, database configurations and application configurations to meet the application
demand and at the lowest cost. Most often than not, it is impossible for humans, no matter how
skilled they are, to proactively monitor and optimize the infrastructure manually and this further
complicates as your cloud footprint grows.

**Cloudability Usage Optimization**

IBM Cloudability’s rightsizing feature provides a powerful, intuitive interface for drilling into
scale up, scale down and terminate recommendations, enabling you to find, implement and track the
success of your optimization opportunities. Cloudability presents two sets of optimization
recommendations:

- Basic Rightsizing: a set of rightsizing recommendations with cost savings balancing risks based
  on your rightsizing preferences you have configured [Rightsizing](get-recommendations-for-scaling-your-cloud-resources-with-rightsizing.html).
- Advanced Rightsizing: a set of optimization recommendations powered by Turbonomic focused on
  workload performance assurance at the lowest possible cost [Advanced Rightsizing](advanced-rightsizing-powered-by-turbonomic.html).

## Rightsizing FAQ

Can I opt to view only basic or advanced optimization recommendations?

Your Cloudability admin has an option to show or hide Cloudability generated rightsizing
recommendations shown in the Basic tab. Turbonomic powered cost and performance optimization
recommendations will always be seen in the Advanced tab
