# Pie charts

Applies to: TBM Studio 12.0 and later

Report TBM Studio offers the following types of pie charts.

## Donut Half

![](../../resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/studio/stu622.png)

Pie charts only work with positive numbers. If there are negative numbers, a message stating
there is inappropriate data is displayed in place of the pie chart.

A pie chart will always show slices representing the total (100%) of the value being charted. For
example, assume you are creating a pie chart that shows the top five data centers based on total
cost. They represent 70% of the total cost. An additional slice called Other is added to the pie
chart to represent the other 30%. You cannot hide the Other slice.

## Pie chart properties

The pie chart properties are available on the Pie tab. Experiment with the options to see
what they do.

![](../../resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/studio/stu623.png)
