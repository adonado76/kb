# Introduction to TBM Studio

TBM Studio is a suite of tools that helps you manage your
technology business spend. It includes three major components:

- Data Studio
- Model Studio
- Report Studio

- **Data Studio**: Lets you [gather the right data, intelligently transform](../data_studio/data-transform-chapter-title-page.html "Applies to: TBM Studio 12.0 and later") it, and enhance it over time.
- **Model Studio**: Lets you can [visually customize allocations and configure a flexible cost model](../model_studio/models-chapter-title-page.html "Applies to: TBM Studio 12.0 and later") to
  accurately reflect IT costs and services.
- **Report Studio**: Lets you can [customize existing reports or create new ones](../reports/intro_report_studio.html "Applies to: TBM Studio 12.0 and later") that include tables, charts as
  well as components for filtering the reports, entering data, and navigating between reports.

Instances, domains, and projects organize your work:")
