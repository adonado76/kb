# Dynamic sizing and positioning

**Applies to**: TBM Studio 12.0 and later

Dynamic layout options control the size and position of report components. Size options adjust
the size of components and container of the content. Position options control the placement of
components in their container. The container can be an entire report, or a group box or a tabbed
component. To apply these options, use the **Dynamic** menu on the **Format** tab.

The layout options can be applied to:

- Tables
- Charts
- Group Boxes
- Tabbed Components

## Apply the options

To apply the options:

1. Select the component.
2. Open the **Dynamic** menu on the **Format** tab.
3. Select one of the options.

Only one option can be applied to a component at any given time.

## Option descriptions

**Dynamic Off** - Turns off dynamic sizing and positioning.

- **Center** - Positions the component in the center of the report, group box, or tabbed
  component. The size of the component is not changed.

  ![Positions the component](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep232.png)
- **Center and Grow** - Positions the center of the component in the center of the report,
  group box, or tabbed component. As you move the left edge of the component, it adjusts the right
  edge to keep the box centered.

  ![Center and Grow](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep233.png)
- **Fill Right** - Extends the component to the right border of the report, group box, or
  tabbed component.

  ![Fill Right](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep234.png)
- **Fill Right and Bottom** - Extends the component to the right and bottom borders of the
  report, group box, or tabbed component.

  ![Fill Right and Bottom](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep235.png)
- **Dock Right** - Moves the component so the right edge is touching the right border of the
  report, group box, or tabbed component. The size of the component is not changed.

  ![Dock Right](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep236.png)
- **Dock Bottom** - Moves the component so the bottom edge is touching the bottom of the
  report, group box, or tabbed component. The size of the component is not changed.

  ![Dock Bottom](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studioimages/reports/rep237.png)
