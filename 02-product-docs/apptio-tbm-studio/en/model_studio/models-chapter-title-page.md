# About Model Studio

Applies to: TBM Studio 12.0 and later

A data model is a logical and graphical representation of the flow of data relevant to the cost,
budget, utilization, service levels, consumption, or other metrics of your IT services. In Studio, a
model is considered a metric, where a metric is a value. A data model shows how a wide range of
financial or operational data are related to the cost and performance of the products and services
that IT provides to the business.
