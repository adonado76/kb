# How to access Cloudability

## Overview

You can access this product through Apex.

Apptio Experience ( Apex ) provides a modern, clean interface with consistent user interface patterns that brings product consistency to the platform and allows users to access their applications through a unified product navigation.

## Why Apex?

Apex gives users the following advantages:

- brings all products into a consistent user experience
- reinforces the common aspect of the Apptio/IBM platform
- offers shared platform capabilities, like comments & collaboration, bookmarks and notifications

Apex impacts the navigation and information architecture of products. There are no functional changes to the capabilities within products.

## Apex design and navigation

The Apex design makes it easy for users to navigate their products and find the data they are looking for.

- You can navigate Apex easily using the collapsible left panel navigation . Open the left navigation panel to navigate across products, then close it to focus on the current screen.
- Move quickly between products using the application switcher in the top right corner of the screen.
- Launch contextual product help or manage your preferences using the toolbar at the top of the screen.
