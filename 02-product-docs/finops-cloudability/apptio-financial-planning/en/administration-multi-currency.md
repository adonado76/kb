# Multi-currency

*Cloud Financial Planning supports planning in local currency, where local currency is the base currency as defined by the multi-currency configuration of Cloudability .*

Cloud Financial Planning will convert billed currencies from your CSP into your configured base currency when generating forecasts and comparing actuals performance against plan. All plan (forecast and budget) amounts are in the base currency.
