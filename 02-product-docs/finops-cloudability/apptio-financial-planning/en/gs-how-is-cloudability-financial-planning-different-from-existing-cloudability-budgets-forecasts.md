# How is Cloudability Financial Planning different from existing Cloudability Budgets and Forecasts

*Cloudability Financial Planning introduces a new entity called "Plans." These plans incorporate both forecasts and budgets, providing a convenient way to manage distinct yet interrelated financial data: what you plan to spend and what you are approved to spend, all within a single document.*

When you create a plan, a forecast is automatically generated for you based upon your prior spending patterns. You have complete control over the level of granularity and detail, allowing you to select the dimensions and time periods that suit your planning needs. Notably, unlike the existing Cloudability forecasts, the forecast within a plan can be edited. Plans can also be segmented by cost ownership and shared with other users to facilitate collaborative planning and consolidation of inputs.

Cloudability Financial Planning is designed to streamline essential financial planning processes, such as annual budgeting, monthly variance analysis and forecast updates. To facilitate review and approval processes, you can assign different users for setting budget amounts and those responsible for providing forecast updates. Additionally, it comes with interactive analytics to monitor spending performance and analyze budget and forecast variances, automating the tasks necessary to support the monthly forecast review and update process.
