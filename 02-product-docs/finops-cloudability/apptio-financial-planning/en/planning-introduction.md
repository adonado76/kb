# Introduction

*Cloudability Financial Planning integrates seamlessly with your existing Cloudability setup. To get started with Cloudability Financial Planning, ensure that Cloudability is configured to connect to your cloud service providers and that you have the necessary dimensions and views setup to support your reporting needs.*

If you are able to report on actual spending, then you can easily create plans using the same dimensions in your reports. Plans allow you to edit forecast amounts and regularly compare actual spending to planned spending without any additional effort. However, the dimensions and views set up to support your cloud spend reporting may not be sufficient for your budgeting and forecasting needs. To enhance support for a consolidated budgeting and regular forecast update process, you may need to create additional views and dimensions.
