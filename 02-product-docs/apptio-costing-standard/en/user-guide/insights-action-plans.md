# Insights and Action Plans

Insights and Action Plans are designed to automate the identification and delivery of
opportunities to save money, reduce risk, and improve data.

Insights automatically analyzes your data in Costing Standard and uses an Insight Library to
identify areas for cost savings, data improvement, and risk reduction. You can use these insights
into your data to ensure you are avoiding cost issues and to optimize your spend and resource
use.

Action Plans provide a data driven way to set cost savings targets and automatically track the
progress to achieving those targets.
