# IBM Apptio Product TCO Solution

Organizations have started using product-based approach and are thinking how they plan, fund, and
measure the value of technology investments. This change represents a new delivery model which requires

- Clear visibility on product outcome.
- New financial governance practices.
- Shared understanding of how products grow and improve over time.

## Overview

IBM Apptio Product TCO is a purpose-built solution designed to provide a clear and defensible
view of product costs and composition. This solution delivers end-to-end visibility across the
entire product lifecycle. By integrating financial and operational data, it provides a single,
product-centric view, connecting OpEx, CapEx, and cost drivers across consumers, applications, and
projects.

Using this solution you can:

- Continuously track Product costs to eliminate waste and improve budgeting.
- Manage resourcing at every lifecycle stage to shift spend from maintenance to growth.
- Create portfolio views for every stakeholder to get the insights to make decision faster.

![Product TCO](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/product-tco-overview.png)

## Solution

Product TCO supports both product-based and hybrid delivery models, providing the product
transparency and control needed to manage organizational transitions effectively. By directly
linking financials to end products, leaders can optimize product investments, manage product
lifecycles with data-driven insights, and make informed roadmap decisions. As a result,
organizations gain measurable improvements in product cost efficiency, building stronger alignment
between product investments and strategic outcomes.

- **Product Cost & Driver Visibility**  
  By automating the allocation of OpEx and
  CapEx to products, organizations can gain a clear understanding of how costs across various
  dimensions, including consumers, applications, projects, and other areas, directly impact the
  product level. This enables the continuous tracking of product costs, allowing organizations to
  eliminate waste and improve budgeting.  
  The report answer critical questions such as:
  - What is the fully loaded cost for Product X this period?
  - Where do we have budget variance?
  - What projects directly support which products?
  - What is the composition of my labor resources?
  - Which vendors impact my product with the highest costs?

![Product Cost](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/product-cost.png)

- **Product Lifecycle & Investment Management**

  To optimize product performance and
  investment, it is essential to manage resources and investments across every stage of the product
  lifecycle, from launch to retirement. Get a comprehensive snapshot of your product portfolio to
  identify products that are new, aging, or nearing retirement. Align OpEx and CapEx with each
  lifecycle stage to avoid overinvesting in aging products and underinvesting in products driving
  business growth.

  With these insights, organizations can make informed decisions to optimize
  their product portfolios, ensuring they remain relevant and strategically aligned with their
  business priorities.

    
  The report answer critical questions such as:
  - What products are approaching the target retire date?
  - Do the product OpEx / CapEx costs align with lifecycle stage?
  - How can we redirect our spend to minimize product maintenance costs?
  - Do I have any remaining costs or components associated with my retiring product?
  - Are costs assigned to any retired products and what are their sources?

![Product Life Cycle](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/product-life-cycle.png)

- **Customizable Portfolio Hierarchies**

  Organizations create portfolio views for every
  stakeholder to provide insights that enable to make decision faster. They roll up products into a
  customizable four-level product hierarchy and the TBM's standardized Solution taxonomy, allowing for
  easy comparison and analysis across different portfolio views and delivery models. With these insights,
  - Leaders can pinpoint which product families dominate spend
  - They can decide where to allocate funding.
  - They can analyze how portfolios align with organizational strategy.

    
  The report answer critical questions such as:
  - How does Product X roll up to our organization's hierarchy vs the TBM taxonomy?
  - Where do we have duplicative products?
  - Which products in the portfolio deserve more funding?

![Portfolios](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/portfolios.png)

## Getting Started

1. Go to the Components icon under **Project** tab and install **Product TCO** and **Product
   TCO reporting**.

   ![Install Product TCO](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/product-tco-install.png)
2. To classify offering records as products, choose **All Business Services** option from the
   **Select Destination** list and Select **Type** as **Product** under the **Service
   Offering** column.

   ![Service Offering](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/service-offerings-product-tco.png)
3. To review reports, expand the **Report Collections** menu and select **Product** .

   ![Report Collection](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/report-collection-product-tco.png)
