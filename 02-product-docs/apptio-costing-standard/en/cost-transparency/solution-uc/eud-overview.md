# End User Devices Overview

## Overview

End User Devices use cases focus on understanding the total cost of ownership of workforce
devices such as PCs, laptops, smartphones, tablets, and other client compute equipment.
These use cases help organizations gain visibility into device-related spend, allocate costs
to consuming services, and improve cost accountability across the workforce.

## Personas

- **IT Finance** - Reviews end user device spend, monitors budget performance, and
  analyzes cost drivers across hardware, software, and support costs.
- **Service Owner** - Evaluates device costs associated with end user services and
  workforce enablement to support service pricing and optimization decisions.

## Use Cases

**Cost Allocation to Services**

Allocate end user device costs to end user services and specific business services to
improve cost transparency and accountability.

## Outcomes

- Accurate allocation of device costs to end user and business services.
- Better budgeting and forecasting for device refresh and workforce growth.
