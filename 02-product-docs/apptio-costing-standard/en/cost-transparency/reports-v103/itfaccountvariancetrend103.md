# IT Finance - Account Variance - Trend report (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

Use this report to view the spend and budget by month for the current fiscal year.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itfaccountvariancetrend103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Finance > Account Variance > Variance Trend View

## Roles

This report is designed for IT Finance personnel.

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- View the spend and budget by month for the current fiscal year.
- Review the supporting data to see the budget variance and the percent variance by month.

## Questions answered

You can use the information presented on this report to answer the following questions:

- How is my budget variance for this cost center fluctuating over time?
- Is the spend trending up, down, or remaining constant?
- Is my spend tracking to the planned budget or am I seeing spikes in over/under budget
  variance?
- Is action required to mitigate budget risk?

## Next actions

View the account transactions for the current or previous months by clicking an account on the IT
Finance - Account Variance report.
