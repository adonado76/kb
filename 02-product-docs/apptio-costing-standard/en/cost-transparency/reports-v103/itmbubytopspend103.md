# IT Management - Business Units - By Top Spend report (v103)

Use this report to see the top spend by business unit.

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

![image](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmbubytopspend103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Business Units > By Top Spend

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- Business unit owners
- CIOs
- CFOs

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- See the top spend by Business Unit.
- For each unit see its owner, business relationship manager, and other data.

## Questions answered

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:

- Which business units consume the most IT costs?
- Is action required to mitigate risk?

## Next actions

- Click the name of a business unit to access a Business Units – Individual Consumption Details
  report for that business unit.
- Contact the assigned Business Relationship Manager if follow-up is required.
