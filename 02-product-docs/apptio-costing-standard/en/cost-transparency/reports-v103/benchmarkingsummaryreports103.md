# Benchmarking Summary reports

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

Benchmarking automates the ongoing comparison of costs against a selection of peer organizations.
IT costs are calculated the same way for each organization according to ATUM® — Apptio's industry standard IT cost model
— and are based on source financial systems including the General Ledger (GL). Costs are calculated
at the IT tower and subtower level and presented across a standard set of cost pools (e.g.:
hardware, software, internal labor, external labor, etc.).

By comparing your IT organization's calculated IT spend to a set of peer organizations, you can
gain insights required to drive ongoing IT improvement:

Identify cost optimization targets
:   Use unit cost metrics for IT support and infrastructure, and make confident peer comparisons.

Drive performance improvements
:   Support governance activities, stimulate continuous improvement, and create incentives based on credible targets.

Justify IT spend
:   Plan with relevant facts and metrics, and justify current and future IT spend, and staffing costs.

Enable business centered conversations
:   Demonstrate the value of IT services, and improve dialogue and alignment with business users based on data.

## Benchmarking application

The Benchmarking Summary reports are part of the Costing Standard Foundation application. The
Benchmarking application offers a more detailed and extensive set of benchmarking reports.

## KPIs highlight key benchmarks

The KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) running across the top of the reports focus on:

- IT spend as a percent of the total company revenue
- IT operating expense as a percent of the total company operating expense
- IT operating expense as a percent of the total IT spend (OpEx plus CapEx)
- The number of full time employees in IT as a percent of the total employees in the company
- The amount of IT spend per employee in the company

The benchmark KPI figures are based on the cost source amounts for your organization compared to
the peer benchmark figures.

## IT Spend tab

The **IT Spend** tab displays benchmarking data for the towers and cost pools defined in ATUM. The report is part of the Costing Standard Foundation application and will be available if your organization has agreed to share its
cost information with Apptio so it
can become part of the benchmarking data.

![IT spend tab](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/benchmarkingsummary103_itspend1.png)

## Application Distribution tab

The **Application Distribution** report displays benchmarking data for the applications used
in your organization. The benchmarks are based on industry, geography, and size characteristics for
your organization. This report is part of the Benchmarking application and will be available if your
organization has purchased the application.

![application distribution tab](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/benchmarkingsummary103_applicationdistribution2.png)

## Infra(structure) Unit Costs tab

The **Infra(structure) Unit Costs** tab displays benchmarking data for the towers defined by
the Apptio TBM Unified Model (ATUM). The benchmarks are based on
industry, geography, and size characteristics for your organization. The report is part of the
standalone ApptioBenchmarking application and will be available if your organization has purchased
the application.

![Infra costs tab](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/benchmarkingsummary103_infraunitcosts3.png)
