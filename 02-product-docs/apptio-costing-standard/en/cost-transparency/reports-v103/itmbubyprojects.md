# IT Management - Business Units - By Projects Report (v103)

Use this report to review the spend by project.

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmbubyprojects103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Business Units > By Projects

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- Business unit owners
- CIOs
- CFOs

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- Track project costs over the past year.
- Compare project costs across business units.
- Compare project costs for RTB, GTB, and TTB.

## Questions answered

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:

- Do we have the right mix of spend across RTB, GTB, and TTB projects?
- Are we allocating spend to the highest priority projects?
- Is action required to change spend?
