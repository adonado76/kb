# IT Management - Financials - IT Owner Details (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

The IT Management - Financials - IT Owner Details reports shows the budget and variance by cost
center.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmfinancialsitowner_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Financials > Owner

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- CIO
- IT Management

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- Quickly identify which Cost Centers are over or under budget.
- Review the Cost Centers that roll up into the CIO-1 "Owner" level to see how each is performing
  against budget.
- Search/slice by Owner or specific Account Group to find and view their spend.

## Questions answered

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:

- Is this Cost Center Owner on track to hit their budget?
- Is the budget variance trending up, down or remaining constant?
- If they are over or under budget, does the variance warrant further investigation and
  explanation?

## Next actions

- View the 13-month budget variance trend by clicking View in the Trend column.
- View the cost center's transactions by clicking View in the Tx column.
