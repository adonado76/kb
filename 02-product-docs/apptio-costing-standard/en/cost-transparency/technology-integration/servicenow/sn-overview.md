# ServiceNow Overview

## Overview

The integration between ServiceNow and IBM Apptio Costing provides a prescriptive,
scalable, and repeatable way to bring high-quality infrastructure, application, service, and
relationship data from ServiceNow into IBM Apptio’s financial models. By leveraging
ServiceNow’s CMDB and CSDM frameworks, customers gain a reliable, continuously updated
source of truth for configuration items, application portfolios, service relationships, and
operational metadata.

This integration significantly accelerates time-to-value for IBM Apptio implementations by
automating data ingestion, improving the validity of consumptive allocations, and enabling
more trusted and defensible TCO insights for Applications, Services, Business Units, and
other TBM layers.

Through standard Datalink ingress connectors, blueprint mapping, and additional egress
capabilities to drive Apptio TCO insights back into ServiceNow dashboards, the integration
creates a unified and bidirectional operational-and-financial view of the technology
enterprise.

## Target Audience

This integration is designed for organizations that rely on ServiceNow as the system of
record for operational and configuration data and want to strengthen their financial
transparency and TCO modeling in IBM Apptio and democratize this financial intelligence to
key ServiceNow and IBM Apptio users. Typical personas include:

**IT Finance & TBM Teams**

Require accurate, consumptive, and defensible cost allocations using real-time CMDB and
operational data.

**Application, Service Owners & Product Owners**

Need visibility into Application and Service TCO, cost drivers, trends, and consumption
patterns to guide investment and optimization decisions.

**IT Operations & Infrastructure Teams**

Depend on high-quality asset and relationship data from ServiceNow Discovery and Service
Mapping to support consumptive cost modeling and Infrastructure TCO.

**Enterprise Architects (EA)**

Use ServiceNow EA/APM data enriched with IBM Apptio TCO metrics to drive rationalization,
modernization, and application portfolio decisions.

**Digital Portfolio Management (DPM) & Solution Leaders**

Use IBM Apptio TCO insights to drive plan–build–run lifecycle decisions and govern digital
products holistically in ServiceNow DPM.

## Use Cases

**Create a Trusted, Automated, Service-Aware CMDB Foundation**

The integration uses ServiceNow’s CMDB, CSDM structures, and automated Discovery as the
authoritative source for infrastructure, application, and service relationships. IBM Apptio
pulls this data through prescriptive Datalink connectors that automatically ingest,
normalize, and map key datasets, thereby eliminating manual extracts and reducing data
quality issues.

With continuously refreshed operational data, IBM Apptio can apply true consumptive
allocations rather than assumptions, producing accurate, defensible Application and Service
TCO. This provides a reliable foundation for chargeback, cost driver insights, and
TBM-aligned reporting.

**Consumptive, Defensible Application & Service TCO**

The integration aligns ServiceNow technical and business services to IBM Apptio TBM
Solutions, enabling:

- Application and Service TCO reporting
- Chargeback/showback
- Insight into cost drivers (labor, infra, software, storage, tickets, incidents, tasks)
- Trend analysis across environments and portfolios

IBM Apptio egress connectors also return TCO metrics back into native ServiceNow dashboards
for use by Service Owners, Application Owners, and Portfolio Managers.

**Application Rationalization with Enterprise Architecture**

By pushing IBM Apptio TCO data into ServiceNow EA/APM, EA teams gain:

- Full cost visibility for each business application
- Insights into which applications to retain, modernize, consolidate, or retire
- Alignment of TCO with Application Investment Objectives

This unifies architectural, operational, and financial data for more informed
rationalization decisions.

**Digital Portfolio Management (DPM) Optimization**

Bringing IBM Apptio cost, consumption, and investment insights into DPM unlocks end-to-end
portfolio governance:

- One shared view for planning, building, running, and costing digital products
- Ability to track cost vs. value across product lifecycles
- Alignment of product roadmaps with financial performance

**Enhanced Chargeback, Showback & Business Unit Transparency**

With ServiceNow’s accurate mapping of CIs to Applications, Services, and Business Units,
IBM Apptio can:

- Allocate costs based on actual consumption
- Improve trust in IT charges
- Increase stakeholder adoption and accountability

## Outcomes

**Unified Operational and Financial Decision-Making**

By blending CMDB operational data with IBM Apptio’s financial intelligence, teams gain
shared insights across IT Finance, I&O, EA, Service Owners, and Business Units. This
enables faster, more defensible decisions and accelerates transformation initiatives with
clear ROI.

**End-to-End Visibility across the Application and Service Lifecycle**

The integration links Infrastructure, Applications, Services, and Business Units into a
single view of consumption, cost, performance, and value. Leaders can understand how
technology supports the business and make more informed portfolio and investment decisions.

**Stronger Alignment to TBM and CSDM Standards**

The integration reinforces the structure of both frameworks by aligning CSDM operational
models with TBM financial taxonomy. This ensures consistent definitions, cleaner
hierarchies, and better collaboration across architecture, operations, and finance teams.

**Improved Data Quality and Higher-Fidelity Cost Models**

ServiceNow’s automated Discovery and service mapping provide continuously updated
infrastructure and application data. IBM Apptio uses this to drive more accurate consumptive
allocations and to eliminate common issues caused by stale or incomplete CMDB records.

**Faster Time to Value**

Prescriptive connectors and a streamlined onboarding approach reduce manual effort and
accelerate adoption. Organizations can stand up defensible TCO reporting, chargeback, and
cost transparency significantly faster while lowering ongoing maintenance overhead.
