# Service Desk Overview

## Overview

IT Service Management has evolved from delivering predictable IT services to enabling
sustained business value for customers and internal stakeholders. However, many
organizations struggle with limited visibility into service demand, fragmented service
taxonomies, and an inability to clearly connect service costs to business outcomes. As
service expectations increase around speed, quality, and flexibility, organizations also
face pressure to balance internal service delivery with external providers such as SaaS
platforms.

Without a unified view of service demand, cost drivers, and performance metrics,
organizations find it difficult to prioritize improvements, control service desk costs, and
align service operations with business needs.

Apptio Costing Standard enables organizations to manage their service desk portfolio
holistically by establishing a clear linkage between service demand, service delivery, and
underlying cost components. It provides detailed cost transparency across labor,
applications, platforms, hardware, software, and external services, enabling data-driven
service management and accountability.

## Personas

**IT Finance** - Reviews service desk spend, monitors budget performance, and analyzes
cost drivers across ticket volumes and categories.

**Service Desk Manager** - Oversees service desk operations, monitors ticket volumes,
performance metrics, and cost efficiency across priorities and severities.

**IT Service Owners**- Evaluates service consumption patterns, identifies cost-intensive
services, and drives service improvement initiatives.

## Use Cases

**Service Desk Cost Transparency**

Understand the total cost of service desk operations and how costs are distributed across
ticket types, categories, and services.

**Ticket Volume and Cost Analysis**

Analyze ticket volumes and costs by severity, priority, category, location, and service to
identify cost drivers and inefficiencies.

**Service Performance and Efficiency**

Track metrics such as mean time to resolve (MTTR), cost per ticket, and ticket trends to
assess service effectiveness and operational health.

## Outcomes

- Improved visibility into service desk costs and demand drivers.
- Clear understanding of which ticket types and services consume the most resources.
- Better alignment between service costs, service quality, and business expectations.
- Data-driven decisions to optimize service desk operations and control costs.

## Available Service Desk Solutions

Apptio offers two Service Desk solutions to support cost transparency, operational insight,
and service optimization across IT service management. Both solutions focus on understanding
service desk demand and cost drivers, but differ in depth of analysis and decision support.

**Service Desk Cost Overview (CT Apps – Service Desk)**

The Service Desk Cost Overview solution provides a foundational view of service desk costs
and ticket volumes across the organization. It is designed for teams seeking quick
visibility into ticket spend, cost per ticket, and basic breakdowns by category, priority,
severity, and service without deep operational complexity. This solution supports budget
reviews, high-level service performance tracking, and early service optimization discussions
through a focused set of summary reports.

To explore the Service Desk Cost Overview solution, go [here](sd-reports.html#sdrep__sdco).

**Service Desk Insights & Optimization (BI – Service Desk Insights)**

The Service Desk Insights & Optimization solution delivers deeper, operationally
focused analytics into service desk performance and cost drivers. It enables detailed
analysis of ticket costs, volumes, priorities, severities, locations, and services to
support ongoing operational and strategic reviews. This solution helps IT Operations
identify high-cost services, understand demand patterns, and prioritize improvement
initiatives using actionable KPIs and drill-down analysis.

To explore the Service Desk Insights & Optimization solution, go [here](sd-reports.html#sdrep__sddio).
