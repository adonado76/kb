# Compute Overview

## Overview

Compute provides financial and operational transparency into the Compute resource tower
across on-premise, private cloud, and public cloud environments. It enables organizations to
understand the true cost, unit economics, and utilization of physical and virtual compute
resources by combining infrastructure, software, labor, and support costs. This visibility
supports informed decisions around optimization, capacity planning, refresh strategies, and
hybrid infrastructure management.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**: Reviews compute costs, unit rates, and optimization opportunities to
  support budgeting, forecasting, and investment decisions.
- **Service Owners:** Manage compute platforms, utilization, and efficiency across
  on-premise and cloud-based infrastructure.
- **Solution and Application Owners**: Understand the cost and performance impact of
  compute resources supporting applications.
- **FinOps / Cloud Center of Excellence (CCoE)**: Evaluate hybrid compute efficiency
  and support optimization and cloud adoption initiatives

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Hybrid Compute TCO**

  Monitor and compare compute costs across on-premise, private cloud, and public cloud
  environments to understand total cost of ownership at the Compute tower level.
- **Server Utilization and Efficiency**

  Analyze CPU and memory utilization across physical and virtual servers to identify
  underutilized capacity and reduce unnecessary compute spend.
- **Hybrid Infrastructure Optimization**

  Identify cost and performance optimization opportunities across the full compute
  landscape and promote right-sizing aligned with business demand.
- **Track and Communicate Compute Cost Savings**

  Capture and report realized and projected savings from compute optimization
  initiatives across all infrastructure types.

## Outcomes

- Clear visibility into compute cost drivers and unit economics across hybrid
  infrastructure.
- Reduced compute costs through improved utilization, right-sizing, and capacity
  optimization.
- Better-informed decisions for infrastructure refresh, consolidation, and cloud
  migration.
- Stronger alignment between IT, finance, and application teams around efficient compute
  consumption.

## Available Compute Solutions

Apptio offers two Compute solutions to support cost transparency, utilization analysis, and
optimization across on-prem, public cloud, and hybrid infrastructure. Both solutions address
compute cost visibility, but differ in depth, analytical maturity, and operational insight.

**Compute Cost Overview (CT Apps– Servers)**

The Compute Cost Overview solution
provides a foundational view of compute spend and total cost ofownership across the
enterprise. It is designed for organizations seeking quick visibility into compute costs,
high-level capacity metrics, and server TCO without deep operational complexity. This
solution supports early optimization discussions, budget alignment, and initial
private-to-public cloud assessments through a small set of focused reports, including
Infrastructure Summary and Server TCO.

To learn more about the Compute Cost
Overview solution, go [here](compute-reports.html#comprep__cco).

**Compute Insights & Optimization** (BI- Server Insights)

The Compute
Insights & Optimization solution delivers comprehensive, operationally rich analytics
across the entire compute landscape. It enables detailed analysis of cost, capacity,
utilization, unit costs, and application-level server consumption across physical,
virtual, and cloud infrastructure. This solution supports advanced optimization,
consolidation planning, lifecycle decisions, and hybrid cloud strategy by combining
financial, operational, and utilization metrics with actionable KPIs and deep drill-downs.

It helps answer critical questions such as:

- Where compute costs are incurred and whether spend aligns with infrastructure strategy
- Which server platforms require expansion, retirement, or migration
- How unit costs (per server hour, instance hour, core, or GB) are trending
- Which applications and workloads are driving compute demand and cost anomalies
- Where opportunities exist to optimize utilization or shift workloads to public cloud

To explore the full set of detailed Compute reports and advanced analytics available
with the Compute Insights & Optimization solution, go [here](compute-reports.html#comprep__cio).
