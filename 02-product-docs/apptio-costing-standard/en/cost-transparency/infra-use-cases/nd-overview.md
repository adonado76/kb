# Network Devices Overview

## Overview

Across many organizations, network and communication costs remain difficult to predict and
control. Unexpected cost increases often arise from network downtime, overspecified
bandwidth or software, underutilized links, and poor visibility into network asset
lifecycles. Organizations also struggle to accurately and defensibly allocate communication
expenses to business units, especially as sites expand, contract, or shut down. These
challenges result in inflated network spend, budget overruns, and operational risk due to
limited insight into where and how optimization opportunities exist.

Apptio Costing Standard enables organizations to gain transparency into network assets,
costs, and utilization by combining financial, contractual, and operational data. This
allows continuous review of network infrastructure, proactive lifecycle management, and
identification of inefficiencies and waste. With better visibility, organizations can
optimize network spend, avoid unexpected charges, and ensure network services remain aligned
with business needs and service-level expectations.

## Personas

- IT Finance - Reviews network and communication spend, monitors budget performance, and
  analyzes cost drivers across vendors, sites, and services.
- IT Leaders - Oversee network strategy and investments, assess cost efficiency, and
  ensure network services align with business and operational objectives.
- Service Owners - Manage network service delivery, evaluate service-level performance,
  and identify opportunities to optimize cost while maintaining reliability.
- Network and Infrastructure Owners - Monitor network assets, contracts, and utilization
  to support lifecycle management, capacity planning, and optimization initiatives.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

**Network Utilization and Efficiency**

Monitor network lines, contracts, and utilization to optimize communication costs while
maintaining reliable service levels and minimizing downtime and productivity loss.

## Outcomes

- Improved transparency into network and communication spend across sites, services, and
  vendors
- Reduced unexpected costs through proactive lifecycle and contract management
- More accurate allocation of network expenses to business units and services
- Identification and elimination of waste caused by overspecification and underutilized
  capacity
- Stronger financial and operational control over network infrastructure investments
