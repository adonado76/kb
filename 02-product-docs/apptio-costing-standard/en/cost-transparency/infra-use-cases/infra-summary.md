# Summary

Infrastructure use cases within IBM Apptio Costing Standard focus on delivering financial
transparency, cost accountability, and optimization insights across core IT infrastructure
domains. These use cases help organizations understand where infrastructure spend occurs, how
it is consumed, and how costs can be optimized across on-premises, cloud, and hybrid
environments. This section represents the foundational infrastructure layer within the broader
Costing Standard capabilities.

**Compute**

Compute use cases address cost, capacity, utilization, and unit economics across physical
servers, virtual machines, and cloud instances. They support decisions around optimization,
consolidation, virtualization strategy, and migration to public cloud by providing a clear
view of total compute cost and consumption drivers.

**Storage**

Storage use cases focus on understanding storage cost, capacity, allocation, and utilization
across devices, tiers, and environments. These insights help identify over-provisioning,
under-utilization, lifecycle risks, and opportunities to optimize storage spend while
supporting application demand.

**Network Devices**

Network Devices use cases provide transparency into LAN, WAN, voice, and network equipment
costs. They enable defensible allocation of network spend to consuming infrastructure and
services, supporting cost control, optimization, and accurate application and service TCO.

**Mainframe**

Mainframe use cases deliver a comprehensive view of mainframe total cost, consumption, and
unit costs. They support accurate cost allocation, showback and chargeback, utilization
analysis, and optimization decisions for high-transaction workloads and critical business
applications.

**Data Centers**

Data Center use cases address the cost, capacity, and utilization of physical data center
infrastructure across locations. They support efficiency analysis, consolidation planning,
power and space optimization, and informed decisions around migrations and capacity
investments.

**Service Desk**

Service Desk use cases focus on understanding the cost of IT support operations by linking
ticket volume, severity, and effort to underlying costs. These insights help organizations
manage demand, improve efficiency, and align support spend with business value.

**Communications**

Communications use cases provide visibility into communication infrastructure costs such as
circuits, voice, and usage-based services. They enable transparent and defensible allocation
of communication costs to applications, services, and business units to support accurate TCO
and cost accountability.
