# Mainframe Consumption Review

Use this report to understand how mainframe costs are allocated, evaluate spending across
applications and time periods, and identify cost trends to support chargeback and financial
planning. Apply filters and available views to focus on the data relevant to your
analysis.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- Finance Managers
- Business Unit Leaders
- Department Heads
- Chargeback Administrators

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-mf-cons1.png)

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-mf-cons2.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Filter controls (1) | Three filters let you narrow the report by business unit, owner, and department. |
| Account summary (2) | Shows the reporting period and selected business unit and department filters. |
| Key metrics cards (3) | Three cards show projected annual cost, cost year to date, and current period cost. Each card includes comparison indicators showing changes from previous periods. |
| Dimension selector (4) | Use these radio buttons to select which dimension to view in the charts: Application Name or Business Unit. |
| Breakdown by category chart (5) | This horizontal bar chart shows cost breakdown by category, such as CC Suite, Switch Processing, LINQ (Limits and Utilization Inquiry Service), DeathSTAR, and Fraud Detection. |
| Trend by category chart (6) | This line chart shows cost trends over time by category, with monthly data points from August FY2021 to August FY2022. |
| Column selection panel (7) | This panel lets you show or hide columns in the table, such as Application ID, Application Name, Department Owner, and Owner. |
| Mainframe details table (8) | This table shows mainframe consumption data with columns such as business unit, department, application ID, application name, total application cost, percentage allocated, and allocated cost. Application names link to more detailed information. |

## Questions Answered

- How much is each business unit or department being charged for mainframe consumption?
- What is the projected annual cost compared to previous year spending?
- Which applications contribute most to each business unit's mainframe costs?
- How are application costs allocated across different departments?
- What are the cost trends for specific applications over time?
- How does current period spending compare to previous periods?
- Which business units show increasing or decreasing consumption patterns?

## Recommended Actions

- Filter by business unit or department to review specific cost allocations.
- Review the breakdown by category to understand which applications drive costs.
- Analyze trend data to identify applications with increasing or decreasing costs.
- Export allocation details to support chargeback billing processes.
- Compare current period costs to previous periods to identify anomalies.
- Review allocation percentages to ensure costs are distributed appropriately.
- Share consumption reports with business unit leaders to support budget planning.
