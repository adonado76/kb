# AI TCO and Usage

Use this report to analyze AI service spending and usage across models, solutions, and
business units, identifying cost optimization opportunities by lifecycle stage.

This report is intended for the following users:

- AI program managers
- IT finance or TBM analysts
- AI platform owners or service owners
- Business unit leaders

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-aitcou.png)

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-aitco1.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Summary cards (1) | Four cards at the top of the report show AI cost year to date, AI cost for the current period, AI token consumption, and AI solution users. Use these cards for a high-level view of AI spending and usage. |
| Filter options (2) | Five lists let you filter the report by model type, AI solution type, service category, lifecycle stage, and business unit. |
| Tab navigation (3) | Tabs switch between Summary, AI Models, AI Solutions, Business Units, and Definition views. |
| Pivot selector (4) | Use this list to change the dimension that organizes the charts and table, such as lifecycle stage. |
| AI cost rolling 6-month trend (5) | A stacked bar chart shows AI costs for a 6-month period, grouped by lifecycle stage, such as proof of concept, production, in service, pilot, and training. |
| Top 3 AI models chart (6) | A combined bar and line chart compares AI cost year to date and AI usage year to date for the top three AI models. |
| Top 3 AI solutions chart (6) | A combined bar and line chart compares AI cost year to date and AI usage year to date for the top three AI solutions. |
| Top 3 business units chart (6) | A combined bar and line chart compares AI cost year to date and average AI solution users for the top three business units. |
| AI cost and usage overview table (7) | This table includes configurable columns such as service offering, lifecycle stage, AI solution type, business criticality, service owner, service category, platform name, model name, vendor, usage unit of measure, current-month AI cost, and AI usage. You can show or hide columns and sort the table by column. |

## Questions Answered

- How much do AI solutions cost, and what percentage of the total IT budget do they represent?
- Which AI models have the highest costs and the highest usage?
- Which AI solutions have the highest costs and usage?
- How many users are using AI solutions across the organization?
- Is AI spending increasing or decreasing over time?
- Which business units have the highest AI spending?
- How is AI spending distributed across lifecycle stages, such as proof of concept, production,
  pilot, and training?
- Which AI solutions are mission critical, and which are business essential?
- Who owns each AI solution, and which service category is it assigned to?
- How does AI usage compare with AI cost?

## Recommended Actions

- Review the AI cost year-to-date card to understand current AI spending.
- Use the Top 3 AI models chart to compare AI cost and AI usage for the leading models.
- Review the AI cost rolling 6-month trend chart to identify changes in spending over time.
- Filter the report by lifecycle stage to compare spending across production, proof-of-concept,
  pilot, and other stages.
- Review the Top 3 business units chart to compare AI spending and user counts by business unit.
- Sort the AI cost and usage overview table by current-month AI cost to identify the most expensive
  AI services.
- Show the model name and vendor columns to compare cost and usage across models and vendors.
