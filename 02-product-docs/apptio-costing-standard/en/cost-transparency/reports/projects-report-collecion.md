# Projects Report Collection

This collection provides a single view of project OpEx and CapEx, broken down by labor,
vendors, and expense area for each. You can view project investment and spend alongside status,
priority, and budget.

Project investment is linked to the applications and business units they support:

- [Projects Review](project-review.html)
- [Projects Portfolio](project-portfolio.html)
- [Projects List](project-list.html)
- [Projects at Risk](projects-at-risk.html)
