# Workforce Overview

## Overview

Workforce provides visibility into an organization’s labor workforce, including internal,
external, contingent, and offshore resources. It helps organizations understand labor
composition, distribution by role and location, and the costs associated with labor planning
and management.

The capability supports analysis of labor mix, workforce utilization, headcount movement, and
labor-related financial metrics to improve workforce planning, cost control, and alignment
with business objectives.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**

  Reviews labor costs, workforce mix, and labor-related financial performance to support
  budgeting, forecasting, and cost control.
- **Cost Center Owner / Cost Center Manager**

  Monitors headcount, vacancies, workforce mix, and labor costs within their area of
  responsibility.
- **Service Owner / Solution Owner**

  Analyzes labor resources supporting services or solutions, including internal and
  external workforce, to ensure delivery targets are met efficiently.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Labor Cost Analysis**

  Understanding total labor cost and its drivers is essential before allocation or
  benchmarking can be effective.
- **Headcount Planning & Variance**

  This is the primary entry point for Workforce & Time Tracking. Customers first
  need visibility into approved vs. actual headcount to manage staffing and budget risk.
- **Labor Mix Optimization**

  Once headcount is visible, customers focus on optimizing internal, external,
  offshore, and contingent labor to balance cost and efficiency.
- **Labor Cost Allocation**

  This builds on cost visibility and enables accountability across projects,
  applications, products, services, and platforms.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Workforce Planning**

  Forecast and align workforce levels with operational and investment demands to
  minimize idle capacity and avoid skill shortages.
- **Capacity & Demand Forecast**

  Predict future personnel needs based on project pipelines and business-as-usual
  workloads to proactively manage staffing costs and resource allocation.
- **Skills & Resource Allocation**

  Align workforce skills to roles and tasks to reduce skill gaps and improve
  productivity and role effectiveness.
- **Labor Rate Benchmarking**

  Compare internal and vendor labor rates against industry benchmarks to identify
  cost-saving opportunities and pricing inefficiencies.

## Outcomes

- Improved alignment of headcount with budget, demand, and strategic priorities.
- Better control over workforce costs through visibility into labor mix, rates, and
  allocation.
- Reduced staffing risks through proactive workforce and capacity planning.
- Increased accountability for labor costs across projects, services, and solutions.
- More effective utilization of skills and resources to support delivery and productivity
  goals.
