# Vendor Overview

## Overview

Vendor reporting provides visibility into IT vendor spend across the organization, enabling
analysis of spend distribution, concentration, and trends across vendors. It helps
organizations understand how vendor costs contribute to IT services and initiatives,
supporting cost control, rationalization, and optimization decisions.

This capability focuses on **vendor spend analysis only**. For deeper visibility into
**contracts, purchase orders, or accounts payable**, refer to the **Vendor Insights
reports**, which extend vendor analysis with commercial and transactional views.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**
- Reviews vendor spend patterns, supports budgeting and forecasting, and identifies cost
  optimization opportunities.
- **Vendor Manager**
- Manages vendor relationships, evaluates vendor concentration, and supports
  rationalization initiatives.
- **Cost Center Owner**
- Monitors vendor spend within their area of responsibility and supports cost
  accountability.
- **Service Owner / Solution Owner**
- Understands vendor spend supporting services and solutions to assess cost
  efficiency.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Top Vendor Spend Analysis**
- Identify top-spending vendors to focus optimization, renegotiation, or consolidation
  efforts.
- **Track & Eliminate Vendors with the Least Spend**
- Identify low-spend vendors to reduce fragmentation, consolidate spend, and improve
  operational efficiency.
- **Strategic Vendor Classification & Rationalization**
- Classify vendors by strategic importance (for example, strategic, preferred, or
  transactional) to support vendor rationalization and drive economies of scale.
- **Vendor Spend Visibility**
- Provide transparency into vendor spend to improve budgeting, cost control, and
  financial oversight.
- **Vendor Consolidation & Rationalization**
- Identify overlapping vendor capabilities to reduce redundancy and optimize vendor
  portfolios.
- **Vendor Cost Allocation**
- Allocate vendor costs across cost centers, projects, and solution offerings to improve
  transparency and support TCO analysis.
- **Vendor Cost Analysis & Benchmarking**
- Analyze vendor spend patterns and compare costs across vendors or regions to support
  negotiation and optimization strategies.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Vendor Budget & Forecasting**
- Estimate and track vendor spend to improve budgeting accuracy and reduce forecast
  variance.
- **Vendor Portfolio Optimization**
- Evaluate vendor relationships holistically to build a cost-effective, high-performing
  vendor portfolio.

## Outcomes

- Improved visibility into vendor spend across the organization.
- Reduced vendor fragmentation through consolidation and rationalization.
- Better cost control and budgeting accuracy for vendor-driven spend.
- Stronger negotiation leverage through spend concentration insights.
- Increased transparency and accountability for vendor costs supporting IT services and
  solutions.
