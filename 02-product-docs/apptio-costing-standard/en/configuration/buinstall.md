# Install and configure the CT - Business Units component

The Business Units module allocates costs to the business units in your
enterprise.

## About this task

The module has one component: CT - Business Units. When you install the component, it adds the
Service Allocations Direct, Service Allocations Indirect, and Business Unit Allocation objects at
the top of the model and consolidates all relevant costs from the many objects in the model. The
result is that each business unit can see the total cost of the applications and services it
consumes.

**NOTICE**

The Applications and Services Module must be installed and configured before you can install the
Business Units component.

When you install the CT Apps - Business Units component, a new Business Unit Allocation group is
created with four tables:

- Business Unit Allocation (model table)
- Business Unit Allocation Master Data
- Service Allocations Direct (model table)
- Service Allocations Direct Master Data

**Parent topic:** [Costing Standard](../home.html)

## Master data

For a description of the fields in the master data tables, see the information on the CT
Apps - Business Units component page in the product. To display the page:

### Procedure

1. Click the **Project** tab in the Ribbon.
2. Click **Components** in the Ribbon.
3. Click the **CT Apps - Business Units** component.
