# Business Unit Review

## Business Unit Top Spend

![Business unit top spend report](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/business%20unit%20review.jpg)

- Key Elements
  - Review KPIs on Business Unit Spend Analysis
  - Review Business Unit Spend on Top Business Units
  - Review Project Investments by Business Initiative, Business Sponsor, Spend Type

- Questions Answered
  - How does IT spend compare across BUs? Across revenue generating vs. non-revenue generating
    BUs?
  - What is the average IT spend per employee in each BU?
  - How does IT spend trend over time?
  - What is the total staff?

## Business Unit Detail

![A screenshot of a cell phone Description automatically generated](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/business%20unit%20review_1.jpg)

- Key Elements
  - Review Business Unit Spend in Detail
  - Review Business Unit Investments
  - Review Business Unit Direct vs. Indirect spend

- Questions Answered
  - How does IT spend compare across BUs? Across revenue generating vs. non-revenue generating
    BUs?
  - What is the average IT spend per employee in each BU?
  - What is the breakdown of Direct vs. Indirect

## Business Unit Detail by Applications & Projects Drilldown

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/business%20unit%20review_1.jpg)

- Key Elements
  - Review individual Business Unit in detail
  - Review Business Unit Run vs. Change
  - Review Business Unit Applications and Investments

- Questions Answered
  - What is the breakdown of Run vs. Change?
  - What are the applications that are contributing to this BU and how are they trending?
  - What Projects is this Business Unit investing in?

## Project Spend Details Drilldown

![A screenshot of a social media post Description automatically generated](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/business%20unit%20review_2.jpg)

- Key Elements
  - Review Project Trends
  - Review Projects Details

- Questions Answered
  - What are the trends of the projects? Are there any outliers?
  - Who is the Business Sponsor for each project and are they on track?
  - What is the current investment YTD compared to Approved Budget?
