# Data Center Report Collection

Use the Data Center (DC) report collection to compare and contrast cost, capacity, and
utilization across your data centers. The reports allow you to analyze your data center spend,
better understand your data centers with key metrics, and track efficiency over various time
frames.

Use this report collection to understand the overall financial health of your data center
infrastructure against plan, with actionable, insightful KPIs and high-level analytics. This
information is especially helpful in isolating and understanding anomalous spend.

This report collection is designed for the following roles:

- Director of IT
- Head of Operations
- DC operations manager

This report collection aligns to the following business goals:

- View and compare data center costs
- View cost per kilowatt and square foot
- Understand how data center statistics, such as power capacity, total rack space, and square
  footage, relate to overall efficiency

These reports enable you to determine the following:

- Which data centers cost the least per unit to operate
- The capacity available at each data center
