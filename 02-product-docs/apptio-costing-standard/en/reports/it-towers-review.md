# IT Towers Review

## Summary

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/it%20towers%20review.png)

Question answered:

- Is Cost in line with Budget? What is our remaining budget for the year? What is the budget
  depletion point?
- Which towers have the most cost? Is this in line with expectations?
- How are Cost Pools driving tower costs? Is there anything unexpected? Which cost pool is driving
  the most tower costs?
- Are there any unexpected changes in tower spend trends?
- What is the Cost, Budget, and Variance based on the IT resource Tower and IT Resource sub Tower
  ?

## IT Resource Towers Drilldown

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/it%20towers%20review.jpg)

Questions answered:

- Which sub towers are driving the variance for this tower?
- Is the variance consistent? Drill in to Tx (See screenshot Below) to see what transactions are
  driving it.
- How are costs trending period over period?

## IT Sub Towers Drilldown

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/it%20towers%20review_1.jpg)

- How are your quantity and average unit costs trending over time?

## Sub Tower by Vendor Detail

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/it%20towers%20review_2.jpg)

Questions answered:

- Which vendors are driving the costs for this sub tower? Are these project-based vendor costs or
  support / contract costs?
- Is this one of our strategic partners?
- Where can I find more details on these vendors?

## IT Tower Quantity & Unit Cost Detail

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/ct%20report%20collections/it%20towers%20review_3.jpg)

Questions answered:

- What changes to quantities and unit costs affected costs this quarter? Are those changes
  systemic or a one-off?
- What is the average unit cost by sub tower? How do these compare to the trailing 12-month cost
  and the targets that were set?
- How are unit costs trending?
