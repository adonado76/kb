## Out of the Box Reports

- [Consumer Review](out_of_the_box_reports/business-unit-review.html "This report reviews your organization's business unit consumption broken out by key spend drivers. It also analyzes the spend types and categories that your business units are consuming.")
- [Financial Review](out_of_the_box_reports/financial-review.html "This report provides an executive view of the overall OpEx budget variance and OpEx spend of your organization. The report breaks down IT cost per cost pool and owner so you can determine which IT owners are responsible for the largest IT spend. Various charts in the report also help you determine whether variances are real or caused by miscategorization.")
- [Labor Review](out_of_the_box_reports/labor-review.html "This report provides high-level metrics related to the cost and headcount of internal labor and external contractors, including the number and nature of open positions, the balance of internal versus external labor, and the percentage of labor spend.")
- [Project Review](out_of_the_box_reports/project-review.html "This collection provides a single view of project OpEx and CapEx, broken down by labor, vendors, and expense area for each. You can view project investment and spend alongside status, priority, and budget. Project investment is linked to the applications and business units they support:")
- [Solutions Review](out_of_the_box_reports/solutions-review.html "This report reviews your top application and service spend (broadly categorized as Solutions) and allows organizations to visualize what apps and/or services (and service types) are consuming the most spend.")
- [Vendor Review](out_of_the_box_reports/vendor-review.html "This report reviews your vendor portfolio and the associated spend across strategic, preferred, and transactional vendors.")
- [Model Views](out_of_the_box_reports/model-views-ce.html)
