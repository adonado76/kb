# Introduction to IBM Costing Essentials

## About the Costing Essentials

Costing Essentials combines financial and organizational data associated with IT spend in a
unified model, automatically classifying and allocating key spend drivers for visibility and
transparency for organizations. Costing Essentials provides the opportunity for more efficient
utilization of resources, cost optimization, improved business alignment and the ability to
capitalize on shifting market conditions and opportunities more quickly. Areas of coverage and focus
include labor, vendor, projects, cloud and contracts while providing views into applications,
services, and business units. Costing Essentials uses a simplified cost model to allow users to
allocate IT costs to the business units, services, or applications based on headcount or other
similar (non-consumptive type) allocation strategies.
