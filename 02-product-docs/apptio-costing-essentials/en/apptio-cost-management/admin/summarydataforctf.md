# Summary of data sources needed for Costing Essentials Foundation

To configure Costing Essentials Foundation, you'll need the following data sets:

- Actual Headcount by Cost Center
- Actual Headcount by Cost Center
- Budget (OpEx and CapEx)
- Chart of Accounts
- Cloud Invoice
- Contract details
- General Ledger (OpEx and CapEx)
- List of Business Units
- List of Cost Centers
- List of Fixed Assets
- List of Projects
- List of Services, Applications, or Products
- List of Vendors
- Planned Headcount by Cost Center
