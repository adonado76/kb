# Organization Mapping

Provides ability to complete data enrichment for your IT Organizations (Business
Units).

## Organization Data Enrichment

Use this ET to update your IT Organizations (Business Units) :​

- Department Name​
- Business Unit​
- Business Unit Type​
- Business Unit Leader​
- Governing Body​
- Business Relationship Manager​
- Internal Headcount​
- External Headcount
