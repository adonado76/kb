# Vendor Review

This report reviews your vendor portfolio and the associated spend across strategic,
preferred, and transactional vendors.

## Use cases

This report solves the following use cases:

- Understand vendor portfolio. Identify the top 10 vendors and their associated spend.
- Categorize vendors by strategic importance.
- Track committed spend.
- Track and eliminate vendors with the least spend.
- Track and control top vendor spend.
- Map vendor contracts to technology offerings.
- Identify expiring contracts prior to renewal.
- Categorize and track vendor spend by strategic importance and function.

## Personas

This report is designed for use by the following roles:

- Application / Platform Owner
- IT Finance
- Vendor / Supplier / Procurement Manager

## Questions answered

The report answers the following questions:

- What vendors are costing my organization the most money?
- How do I understand their OpEx and CapEx impact across my organization?
- What changes should we make to re-balance vendor spend?
- How fragmented / concentrated is spending across vendors?
- Do we have redundant vendors?
- Where do we have variances in spend?

| Key element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| (1) Report collection | This report collection provides the vendor details you need to review your spend variances and forecast accuracy:  - Vendor Review GL (default view) - Vendor Review AP - Vendor Analysis - Contracts - Purchase Orders |
| (2) KPIs | KPIs provide a high-level view of the following:  • Vendor OpEx YTD: This KPI shows your Vendor OpEx YTD spend vs the Vendor CapEx YTD.  • Strategic Vendor Spend: This KPI shows your strategic vendor spend vs the preferred vendor spend.  • Strategic Vendor Count This KPI shows your trategic vendor count vs the preferred vendor count.  • Addressable: This KPI helps you determine the agility of your IT spend by looking at the ratio of fixed and variable expenditures for the fiscal year. |
| (3) Top vendor details | Analyze the top vendor details and annual target spends. Also analyze top 10 vendor spends and trending spends. |
| (4) Vendor spend details | View the vendor spend details by cost center (default view), vendor category, cost pool, and project. Select the time period to customize the table with the metrics you want to see. |
