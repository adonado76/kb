# Projects Review

This collection provides a single view of project OpEx and CapEx, broken down by labor,
vendors, and expense area for each. You can view project investment and spend alongside status,
priority, and budget. Project investment is linked to the applications and business units they
support:

Important: he content for this section is work in progress.

## Use cases

This report solves the following use cases:

- Analyzing the project’s business initiative and the impact it had on the business
- Reviewing the list, details, status, performance, and goals of all the projects

## Personas

- All users

## Questions answered

The report answers the following questions:

- How are we trending compared to budget spend?
- How much are we spending compared to total spend?
- What percentage of projects started on time and completed on budget?
- What is our YTD project spend based on Business Initiative?
- How much are we spending on all Projects compared to Approved Budget?
- How did costs compare to approved Budget?

| (3) Project Spend (current Period) | Analyze the following:  • project spends, project details and the trend for of the current period. |
| (4) All Project Spend | You can do the following:  • Review all the Project Details  • Understand the details behind all projects including project Budget.  • Review all Projects and Business Initiative for the project  • Review Approved Budget to Budget Remaining  • Review the trend for all the projects by business initiative, project portfolio, business sponsor, spend type, and project manager. |
