# Solutions Review

This report reviews your top application and service spend (broadly categorized as
Solutions) and allows organizations to visualize what apps and/or services (and service types) are
consuming the most spend.

## Use cases

This report solves the following use cases:

- Analyze vendor spend directly associated with a specific technology offering or higher-level
  category to identify overlapping and/or redundant suppliers.
- Analyze project or product investment spend directly associated with a specific technology
  offering or higher-level category.
- Analyze internal and external labor costs and activity type by role and by location for specific
  technology offerings.
- Understand the primary, direct cost drivers across the technology offering portfolio, broken out
  by labor, vendor products and services, and project or product investments.
- Allocate technology offering spend to the consuming organizations using prescriptive direct and
  indirect methods

## Personas

- Application / Platform Owner
- IT Finance

## Questions answered

The report answers the following questions:

- What apps and services make up the most spend in my organization?
- How much of my application / service spend is split across various categories and types?

| Key element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| (1) Report collection | This report collection provides the following solution details:  - Solutions Overview (default view) - Solution Offerings - Business and Corporate - Infra and Cloud - Cloud Summary |
| (2) Total Spend YTD by Category | Analyze the total spend chart and the trend report of the fiscal year by solution type or solution category. |
| (3) Costs by Type and Category | View the costs by solution type and solution category. Select the time period to customize the table with the metrics you want to see. |
| (4) KPIs | KPIs provide a high-level view of the total spending in the fiscal year for different solutions like business, workplace, delivery, infrastructure, platform, and shared& corporate. |

## Solution Offerings

This report displays the following:

- Total spend YTD by Offerings, OnPrem/SaaS solutions, and Trends
- KPIs for OpEx YTD, CapEx YTD, Deprication YTD
- Offering details by offering id, unit costs and targets, etc.

## Business and Corporate

This report displays the business and corporate spends for the following:

- Yearly trend charts and summary of On Prem and Public Cloud deployments
- KPIs for cloud business/on prem/cloud corporate/on prem corporate solutions
