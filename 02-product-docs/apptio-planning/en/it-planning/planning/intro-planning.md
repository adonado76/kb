# Introduction to Apptio Planning

Apptio Planning is a collaborative financial planning and forecasting solution designed
for technology, finance, and business teams to create, manage, and analyze budgets, forecasts,
and investment plans with transparency and control.

With Apptio Planning, you can align technology spend to business priorities, manage costs
more effectively, and make faster, data-driven decisions.

**Core Capabilities** 

- **[Budgeting & Forecasting](plan-states.html "Apptio Planning uses Plan States to control the lifecycle of a plan. Each state governs who can access the plan, what actions can be taken, and when data is locked for reporting. This ensures version control and proper governance during the planning process."):** Create detailed
  budget and forecast plans at the department, project, labor, contract, and asset level.
- **[Labor Planning:](labor-planning.html "The Labor Planning module enables organizations to plan and budget for workforce-related expenses — including salaries, benefits, taxes, and associated cost allocations. It helps you model staffing needs, assign costs to departments, and align workforce strategy with business priorities.")**Plan for headcount, salaries, and labor costs.
- **[Contract Planning](manage-contracts.html "The Contract Planning module enables you to budget, forecast, and manage vendor and supplier contracts within your IT financial planning model. By modeling contracts — their commitment, cost accrual, and amortization — you gain greater visibility into fixed and recurring obligations, supporting better decision-making around renewals, spend commitments, and expense timing."):** Manage contracts
  with support for multiple amortization methods.
- **[Asset Planning](manage-assets.html "The Asset Planning module enables you to budget, forecast, and manage capital investments in fixed assets. Fixed assets are long-term tangible resources—such as servers, hardware, or equipment—that a business owns and uses over multiple years. These assets are capitalized and depreciated over time rather than expensed immediately."):** Track assets with
  configurable depreciation schedules.
- **[Project Planning](iip/gs-integrated-investment-planning.html):**Prioritize and manage projects, while capturing and analyzing project costs across
  both build and run activities.
- **[Project Labor Activity Planning](iip/labor-resource-planning.html):**Capture labor activity and cross-charges to ensure complete project cost visibility.
- **[Variance Analysis](analyze-budget-variance.html "The Variance Analysis feature helps you compare a current forecast plan against a baseline (budget or previous forecast), identify significant differences, and build a commentary trail that explains the drivers behind variances."):** Perform
  variance analysis to identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities for cost optimization.
- **[Workflows & Collaboration](approval-workflows.html):**Submit, review, approve, and return plans using configurable approval workflows.
- **[User Permissions](user-role-perm.html):** Control user access
  with role-based permissions and data-level security.
- **[Analytics & Reporting](analyze-plan-forecast.html "The Summary pageprovides interactive visualizations of your plan—displaying direct and indirect expenses, and if enabled, project labor charges tied to headcount data. Use this page to explore financial performance, identify trends, and uncover variances."):** Analyze
  plans and forecasts using built-in reporting and KPI dashboards.
- **[Integrations](adm/adm_capabilities.html "Apptio Planning integrates with Apptio Costing using Automated Data Management (ADM). ADM is a shared platform service designed to provide a unified, secure, and scalable data exchange experience across Apptio applications."):** Connect seamlessly
  with Apptio Costing and other data sources.
