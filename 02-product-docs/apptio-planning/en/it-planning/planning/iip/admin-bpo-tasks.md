# Admin or Budget Process Owner tasks

Important: *Available with the* ***Apptio Planning Standard****subscription*

◆ Applies to: Costing & Planning and Costing & Planning Plus customers that do not
have Project Financial Planning or Service Demand
Planning enabled. If you are using Project Financial Planning
or Service Demand Planning and want to access Integrated Investment Planning, contact your Customer Success Team.

Admin and Budget Process Owner can do the following tasks:

- Enable Integrated Investment Planning feature.

Under ![settings](../../../../../../03-media/apptio-planning/resources/images/icons/icon-settings_20x20.png) > Company Profile, select Enable Integrated
Investment Planning, and save the changes.
