# Adjust Baseline Values

Note: Admin or Budget Process Owner roles are required to adjust baseline values.

After creating a plan, you may need to adjust the baseline values before making the plan
accessible to budget owners. These adjustments can be applied globally across all Departments
and Accounts or targeted to specific Departments and Accounts.

## How to Adjust Baseline Amounts

1. Open the plan and navigate to the **Expenses** page, then select the **Other**
   tab.
2. From the **Actions** menu, choose **Adjust Amounts...**
3. Choose an **Adjustment Type**:
   1. **Percentage** – Adjust values by a percentage of the original amount.
   2. **Absolute** – Adjust by a fixed currency amount (only available when multiple
      currencies are not enabled).
4. In **Adjustment Amount**:
   1. For **Percentage**, enter a whole number (e.g., 10 for +10%, -5 for –5%).
   2. For **Absolute**, enter a currency amount.
5. Use the **Apply To** options to choose the scope of the adjustment by Department and
   Account.
6. Select a **start** and **end period** in the **Period Range**.
7. Click **Adjust** to apply the change.

## Important Notes

- The plan must be in the **New** state to apply adjustments.
- You can still manually adjust individual lines afterward by navigating into specific
  Cost Objects and editing monthly values directly.
- **There is no undo** — adjustments are permanent once applied.
