# Revise a plan or forecast

A plan approver may need to return budget plans or forecasts to budget owners for various
reasons.

## About this task

A plan approver may need to return budget plans or forecasts to budget owners for the following
reasons:

- A specific line or aggregate item requires adjustment.
- A broad adjustment based on new guidance or priorities from the Budget Process Owner is
  required.

A plan approver may need to return a budget plan or forecast to budget owners. When a plan is
returned to you, a red banner is displayed, stating that the plan has been returned.

## Procedure

1. View comments from approvers.

   On the red banner, click Read Comment.
2. Revise and resubmit a plan.

   - To manually create a new version of a plan, click Create a new version in
     the red banner.
   - Begin editing your budget values. When you do so, the version number will automatically
     increment.

   Tip: If your review process involves multiple approvers, we recommend you preface your
   comments with your name or initials and the date.
