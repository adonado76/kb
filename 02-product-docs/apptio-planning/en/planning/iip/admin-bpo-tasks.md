# Admin or Budget Process Owner tasks

◆ Applies to: Costing & Planning and Costing & Planning Plus customers that do
not have Project Financial Planning or Service Demand Planning enabled. If you are using Project
Financial Planning or Service Demand Planning and want to access Integrated Investment Planning,
contact your Customer Success Team.

Admin and Budget Process Owner can do the following tasks:

- Enable Integrated Investment Planning feature.

Under ![](../../../resources/images/icons/icon-settings_20x20.png) > Company Profile,
select Enable Integrated Investment Planning, and save the changes.
