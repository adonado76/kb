# Finalize a plan or forecast

## About this task

After a Budget Process Owner has reviewed the summary for the budget plan or forecast and
completed all necessary adjustments, they are ready to finalize the plan.

## Procedure

**To finalize a plan or forecast:**

1. Navigate to
   Planning > Plans and select a
   plan.
2. Click Finalize Plan.
3. Click Finalize. The plan's status is changed to
   `Final`.

   Tip: If needed, you can reopen a previously-finalized plan
   for further editing. For more information, see <open-plan-forecast.html#reopen>.
