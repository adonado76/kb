# Training Objectives

*This section outlines how to train different audiences so Billing is actually used and trusted, not just technically deployed. Use it to design onboarding, recurring training, and handoffs when people change roles.*

## Training objectives

Across all roles, training should help people:

- Understand what Billing is and where it fits with Costing.
- Know which reports they are expected to use and when.
- Understand enough of the data and model to trust the numbers and raise good questions.
- Run the monthly cycle with minimal hand-holding (for Admins and Analysts).

You do not need uniform depth for everyone. The goal is targeted competence, not everyone learning TBM Studio.
