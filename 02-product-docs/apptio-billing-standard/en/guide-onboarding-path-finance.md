# Onboarding path for Finance

*Finance training should be practical: how to reconcile and post, not how to write ETL.*

## Conceptual alignment

Topics :

- How Billing relates to Costing and the GL.
- Difference between showback and chargeback in this implementation.
- What Billing journals represent (and do not represent).

## Journal and reconciliation training

Focus :

- Journal export reports and their column layout.
- Mapping from Billing fields to GL segments.
- Reconciliation steps: Billing total vs journal total. Billing vs Costing recoverable cost.

Activities :

- Walk through one full period: Open the invoice summary, journal export, and actual GL posting. Show how to reconcile the three. Agree how to handle corrections and late changes.
