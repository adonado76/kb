# Training Scope by Role

*This subsection summarizes what each role needs to learn. The detailed training matrix can be documented separately.*

## Admins & Analysts

Core responsibilities:

- Maintain Billing configuration in TBM Studio (or coordinate with a partner/IBM team).
- Run the monthly billing cycle and QA checks.
- Support investigations and questions from other roles.

Training focus:

- Sections 3 through 16 of this guide, with deeper emphasis on: Architecture, environments, and data flows. Configuring Billing Essentials and/or Billing Standard. Running models, validating outputs, and generating exports. Advanced topics that are in scope (cloud, projects, transfer pricing).

## Service and Product Owners

Core responsibilities:

- Understand how their services/products are billed and to whom.
- Review and approve bills for their area.
- Provide input on catalog, units, and rates.

Training focus:

- Conceptual overview of Billing and its relationship to Costing.
- How to read invoice-style reports, detail reports, and unit-rate views.
- How to interpret trends and raise targeted questions.
- For Billing Standard: how domain views (cloud, applications, infrastructure, projects) relate to their services.

## Finance

Core responsibilities:

- Use Billing for internal revenue and cost allocations where chargeback is in scope.
- Reconcile Billing outputs to Costing and the general ledger.
- Support discussions on plan vs actual vs price and under/over recovery.

Training focus:

- How Billing calculates charges at a high level.
- Journal export reports and required segment mappings.
- Reconciliation flows: Billing to Costing, Billing to GL.
- For Billing Standard: how variance and transfer pricing reports work.

## Senior Leaders & other Stakeholders

Core responsibilities:

- Use Billing to support decisions, not to maintain configuration.

Training focus:

- What questions Billing can answer reliably.
- Which summary reports and dashboards to use.
- How to interpret trends and variances at a high level.
- Who to contact for deeper analysis.
