# Plan vs actual vs price

*Applies primarily to Billing Standard, but the concepts can be used in a simplified way with Billing Essentials if plan data is available.*

## Data needed

To compare plan vs actual vs price, you need:

- Actual cost From Costing outputs.
- Plan or budget From planning tools, spreadsheets, or manually maintained tables. Aligned to the same services and consumers used in Billing.
- Price From Billing rate or price tables.

## Reporting patterns

Useful views:

- For each service and period: Plan cost per unit. Actual cost per unit. Price per unit. Variance columns: Actual vs plan. Price vs cost.
- For each consumer and period: Plan charges. Actual charges. Variance and variance percentage.

Use these views to support:

- Annual and quarterly rate reviews.
- Discussions about under recovery or over recovery.
- Adjustments to future rates or service design.
