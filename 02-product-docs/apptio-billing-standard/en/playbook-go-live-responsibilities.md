# Go-live responsibilities

*It helps to be explicit about who owns what during go-live.*

Typical allocation:

- Admin / Analyst team:Owns model runs, QA checks, and troubleshooting in Billing and Costing.
- Service or Product Owners:Validate that bills make sense for their services.
- Finance: Validates journal outputs and reconciliations.
- Technology leadership:Owns the decision that Billing is ready to be the single source of truth for the agreed scope.

You do not need a complicated RACI chart, but you do need a simple, shared understanding of who is on the hook for each part of the playbook.
