# Onboarding path for Service and Product Owners

*This path is shorter and focused on interpretation, not configuration.*

## Intro session

Goal : Explain what Billing will do for them.

Topics :

- What Billing is and is not.
- How their services or products appear in Billing.
- How units and rates are defined.

## Hands-on session

Goal : Make them comfortable using the reports they will see monthly.

Focus :

- Navigate to the correct Billing reports.
- Slice charges by service, consumer, and period.
- Drill from summary to detail.
- Export to a spreadsheet when deeper analysis is needed.

Activities :

- Review the last dress rehearsal or test period: Ask them to explain a few lines of their bill out loud: What service is this? Who is being charged? Does this pattern make sense based on what actually happened?
