# Benchmark data sources

Benchmarking blends external datasets with your own information.

- Market and research providers that collect IT spend and staffing data across many organizations.

- IT spend as percentage of revenue
- IT spend per employee
- IT FTE (full-time equivalent) ratios and similar macro indicators

- Based on full fiscal year submissions
- Normalized to standard industry sectors and size bands
- Typically refreshed on an annual or periodic cycle, not monthly

IT OpEx benchmarks from the Apptio community

Source type:

- Anonymized and aggregated customer data, on an opt-in basis.

- Distribution of IT OpEx across Cost Pools
- Distribution of IT OpEx across Resource Towers
- Archetype profiles that identify common spend patterns

- Input data aligned to TBM Cost Pools and Resource Towers
- Individual contributors are anonymized
- Metrics are only exposed above minimum sample thresholds

Infrastructure cost and efficiency benchmarks

Source type:

- Infrastructure benchmarking programs and large-scale infra studies.

- Unit cost metrics such as cost per server or cost per TB
- Efficiency metrics such as FTE per 100 servers

- Based on detailed cost and volume submissions by Resource Tower and Resource Sub-Tower
- Uses standard infra tower definitions that align with TBM
- Often includes multiple years for trend and stability checking

- Organizational profile Annual Revenue Annual Operating Expense Average Annual Headcount
- IT cost data Annual Spend broken down by:
- Operating Expense
- Depreciation Expense
- Capital Expense Annual Operating Expense by Cost Pool Annual Operating Expense by Resource Tower
- Volume and capacity data by Resource Sub-Tower Device counts, TBs, ports, FTE counts, and similar
