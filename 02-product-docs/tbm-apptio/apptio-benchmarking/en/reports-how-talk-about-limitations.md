# How to talk about limitations

You are going to get pushback. Handle it without throwing the tool under the bus.

- Peers may have different scope or operating models.
- Benchmark definitions may not match every team’s internal metrics 1 to 1.
- Some metrics have thinner coverage and should be treated as advisory.

- Be explicit about peer group definition .
- Explain what is included in the metric (numerator and denominator).
- Use benchmarks to decide where to dig; then rely on your own data for the detailed decisions.

- “ The benchmarks are wrong, ignore them. ”
- “ We are unique, nothing applies to us. ”
