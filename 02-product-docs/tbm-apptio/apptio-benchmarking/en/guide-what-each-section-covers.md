# What each section covers

- Overview & Key Concepts What IBM Apptio Benchmarking is, where it fits with Costing and TBM, the main benchmark types, and data sources.
- Implementation Checklist A checklist that summarizes the practical steps to implement Benchmarking.
- Quickstart Prerequisites, access, and the first-time flow to get from “we have Benchmarking” to “we can show a credible comparison.”
- Configuration Guide Detailed wiring: TBM taxonomy alignment, organizational profile, cost and volume data, peer group defaults, refresh cadence, and configuration governance.
- Using Benchmarking Reports How to read and apply Benchmarking views and benchmark-enabled Costing reports, with concrete usage scenarios.
- Data & Methodology Reference Where the benchmarks come from, how metrics are defined and calculated, how peer groups are built, and what the statistical outputs actually mean.
- Operations & Governance How to run Benchmarking as part of your TBM program: roles, RACI, cadences, workflows, and how it plugs into planning and tower governance.
- Troubleshooting & FAQ Symptom-based troubleshooting (things look wrong, missing, or slow) and short, reusable answers for common executive questions.
- Appendix A: Glossary & Acronyms Definitions for TBM terms, Benchmarking concepts, and program jargon used across the guide.
- Appendix B: Version & Compatibility Notes High-level alignment with TBM versions and Costing, plus guidance for legacy implementations and migrations.
