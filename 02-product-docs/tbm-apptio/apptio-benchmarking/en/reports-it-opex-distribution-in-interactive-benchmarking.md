# IT OpEx distribution reports in Interactive Benchmarking

These answer “How is our IT spend structured compared to peers?”

## IT OpEx Benchmarks by Cost Pool

![IT OpEx Benchmarks by Cost Pool](../../resources/images/it%20benchmarking_images/itopex-benchmark-cost-pool.png)

- See your IT OpEx mix at a glance.
- Compare each Cost Pool to the benchmark distribution, not just a single number.
- Separate “we’re different” from “we’re wrong”.
- Pinpoint the specific levers driving variance.

## IT OpEx by Resource Tower

![IT OpEx by Resource Tower](../../resources/images/it%20benchmarking_images/itopex-resource-tower.png)

- Understand where IT OpEx is actually going, in language Ops and Infra leaders use.
- Benchmark each tower against peers, not just total IT OpEx.
- Identify outliers fast using box plot distributions.
- Distinguish structural differences from inefficiency.
- Improve accountability by aligning metrics to ownership.

## How to interpret

- If your Labor share is 65 percent and the peer median is 45 percent, you are more people-intensive.
- If your Outside Services share is 50 percent and peers sit at 30 percent, you lean more on vendors.
- If your Data Center tower is significantly larger than peers while you claim “cloud first,” something does not match the story.

- Does our mix match our stated operating model?
- Are we more vendor-heavy or people-heavy than peers?
- Which towers are disproportionately large or small?
