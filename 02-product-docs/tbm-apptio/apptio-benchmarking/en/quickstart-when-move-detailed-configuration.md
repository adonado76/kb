# When to move on to detailed configuration

- Open Interactive Benchmarking reliably.
- See at least one Industry and one IT OpEx view with sensible values.
- Explain the peer group and time period you are using.
- Defend, at a basic level, what is included in the metrics you show.

- Use the Configuration Guide section to update/refine: TBM/ATUM version selection. Infrastructure cost and volume alignment. Peer group selections.
- Use the Using Benchmarking Reports section to prepare better stories and embed benchmark output into regular reporting and Resource Tower discussions.

Do not over-engineer this stage. Get to a usable baseline, then iterate with better mapping and governance rather than trying to get everything perfect on day one.
