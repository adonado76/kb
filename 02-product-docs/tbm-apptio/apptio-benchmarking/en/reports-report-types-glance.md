# Report types at a glance

You will generally see four main flavors of Benchmarking content.

1. Industry benchmark views Macro IT economics: IT spend as percentage of revenue, per employee, per FTE Good for board, strategy, and top-level questions
1. IT OpEx distribution views Shape of spend across Cost Pools and Resource Towers Good for understanding structural differences in cost
1. Infrastructure unit cost and efficiency views Cost per server, cost per TB, FTE per 100 devices, and similar Good for deep dives with Resource Tower Owners
1. Benchmark-enabled Costing reports Benchmark values brought directly into Costing Good for tracing benchmark gaps down to accounts, vendors, services, or applications
