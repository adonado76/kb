# Accessing Benchmarking

1. Sign into IBM Apptio Benchmarking directly by navigating to this web address: https://itbmx.apptio.com
1. Access IBM Apptio Benchmarking via Access Administration by: Navigating to Access Administration in your web browser https://frontdoor.apptio.com Click on the Interactive Benchmarking button

- A landing page with entry points such as Interactive Benchmarking .
- Tiles to benchmark-enabled Costing reports, if configured.
- A path to Settings or configuration for Benchmarking, visible to users with admin rights.

- Confirm with your IBM Apptio contact the product is provisioned to you.
- Confirm your user role includes access to Interactive Benchmarking. At a minimum, the role you are assigned in Frontdoor should include the ViewInteractiveBenchmarksAndCostData to access Interactive Benchmarking. To complete all setup actions, the role should include the ViewInteractiveBenchmarksAndCostData , ConfigureInteractiveBenchmarking , and RetrieveInteractiveBenchmarkingCostData permissions.
- Confirm you are in the correct environment (for main).
