# KPIs for Benchmarking itself

If you never measure Benchmarking usage or impact, it will silently slide into shelfware.

- Number of unique users accessing Benchmarking per quarter
- Number of benchmark-enabled Costing reports run during planning season
- Count of QBR or steering packs that include at least one benchmark slide

- Percent of major IT initiatives whose business cases reference benchmarks
- Number of tower optimization initiatives explicitly tied to benchmark gaps
- Reduction in unstructured “cut X percent” demands because you have structured comparisons

You do not need all of these, but you should track something to prove this product is doing more than sitting there.
