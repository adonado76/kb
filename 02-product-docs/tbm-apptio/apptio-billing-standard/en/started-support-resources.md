# Support resources

Apptio Billing provides a variety of support resources to help you learn, troubleshoot, and stay informed. These resources connect you with documentation, training, and the broader Apptio community so you can get the most out of the product.

- Release Notes: Stay up to date with the latest enhancements. View all What’s New posts to track product changes and new capabilities.
- Videos and Training: Access self-paced training resources, including video tutorials, webinar recordings, and guided walkthroughs to build your skills.
- Community Q&A: Browse questions and answers from other Apptio users, or post your own to get help from peers and experts.
- Feature Ideas: Help shape the product by submitting new feature requests or voting on ideas shared by the community.
- IBM Support: Use the Support portal to submit a support request.
