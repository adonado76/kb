# Who this guide is for

*This section explains who the guide is for, how edition notices work, what each major section covers, and where different personas should begin.*

## Primary Audience

These roles configure, operate, and troubleshoot Billing:

- Admin : Owns configuration, environments, promotions, and operational runbooks.
- Analyst : Prepares analysis, validates bills, and works closely with Finance and Service or Product Owners.
- Partner : External delivery partner working on behalf of the IT organization.
- Turnkey Admin Services (TAS) : IBM or Partner resource acting as the Admin for a Billing deployment.

## Secondary Audience

These roles typically consume reports and make decisions:

- Service or Product Owner : Owns one or more services or products that are billed or shown back.
- Senior Leader : VP, CIO, CTO, CFO, or similar leader who sponsors or relies on Billing outcomes.
- Stakeholder : Finance, Procurement, or other non-Technology functions that use Billing outputs.
- Consumer : Any group or business unit that receives products or services charged or shown back.
