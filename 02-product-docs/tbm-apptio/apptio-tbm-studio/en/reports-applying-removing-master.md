# Applying and Removing Master Reports

Apply a Master Report

To apply a master report to a custom report:

1. Check out the custom report in edit mode.
1. Navigate to the Report tab in the ribbon.
1. Open the Masters drop-down list.
1. Select the master report you want to apply.
1. Save and check in the report.

Remove a Master Report

To remove a master report from a custom report:

1. Check out the custom report.
1. Navigate to the Report tab.
1. Open the Masters drop-down list.
1. Select None at the bottom of the list.
1. Save and check in the report.

Deleting Master Reports

To delete a custom master report:

1. In Report Edit mode, click the Reports section in the Project Explorer .
1. Filter the list of reports by Master Reports to display only masters.
1. Check out the master report you want to delete.
1. Click Delete on the Home tab.
1. Check in to complete the deletion.
