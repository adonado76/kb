# Master Report Setting

Master reports serve as templates that can be applied to multiple reports, ensuring consistent layout and appearance across a set of reports.

Key characteristics:

- Master reports are always top-level reports
- Components from a master report appear as "wallpaper" - visible but not editable in derived reports
- Components added to derived reports sit on top of the master layout
- Standard master reports are provided by the system and cannot be deleted

To create a master report:

1. Check out the report
1. Navigate to Report tab > Advanced group
1. Click the Master Report option
1. Save and check in
