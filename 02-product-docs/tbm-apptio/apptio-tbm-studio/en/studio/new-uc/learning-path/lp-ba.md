# Learning Paths for Business Analysts

**Objective:** Analyze IT costs, understand cost allocations, and create insightful
reports to support business decisions

**Who this is for:** Business analysts, finance analysts, cost analysts, and anyone who
primarily consumes and customizes reports to understand IT spending patterns

## Foundation (30 minutes)

**What you'll learn**: Essential TBM concepts and how to navigate existing reports

**Learning objectives:**

- Understand what TBM is and why it matters to your organization
- Navigate the TBM Studio interface confidently
- Read and interpret existing cost reports
- Use filters and time periods to focus your analysis
- Understand basic TBM terminology (cost pools, allocations, drivers)

**Topics to complete:**

1. [What is TBM Studio?](../getting-started/what-istbm.html)(5 min)
2. [Navigate the Interface (10 min)](../getting-started/tbm-navigation.html)
3. [Read Your First Report (10
   min)](../howtoguides/create-reports/others-report.html)
4. [Work with Perspectives (5
   min)](../howtoguides/create-reports/create-cust-pers.html)

**Prerequisites:** None - this is your starting point

## Essential Skills (2 hours)

**What you**'ll learn: Customize reports, work with data, and create basic analyses

**Learning objectives:**

- Customize existing reports to answer specific business questions
- Create and apply custom perspectives for your analysis needs
- Use editable tables to enrich cost data with business context
- Export and share reports with stakeholders
- Understand how costs flow through allocation models

**Topics to complete:**

1. [Customize Existing Reports](../howtoguides/create-reports/report-cust.html)(30 min)
2. [Create Custom
   Perspectives](../howtoguides/create-reports/create-cust-pers.html) (30 min)
3. [Work with Editable
   Tables](../howtoguides/work-with-data/create-blank-et.html) (30 min)
4. **Understand Cost Flows** (20 min) *→ Section 4.3*
5. [Export and Share
   Reports](../howtoguides/create-reports/export-print-rep.html) (10 min)

**Prerequisites:** Foundation level completed

**Estimated practice time:** Add 1-2 hours working with your organization's actual
reports

## Advanced (4 hours)

What you'll learn: Build custom reports from scratch, create complex analyses, and become a
power user

**Learning objectives:**

- Build new reports from scratch using model data
- Create sophisticated calculated metrics and KPIs
- Understand complex allocation scenarios
- Design report collections for executive audiences
- Publish and maintain reports for your organization

## Topics to complete

1. [Build Reports from
   Scratch](../howtoguides/create-reports/report-basic.html) (60 min)
2. Create Calculated Metrics (45 min) *→ Section 5.4*
3. Master Allocation Flows (45 min) *→ Section 4.3*
4. [Build Report Collections](../howtoguides/create-reports/build-rc.html) (45 min)
5. [Publish Reports](../howtoguides/create-reports/add-et-rep.html) (30
   min)
6. [Advanced Analysis
   Techniques](../howtoguides/create-reports/dynamic-sizing.html) (15 min)

**Prerequisites:** Essential Skills completed, familiarity with your organization's cost
model

**Next Steps:**  Explore Administrator path if you need to manage users or
permissions
