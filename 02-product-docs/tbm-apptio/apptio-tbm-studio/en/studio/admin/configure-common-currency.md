# Configure the common currency

**Applies to**: TBM Studio 12.0 and later. You can change the common currency for your
individual profile, or for entire TBM Studio projects.

## Change common currency for your profile

It is possible to change common currency for only your user account without affecting the project
for other users.

1. Navigate to ![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/icons/icon-user-account_18x20.png).
2. In **Currency**, select your preferred currency.
3. Click **OK**.

   ![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studio-change-curencies.png)

   Note: Your profile
   currency preferences will take priority over the Project Settings on most pages within TBM Studio.

## Change base currency for the entire project

With the correct permissions, you can change the common currency used in the entire project,
which will affect all users who can access the project.

1. On the **Project** tab, in the **Project Setup** group, click **Project
   Settings**.

   ![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-tbm-studio/resources/images/studio_images/studio_admin_base_currency_sui.png)
2. In **Base Currency**, select your preferred currency.
3. In **Number Compaction**, set your preferred compaction for thousands, millions, billions,
   and trillions.
4. In **Currency Symbols**, set your preferred currency symbol.
5. Click **Save** to apply the changes.

   Note: Project Settings can only be changed if they are
   not currently checked out to any other users.
