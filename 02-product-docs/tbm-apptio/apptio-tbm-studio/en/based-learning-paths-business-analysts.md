# Learning Paths for Business Analysts

*Objective: Analyze IT costs, understand cost allocations, and create insightful reports to support business decisions*

Who this is for: Business analysts, finance analysts, cost analysts, and anyone who primarily consumes and customizes reports to understand IT spending patterns

## Foundation (30 minutes)

What you'll learn : Essential TBM concepts and how to navigate existing reports

Learning objectives:

- Understand what TBM is and why it matters to your organization
- Navigate the TBM Studio interface confidently
- Read and interpret existing cost reports
- Use filters and time periods to focus your analysis
- Understand basic TBM terminology (cost pools, allocations, drivers)

Topics to complete:

1. What is TBM Studio? (5 min)
1. Navigate the Interface (10 min)
1. Read Your First Report (10 min)
1. Work with Perspectives (5 min)

Prerequisites: None - this is your starting point

## Essential Skills (2 hours)

What you 'll learn: Customize reports, work with data, and create basic analyses

Learning objectives:

- Customize existing reports to answer specific business questions
- Create and apply custom perspectives for your analysis needs
- Use editable tables to enrich cost data with business context
- Export and share reports with stakeholders
- Understand how costs flow through allocation models

Topics to complete:

1. Customize Existing Reports (30 min)
1. Create Custom Perspectives (30 min)
1. Work with Editable Tables (30 min)
1. Understand Cost Flows (20 min) → Section 4.3
1. Export and Share Reports (10 min)

Prerequisites: Foundation level completed

Estimated practice time: Add 1-2 hours working with your organization's actual reports

## Advanced (4 hours)

What you'll learn: Build custom reports from scratch, create complex analyses, and become a power user

Learning objectives:

- Build new reports from scratch using model data
- Create sophisticated calculated metrics and KPIs
- Understand complex allocation scenarios
- Design report collections for executive audiences
- Publish and maintain reports for your organization

## Topics to complete

1. Build Reports from Scratch (60 min)
1. Create Calculated Metrics (45 min) → Section 5.4
1. Master Allocation Flows (45 min) → Section 4.3
1. Build Report Collections (45 min)
1. Publish Reports (30 min)
1. Advanced Analysis Techniques (15 min)

Prerequisites: Essential Skills completed, familiarity with your organization's cost model

Next Steps: Explore Administrator path if you need to manage users or permissions
