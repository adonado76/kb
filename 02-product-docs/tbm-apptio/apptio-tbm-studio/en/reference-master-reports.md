# Master Reports

## Overview

Master reports serve as reusable templates that provide consistent layout, branding, and design elements across multiple reports in TBM Studio. By creating a master report and applying it to your custom reports, you ensure visual consistency and reduce the maintenance burden of updating common elements across your reporting suite.
