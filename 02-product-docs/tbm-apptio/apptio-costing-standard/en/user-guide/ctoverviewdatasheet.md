# Costing Standard Overview Datasheet

Understand Costs & KPIs to Drive more Value with Your Technology
Business.

## Overview

Make better resource decisions faster by putting actionable, current, and trusted information
about IT costs at the fingertips of decision-makers each month. Begin with the Apptio Costing
Standard Foundation module to expose the true cost structure, drivers, and trends by projects,
internal and contract labor, vendors, internal functions like application support and service
management, and the total costs of your infrastructure. Then, see how these foundations drive the
total cost of products you deliver with the Apptio Costing Standard Applications and Services
module. Finally, drive value-oriented, fact-based conversations by seeing how applications and
services support the business with the Apptio Costing Standard Business Unit module.

## Key Benefits

- Replace assumption and emotion with facts that drive decision agility
- Establish greater trust with business partners
- Spend less time wrestling data, more on analysis
- Leverage best practices and align with peer benchmarks
- Fact-based costs you can understand, trust, and use
- Adoption in 90 days or less with your raw data
- Better manage vendor, project, and labor costs
