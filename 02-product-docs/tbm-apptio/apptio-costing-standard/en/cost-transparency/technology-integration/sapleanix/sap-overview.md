# SAP LeanIX Overview

## Overview

The integration between SAP LeanIX and IBM Apptio creates a unified end-to-end link between
an organization’s enterprise architecture (EA) data and its IT financial management (ITFM)
data. By combining SAP LeanIX’s rich architecture metadata (applications, business
capabilities, IT components, user groups, providers, projects, etc.) with IBM Apptio’s cost
modeling and financial intelligence, stakeholders gain a comprehensive, cost-aware view of
the IT landscape.

With this integration:

- SAP LeanIX becomes an upstream source of truth for EA data that can be automatically
  exported to IBM Apptio.
- IBM Apptio processes the imported EA data along with cost/usage data to produce cost
  analyses, application TCO (Total Cost of Ownership), budget forecasts and financial
  views mapped to the architecture.
- This linkage facilitates more accurate budgeting, costing, and data-driven decision
  making across EA, IT, and finance organizations.

The integration supports two modes: Base Integration (export EA data from SAP LeanIX into
IBM Apptio) and Advanced Integration, which includes exposing IBM Apptio’s Application TCO
metrics for consumption (to be pulled) by SAP LeanIX.

## Target Audience

This integration is designed for joint SAP LeanIX and IBM Apptio customers, particularly
those with an established cost model in IBM Apptio, especially as it relates to the
foundational financial layer being in place. This integration is intended for stakeholders
who need to align architecture data with financial data. Typical audiences include:

- **Enterprise Architects**- Enterprise Architects, who want to connect application
  landscapes, dependencies, business capabilities and IT components with cost transparency
  and financial metrics.
- **IT Finance / Budgeting Teams / CIOs / CFOs** - Finance stakeholders, who need
  visibility into total cost of ownership, cost per application or service, and accurate
  budgeting based on real architecture data.
- **IT Portfolio / Application Owners / PMO** - Portfolio and Solution owners, who
  manage application portfolios, rationalization, cloud migration, lifecycle planning;
  with cost data to guide decisions.
- **Business Unit Leaders / Product Owners** - BU Leaders who need clarity on cost vs
  value of services/products delivered to the business.

## Use Cases

**Application Rationalization & Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis**

- Export application, provider, IT-component, user-group, business-capability data from
  SAP LeanIX into IBM Apptio. Obtain full TCO per application (software, hardware,
  maintenance, labor, etc.). This helps to identify redundant or costly applications for
  retirement or consolidation.
- Combine architectural metadata with cost data to drive rationalization projects with
  financial backing (not just technical or functional fit).

**Application Portfolio Management & Budget Forecasting**

- Use architecture data + cost modeling to forecast application run cost, development
  cost, maintenance, and growth over time. Helps budget planning and long-term IT
  strategy.
- Use architecture-driven views (e.g. by business capability, user group, IT component)
  to allocate costs logically across business units or cost centers.

**Technology Risk Management & Lifecycle / End-of-Life Cost Analysis**

- Use integration to assess financial implications of maintaining legacy applications or
  retiring old systems. Cost-based risk and return analysis helps IT and business
  stakeholders make informed decisions.
- Use consolidated data to prioritize decommissioning or modernization based on cost,
  risk, business capability, etc.

**Improved Collaboration across EA, IT, and Finance**

- Provide a “single source of truth” so that architecture, finance, and business
  stakeholders speak the same “cost + architecture” language.
- Reduce manual data exports/imports (formerly Excel-based), minimize errors, and save
  time through automated data flows.

## Outcomes

- **Cost Transparency:**Organizations achieve unified visibility into IT costs at the
  level of applications, services, capabilities, and infrastructure; mapped to architecture
  data.
- **More Accurate Financial Planning & Budgeting:**With architecture-driven cost
  data, forecasting, budgeting, and allocation become more precise and justifiable.
- **Data-Driven Architectural Decisions:**Combining architecture metadata with cost
  leads to rationalization, consolidation, and cloud-migration decisions based on total cost
  and value.
- **Reduced Manual Effort & Errors:**Automated, scheduled data export from SAP
  LeanIX to IBM Apptio reduces or eliminates the manual Excel-based workflows that many
  organizations used previously.
- **Enhanced Cross-Team Collaboration:**EA, IT, finance, and business teams can all
  operate on the same dataset with aligned taxonomy and cost data; improving transparency
  and accountability.
- **Better Portfolio Governance:**Enables lifecycle-based decisions, cost-aware
  application retirement, cloud migration planning, and risk management based on data.
