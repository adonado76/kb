# Overview

Technology Integrations allow IBM Apptio Costing Standard to connect with the
operational systems that manage your IT estate; bringing richer context, higher data quality,
and automated insights directly into your cost model. These integrations extend the value of
Costing Standard by combining financial transparency with real-time operational, application,
and architectural intelligence from industry-leading platforms.

Unlike Platform Integrations, which focus on how Apptio products connect with one another
(such as IBM Apptio Costing and IBM Apptio Planning or IBM Apptio Costing and IBM
Cloudability, via Automated Data Management (ADM)), Technology Integrations connect Costing
Standard to external technology systems you already rely on. They give your end users more
insights gathered from the end-to-end IT ecosystem in your organization and your costing model
more lineage, more precision, and more automation, with less manual effort.

## Why Technology Integrations Matter

Technology Integrations play a critical role in ensuring your cost model reflects the true
structure, usage, and behaviour of your IT environment:

- **Better Accuracy Through Operational Context** - Integrations bring in
  authoritative metadata, such as resource utilization, configuration details, contract
  status, and application dependencies; reducing mapping errors and improving unit
  economics.
- **Faster Time-to-Value** - Instead of manually cleansing and enriching data, the
  system uses automated feeds from trusted tooling, accelerating onboarding and ongoing
  updates.
- **Stronger Insights & Optimization Opportunities** - By merging financials with
  operational signals (e.g., performance, placement inefficiencies, or redundant apps),
  customers can identify savings opportunities that cannot be seen in cost data alone.
- **Governance & Traceability** - Integrations ensure a single, connected view of
  applications, infrastructure, services, and business functions; supporting auditability
  and better decision-making.

Together, these integrations strengthen IBM Apptio’s role as the system of record for IT
financial intelligence, while leveraging the specialized capabilities of your technology
ecosystem.

IBM Apptio Costing Standard currently offers integrations with a select set of
industry-leading platforms. Each integration is designed to introduce either new use cases
or strengthen your cost model and costing insights with a specific type of operational and
architectural intelligence.

Current Technology Integrations supported:

- IBM Turbonomic
- ServiceNow
- SAP LeanIX
