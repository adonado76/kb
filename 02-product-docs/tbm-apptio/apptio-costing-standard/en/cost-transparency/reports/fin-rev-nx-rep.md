# Financial Review

Use this report to analyze year-to-date operational expenses against budget allocations
and identify areas of overspending or cost savings across categories.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- CFO
- CIO
- Executive Leadership
- Board Members
- Finance Leadership

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Key Performance Indicator Cards (1) | Three KPI cards show operating expense, operating expense year to date, and operating expense forecast for the fiscal year. |
| Filter Controls (2) | Five filters let you narrow the report by region, account group, cost center and name, cost center owner, and owner. |
| Dimension Selector (3) | Use this selector to view variance and spending analysis by different dimensions. |
| Top 5 Over Budget YTD Chart (4) | A horizontal bar chart shows the top five categories that are over budget year to date. |
| Top 5 Under Budget YTD Chart (4) | A horizontal bar chart shows the top five categories that are under budget year to date. |
| Top 5 OpEx Spend YTD Chart (5) | A vertical bar chart shows the top five operating expense categories by spend year to date. |
| OpEx Trend Chart (5) | A line chart shows the operating expense trend over time by category. |
| OpEx Variance Against Budget Chart (7) | A combined bar and line chart compares monthly operating expense budget with the operating expense forecast trend. |
| OpEx Variance Detail Analysis Table (7) | This table shows operating expense, operating expense budget, operating expense budget variance, and operating expense budget variance percentage for the selected time period. |

## Questions Answered

- Are we on track to meet our overall OpEx budget targets?
- What is the current budget variance, and is it within acceptable limits?
- Which spending categories are causing the largest budget overruns?
- What is our expected year-end financial position based on current trends?
- Which areas need immediate attention due to high overspending?
- Are there categories that are under budget, and can that budget be reused?
- How is spending changing over time, and are there any unusual patterns?
- Do we need to adjust budgets or take corrective actions to stay on track?

## Recommended Actions

- Focus on categories that are over budget and plan corrective actions
- Drill down into data to understand the reasons behind overspending
- Review under-budget areas to identify savings or reallocation opportunities
- Check spending trends to spot patterns and potential risks early
- Compare budget and forecast to see if costs are increasing
- Work with stakeholders to define actions and ownership
- Update forecasts based on current spending trends
