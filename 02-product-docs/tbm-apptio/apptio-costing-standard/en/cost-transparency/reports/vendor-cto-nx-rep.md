# Vendor Cost Take-Out

Use this report to analyze annual spending with external vendors by function and type,
identifying opportunities for vendor consolidation and renegotiation.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- Procurement Teams
- Vendor Managers
- CFO
- CIO
- Finance Leadership

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-vendor-cto.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Annual Spend Summary Metrics (1) | Six KPI cards show annual operating expense, annual capital expense, annual spend, annual strategic spend, annual preferred spend, and annual transactional spend. |
| View Tabs (2) | Tabs switch between Vendors by Function, Vendors by Resource Tower, Vendors by Cost Pool, and Vendors by Type views. |
| Column Selection Check boxes (3) | This panel lets you show or hide available columns in the table. |
| Filter Controls (4) | Two filters let you narrow the report by cost center name and vendor function. |
| Vendor Data Table (5) | This table includes columns such as vendor function, vendor name, vendor type, cost center name, vendor service, annual operating expense, annual capital expense, and annual spend. |
| Vendor Name Drill-down Links (6) | Vendor names link to more detailed information for the selected vendor. |
| Total Spend Summary Row (7) | The bottom row shows totals for annual operating expense, annual capital expense, and annual spend for the displayed vendors. |

## Key Insights

- How is our spending spread across strategic, preferred, and transactional vendors?
- Which vendors account for the highest share of total spend?
- Do we have too many vendors providing similar services?
- Where can we reduce vendors and simplify the portfolio?
- Which vendors should we focus on for renegotiation or better pricing?
- Are our vendor classifications aligned with actual spend?
- How is spending distributed across different business functions?
- Which areas or functions rely most on external vendors?

## Recommended Actions

- Review vendor categories and ensure high-spend vendors are aligned with the right
  classification.
- Identify areas where multiple vendors provide similar services and evaluate consolidation
  opportunities.
- Focus on high-spend vendors to plan renegotiation or stronger engagement.
- Analyze OpEx and CapEx patterns to better understand the type of vendor relationships.
- Drill down into top vendors to review detailed spending and prepare action plans.
- Look for low-spend vendors that can be reduced or merged to simplify management.
- Review the report regularly to track progress and identify new optimization opportunities.
