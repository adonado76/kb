# Service List

Use this report to view all IT services with their operating and capital expenses,
analyzing spending patterns by quantity and unit cost across different services and time
periods.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- Service Owners
- Financial Analysts
- IT Operations
- Business Analysts
- Cost Center Owners

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-service-list.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Time Period Selector (1) | Use this selector to view report data for the current month, current quarter, or year to date. |
| Filter Drop downs (2) | Five filters let you narrow the report by service type, service category, service name, service offering, and service owner. |
| Column Selector (3) | Use this control to show or hide available columns, including financial metrics, operational metrics, service details, and additional service information. |
| Service List Table (4) | The table shows services by using the selected columns. You can sort by column and open service details from the service name. |
| Summary Totals (5) | The footer row shows totals for operating expense, capital expense, total spend, quantity, and average unit cost for the displayed services. |

## Questions Answered

- What is the complete list of all services and their costs?
- Which services have the highest total spending?
- What is the unit cost for each service and which ones are most expensive per unit?
- How much OpEx versus CapEx does each service consume?
- What services does a specific owner manage?
- Which services are in a specific category or type?
- How many units of each service are being consumed?
- What is the total spending across all services for the selected time period?
- Which services have high costs but low quantities (indicating high unit costs)?

## Recommended Actions

- Sort by Total Spend (highest to lowest) to see your most expensive services and verify they
  align with business priorities.
- Sort by Unit Cost to find services with unusually high per-unit costs and investigate why
  they're more expensive than similar services.
- Filter by Service Owner to see all services a person manages, then review their total spending
  and look for consolidation opportunities.
- Compare OpEx and CapEx columns to identify services that are mostly operational (high OpEx, low
  CapEx) versus those with significant investments (high CapEx).
- Use the Column Selector to add Service Type and Service Category, then sort to group similar
  services together and compare their costs.
- Export the data to Excel for deeper analysis, such as calculating cost trends or creating custom
  charts for presentations.
- Click on high-cost service names to view their detailed pages and understand what's driving the
  spending.
