# Vendor Review

Use this report to analyze vendor spending with budget comparisons and trend analysis,
identifying vendors that are over or under budget to prioritize contract negotiations and cost
management actions.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- Procurement Teams
- Vendor Managers
- Finance Teams
- IT Leaders
- Contract Managers

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-vrev.png)

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-vrevanal.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Summary Cards (1) | Three summary cards show total spend year to date, vendor operating expense year to date, and budget year to date. |
| Filter Options (2) | Five filters let you narrow the report by vendor type, vendor name, vendor function, cost center owner, and cost center and name. |
| Pivot Selector (3) | Use this selector to view the data by different dimensions, such as vendor name, vendor type, or cost center. |
| Top 5 Total Spend YTD Chart (4) | A bar chart shows the five vendors with the highest total spend year to date. |
| Top 5 Total Spend Trend Chart (4) | A line chart shows the spending trend over time for the top vendors. |
| Vendor Analysis Table (5) | This table includes columns such as vendor name, vendor type, vendor service, operating expense year to date, capital expense year to date, total spend year to date, annual budget, remaining budget, and remaining budget percentage. |
| Total Summary Row (6) | The bottom row shows totals for operating expense, capital expense, total spend, annual budget, and remaining budget for the displayed vendors. |

## Questions Answered

- Which vendors am I spending the most money with?
- Am I over budget or under budget with my vendor spending?
- How much of my vendor spending is operational (OpEx) versus capital (CapEx)?
- Is spending with specific vendors going up or down over time?
- Which vendors are classified as Strategic, Preferred, or Transactional?
- How much budget do I have remaining for vendor spending?
- Which vendors are over their annual budget (shown in red)?
- What services does each vendor provide?
- How many active vendors do I have?

## Recommended Actions

- Review the Top 5 Total Spend YTD chart to identify your biggest vendor relationships and verify
  they're providing good value for the money.
- Check the Vendor Analysis Table for any red numbers in the Remaining Budget column - these
  vendors are over budget and need immediate attention.
- Look at the Top 5 Total Spend Trend chart to spot vendors with sharp spending increases and
  investigate why costs are rising.
- Filter by Vendor Type to see how much you're spending with Strategic versus Preferred versus
  Transactional vendors and ensure alignment with your vendor strategy.
- Sort the Vendor Analysis Table by Total Spend YTD to see your complete vendor list ranked by
  cost and identify consolidation opportunities.
- Compare OpEx YTD and CapEx YTD columns to understand which vendors are primarily operational
  versus those requiring capital investments.
- Click on high-spend vendor names to view detailed spending breakdowns and contract information
  for upcoming renewal negotiations.
