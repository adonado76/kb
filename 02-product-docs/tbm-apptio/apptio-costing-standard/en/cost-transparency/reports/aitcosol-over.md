# AI TCO Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become a strategic priority for many organizations,
with CIOs increasingly accountable for driving AI adoption and value across the enterprise. As
AI investments grow, so does complexity—spend is often fragmented across cloud platforms, tools,
models, labor, and vendors, making it difficult to understand true total cost, usage, and
business value.

IBM Apptio’s AI TCO & Usage capability provides a structured approach to understanding,
managing, and optimizing AI investments. It delivers end-to-end visibility into AI costs,
consumption, and adoption, enabling organizations to scale AI responsibly while maintaining
financial control and transparency.

## Persona

• **C-Suite Executives**

Oversee enterprise AI strategy and require clear visibility into AI spend, value, and return
on investment to guide funding and scaling decisions.

• **Business Leaders**

Evaluate whether AI investments are delivering expected business outcomes and align AI usage
with strategic priorities.

• **Solution Owners (Application & Service Owners)**

Understand AI cost drivers, usage patterns, and efficiency across AI models and AI-enabled
solutions.

• **AI & Data Science Teams**

Monitor AI usage, consumption, and cost efficiency to prevent sprawl and support sustainable
AI adoption.

## Use Cases

Supported Out-of-the-box

• **AI Cost Transparency & Allocation**

Establish end-to-end visibility into AI spending across cloud, labor, infrastructure, and
vendor layers, enabling defensible allocation and accountability.

• **Track and Evaluate AI Usage**

Monitor AI input and output token consumption, usage trends, and adoption patterns to
identify inefficiencies, unused models, and consolidation opportunities.

• **Enable Responsible AI Adoption**

Align AI spend and usage with business value by identifying mismatches between cost and
consumption, encouraging accountable and sustainable adoption.

• **Establish AI Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**

Understand the full lifecycle cost of AI investments, track key cost drivers, and detect
cost anomalies early to support informed scaling decisions.

## Outcomes

• Clear, defensible visibility into AI total cost of ownership across the enterprise.

• Improved governance and accountability for AI spend and usage.

• Early detection of AI sprawl, inefficiencies, and underutilized models.

• Data-driven decisions for scaling, consolidating, or retiring AI investments.

• Alignment of AI costs with business value and strategic priorities.

• Foundation for responsible AI showback and future ROI measurement.
