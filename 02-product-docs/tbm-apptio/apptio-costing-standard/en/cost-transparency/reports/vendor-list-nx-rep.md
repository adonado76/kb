# Vendor List

Use this report to view all vendors with their operating and capital expenses,
identifying your largest suppliers and comparing spending across different time periods.

This report is designed for use by the following personas:

- Procurement Teams
- Finance Teams
- Contract Managers
- IT Leaders
- Audit Teams

## Key Elements

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nx-vendor-list.png)

| Element | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Filter Controls (1) | Four filters let you narrow the report by vendor name, vendor type, vendor manager, and vendor function. |
| Time Period Selectors (2) | Use these controls to view vendor details for the current month, current quarter, year to date, or months year to date. |
| Vendor Details Table (3) | This table includes columns such as vendor name, operating expense, capital expense, and total spend. |
| Vendor Name Links (4) | Vendor names link to more detailed information for the selected vendor. |

## Key Insights

- Who are our top vendors based on total spend?
- How much are we spending with each vendor in the selected period?
- How is spending split between operational (OpEx) and capital (CapEx) costs?
- What is our overall vendor spend year to date?
- Which vendors account for the largest share of our IT budget?
- Which vendors should we prioritize for contract review or negotiation?
- How is spending distributed across different vendor types or functions?
- How has vendor spending changed over time, and are there any noticeable trends?

## Recommended Actions

- Identify top vendors by reviewing total spend and prioritize them for closer management.
- Analyze the split between OpEx and CapEx to understand the nature of each vendor
  relationship.
- Drill down into vendor details to review spending patterns and investigate any concerns.
- Use filters to analyze specific vendor types or functions and understand spending patterns.
- Compare spending across different time periods to identify trends or unusual changes.
- Review vendor usage to find opportunities for consolidation and cost reduction.
- Schedule regular reviews to monitor vendor spend and ensure control over costs.
