# About the IT Finance reports (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

The IT Finance reports provide data on:

- The budget for the current year.
- The spend to date.
- Budget variances by cost pool.
- The top spend and variance by cost pools.

To access the IT Finance reports, click **IT Finance** on the Home page. Use the tabs on the
IT Finance report page to access specific data on:

- Account variance
- Cost pools
- Vendors
- Cloud Provider Bill
- Labor
- Fixed Assets
- Outliers
