# IT Management - Business Units - By Department report (v103)

Use this report to see the details for the departments in a business unit.

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmbubydepartment103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Business Units > By Department

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- Business unit owners
- CIOs
- CFOs

## Objectives

Use this report to see the details for the departments in a business unit.

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:
