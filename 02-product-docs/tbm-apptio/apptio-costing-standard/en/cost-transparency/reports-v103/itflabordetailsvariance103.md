# IT Finance - Labor Details - Variance report (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

Use this report to see the top labor spend by role and internal/external breakout.

![image](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itflabordetailsvariance103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Finance > Labor > Cost Center > Budget Variance

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- IT Finance personnel
- Cost Center Owner

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- View the top labor spend by role and internal/external breakout. (Top YTD Costs by Role
  chart)
- Identify the variance between spend and budget by role for the current month, quarter and YTD.
  (Select Date Grouping option)

## Questions answered

You can use the information presented on this report to answer the following questions:

- How does my labor spend break-out by role and internal/external?
- Where is my biggest spend?
- Where is my biggest variance?
- Is my mix between internal/external labor appropriate for our business needs?
- Is action required to mitigate budget or other risks (e.g. key roles completely external)?

## Next actions

- View the 13-month spend and budget to identify abnormal trends by clicking View in the Trend column.
- Investigate staffing and rates by clicking the Staffing & Rate tab.
