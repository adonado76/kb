# Compact Slicers and Filtering on the slicer

To filter on Compact Slicers, you can add a filter to hide a specific cost pool from being
filtered on within a report.

1. Click on the **Slicer**.
2. Click on the **No Filter Applied** (twice) and the **Ad Hoc Query** window will
   update as shown in the following images.
