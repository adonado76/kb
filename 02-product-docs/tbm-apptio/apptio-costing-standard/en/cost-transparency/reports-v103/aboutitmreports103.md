# About the IT Management reports (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

The IT Management reports focus on the budget, forecast, and spend numbers by IT tower. The
reports provide data on:

- The budget for the current year.
- The spend to date.
- Budget variances by ITTower.
- The top spend and variance by ITTower.

To access the IT Management reports, click IT Management on the Home page. Use the tabs on the IT
Management report page to access specific data on:

- Financials
- Projects
- IT towers
- Applications
- Business units
- Public Cloud
- Services
