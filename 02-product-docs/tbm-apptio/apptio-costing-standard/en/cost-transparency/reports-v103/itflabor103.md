# IT Finance - Labor report (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

Use this report to view labor costs by internal and external personnel by cost center.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itflabor103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Finance > Labor

## Roles

This report is designed for IT Finance personnel.

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- View labor costs by internal and external personnel by cost center.
- Understand the labor spend to budget by cost center for the current month, quarter, and YTD.

## Questions answered

You can use the information presented on this report to answer the following questions:

- How much have I spent on labor this year?
- How am I doing against overall headcount and budget plans?
- Which department or cost center accounts for my largest labor spend?
- Where is my biggest variance, by amount?
- Is action required to mitigate budget risk?

## Next actions

To see spend, headcount, and rates by role, click on the specific cost center to open the IT
Finance Labor Detail report.
