# IT Management - Business Units - By Business Unit Report (v103)

Use this report to review the spend by business unit.

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmbubybu103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Business Units > By Business Unit

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- Business unit owners
- CIOs
- CFOs

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- Identify the Business Relationship Manager for a business unit.
- See the average spend per staff member per business unit.
- See total spend and percent fixed and percent variable.

## Questions answered

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:

- Are we spending too little or too much per staff member for a particular business unit?
- Do we have the right mix of fixed and variable spending across business units?
- Is action required to mitigate risk?

## Next actions

report for that business unit.
