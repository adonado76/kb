# IT Management - Summary report (v103)

◆ Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12 or TBM Studio
v11.

## Introduction

The IT Management - Summary report compares budget to spend.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmsummary103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Summary

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- CIO
- IT Management

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- See spend and budget KPIs (KPIbar).
- Understand cumulative spend YTD and projected annual spend (Cost With Projection vs Full Year
  Budget chart).
- Identify budget variance by Cost Pool (Budget Variance by Cost Pools chart).
- Identify top spend and variance by IT Tower (Cost Summary chart and table).

## Questions answered

You can use the information presented on this report to answer the following questions:

- How is my spend tracking against budget so far this year?
- Am I projected to be over or under budget for the year?
- Which cost pools are over or under budget?
- How much have I spent by IT Tower so far this year?
- Where is my biggest spend? • Where is my biggest variance -- by amount and by percent?
- What amount of variance or of percent variance is material compared to my overall IT spend?
- Is action required to mitigate budget risk?

## Next actions

- If variance is immaterial, no concern or action is needed.
- To understand more, click on a specific IT Tower in the Cost Summary table to open up the IT
  Management > IT Tower report.
