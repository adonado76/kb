# IT Management - Financials - By Account report (v103)

The IT Management - Financials - By Account report shows monthly spend by the company
account structure for QTD, YTD, and FY.

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itmfinancialsbyaccount103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Management > Financials > By Account

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- CIO
- IT Management

## Objectives

Use this report to:

- View of IT spend by the company's account structure for QTD, YTD, and FY.
- Filtered by IT Owner (CIO-1) or specific Cost Center Owner to review spend by account sub-group.

## Questions answered

The information presented on this report can be used to answer the following questions:

- Where do we have the largest category spend for a particular IT Owner or Cost Center Owner?
- Are we spending more in this cost category than planned?
- Are there opportunities to better control or reduce spend in an area?

## Next actions

Click an entry in the Account SubGroup column to analyze spend in the category by Cost Center and
to view the detailed transactions.
