# IT Finance - Labor Details - Fixed/Variable Analysis report (v103)

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

Use this report to identify the relative fixed/variable and OpEx/CapEx spend by role.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/itflaborfixedvariableanalysis103_1.png)

## Navigation

IT Finance > Labor > Cost Center > Fixed/Variable Analysis

## Roles

This report is designed for:

- IT Finance personnel
- Cost Center Owner

## Objectives

Use this report to identify the relative fixed/variable and OpEx/CapEx spend by role.

## Questions answered

You can use the information presented on this report to answer the following questions:

- If demand or consumption drops, do I have flexibility to adjust staffing levels?
- What percent of my labor cost is capitalized?
- How does the capitalization of my labor vary by role?
- Are there sourcing changes that I could take to create a more flexible staffing or variable cost structure?
- What is an appropriate internal vs. external labor ratio for our company and my area of responsibility?

## Next actions

- View the 13-month fixed/variable spend to identify trends over time by clicking View in the Trend column.
- Look at the IT Finance Spend report to see which accounts are fixed or variable.
