# About the Data Quality reports

Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12
or TBM Studio v11.

## Introduction

The Data Quality reports focus on the quality and completeness of the data.

Use the Data Quality reports to:

- Identify areas for data improvement
- Review financial allocations and strategies
- Review IT resource allocations and strategies

The reports present information about the following data dimensions:

- Cost source, including labor, vendors, projects, data centers, servers, storage, tickets, applications, and business units.
- Completeness, validity, and uniqueness of the data.
- Allocations of costs.

There are data quality reports for:

- Financials
- It resources
- Services
- Servers
- Storage
- Service Desk
- Data Centers

The Financials report is described [Data Quality - Financials report](dataqualityfinancials.html). The other reports are not described but they are
similar.

## Allocation information

A major focus of the Data Quality reports is the allocation of cost from the cost source to the
different entities in the organization. The allocations are defined by the allocation models. The
main cost allocation model is shown in Figure A below. For more information on defining allocations,
see Allocating Value in Models in the Studio Guide.

![image](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/aboutthedataqualityreports_1.png)

## Access the reports

To access the Data Quality reports, click Data & Allocations on the Home page. From the
Summary report, you can drill-down into detailed reports about the individual dimensions.
