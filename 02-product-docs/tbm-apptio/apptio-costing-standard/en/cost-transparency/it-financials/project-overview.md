# Projects Overview

## Overview

Projects provides visibility into IT project investments and how project spend aligns to
solutions, services, and business priorities. It helps organizations track project costs,
compare spend against approved budgets, and understand how project investments contribute to
run, growth, and transformation initiatives.

By consolidating project financial data and linking it to solution offerings and business
value classifications, Projects supports informed decision-making across planning, execution,
and post-deployment review.

## Personas

- **IT Finance** - Reviews project spend, budget adherence, and investment alignment to
  financial and strategic goals.
- **Project Management Office** (**PMO)** - Monitors project budgets, spend
  variance, and portfolio balance across initiatives.
- **Service or Solution Owner** - Reviews project investments impacting applications,
  services, products, or platforms to ensure alignment with delivery and value
  expectations.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Project Spend Alignment** - Map project investments to solution offerings
  (applications, services, products, and platforms) and business value classifications to
  ensure alignment with strategic priorities.
- **Project Spend Variance Analysis** - Compare actual project spend to approved
  budgets to identify overruns early and support timely funding or scope adjustments.
- **Run–Change or Run–Grow–Transform Classification for Projects** - Classify project
  spend to understand the balance between operational, growth, and transformational
  initiatives and assess alignment with strategic objectives.
- **Project Cost Allocation** - Allocate project costs, including labor and vendor
  expenses, to the appropriate solution offerings or business units to improve cost
  accountability and transparency.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Investment ROI for Projects** - Evaluate project investments against projected
  benefits to prioritize high-value initiatives and validate outcomes after deployment.

## Outcomes

- Improved alignment of project investments with business strategy and solution
  priorities.
- Early identification of project spend variances and budget overruns.
- Clear visibility into how project spend is distributed across run, growth, and
  transformation initiatives.
- Increased accountability through accurate allocation of project costs to solutions and
  business units.
- Better prioritization of projects based on expected and realized return on investment.
