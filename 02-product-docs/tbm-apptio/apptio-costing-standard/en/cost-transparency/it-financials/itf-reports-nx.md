# IT Financial NX Reports

The **IT Financials Report** collection provides visibility into IT spend, budget and
forecast performance, and cost drivers across the organization. They support executive reviews,
periodic financial analysis, and variance investigation by combining high-level summaries with
detailed transaction-level insights.

IT Financials Report collection includes the:

- Financial Review
- Financial Review - CapEx
- Financial Analysis

## Financial Review

This report provides an executive view of the overall OpEx budget variance and OpEx spend
of your organization. The report breaks down IT cost per cost pool and owner so you can
determine which IT owners are responsible for the largest IT spend.

Various charts in the report also help you determine whether variances are real or caused
by mis-categorization.

Use this report to create an executive brief that explains IT spend and to perform the
periodic financial reviews that are essential for effectively managing IT spend. Data in
this report includes general ledger line items so you can adjust plans to accommodate for
variance.

This report is designed for use by the following roles:

- CIO -1 (TBM Office)
- Cost Center owners
- IT Financial analysts

**Insights Provided**:

- Identify where actual spend differs most from budget or forecast and determine which
  cost pools, account groups, or cost centers are driving those variances.
- Understand which IT owners or cost center owners are accountable for the highest spend
  and variance.
- Determine whether variances are valid or caused by mis-categorization or incorrect
  transaction classification.
- Review period-over-period spend trends and analyze the transaction-level details
  contributing to cost changes.
- Evaluate year-to-date performance and identify where forecast adjustments may be
  required to meet financial targets.

## Financial Review – CapEx

This report provides an executive view of the overall CapEx budget variance and CapEx spend
of your organization. The report breaks down IT cost per cost pool and owner so you can
determine which IT owners are responsible for the largest IT spend.

Various charts in the report also help you determine whether variances are real or caused
by incorrect categorization.

Use this report to create an executive brief that explains overall IT CapEx spend and to
perform the periodic financial reviews that are essential for effectively managing IT spend.
This report also provides the ability to see the general ledger line items associated with
variances so you can adjust plans to accommodate for variance.

This report is designed for use by the following roles:

- CIO -1 (TBM Office)
- Cost center owners
- IT financial analysts

**Insights Provided** 

- Identify where the largest CapEx spend occurs across cost pools, IT owners, and cost
  centers, and understand who is accountable for that spend.
- Detect significant variances between actual CapEx and plan (budget or forecast) and
  determine which categories or cost centers are driving those exceptions.
- Verify whether variances are valid or caused by incorrect or inconsistent transaction
  categorization by drilling into GL-level details.
- Analyze period-over-period CapEx spending trends and review the expense line items
  contributing to changes in cost.
- Evaluate year-to-date CapEx performance and identify where forecast adjustments may be
  required to meet financial targets.

![](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/nrs-fin-capexrev.png)

For more details on how to use the Financial Review - CapEx report go [Financial Review - CapEx](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/apptio-commercial/costing-standard/saas?topic=reports-financial-review-capex "(Opens in a new tab or window)")

## Financial Analysis

This report shows a detailed view of month-over-month OpEx and CapEx variance, year-to-date
budget variance, and year-to-date forecast variance. You can use this report to review and
manage IT spend and variance for account groups, account subgroups, accounts, and cost
centers.

This report is designed for use by following roles:

- Cost Center owners
- IT financial analysts

**Insights provided:** 

- Identify where the largest IT spend is concentrated across cost pools, accounts, and
  cost centers.
- Detect areas with the most significant period-over-period changes in OpEx or CapEx.
- Determine whether actual costs are exceeding the approved budget or forecast.
- Understand which specific spend items are driving variances between the latest
  forecast and the budget.
- Validate whether variances are legitimate or the result of incorrect or inconsistent
  transaction categorization, and identify how cost centers may need to adjust forecasts
  to meet financial targets.
