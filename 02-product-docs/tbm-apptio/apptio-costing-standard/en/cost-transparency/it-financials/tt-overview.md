# Time Tracking Overview

## Overview

Time Tracking enables allocation of labor costs based on reported time across projects and
operational activities. It supports accurate project labor costing and effort analysis by
using time-based data rather than assumptions or static allocations. This capability is used
when organizations require detailed visibility into how labor effort contributes to project
and operational outcomes.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**
- Reviews labor cost allocation and supports project-level financial analysis and
  reviews.
- **PMO / Project Managers**
- Analyze project labor effort, cost distribution, and alignment to plans.

## Use Cases

**Custom use cases**

- **Capacity & Demand Forecast**
- Use historical time tracking data to assess labor effort trends and support
  forecasting of future personnel needs based on project pipelines and business-as-usual
  workloads.
- **Skills & Resource Allocation**
- Analyze time spent by role or activity to understand how workforce skills are applied
  across projects and identify opportunities to better align skills to demand.

## Outcomes

- More accurate allocation of labor costs to projects and operational activities.
- Improved visibility into project labor effort and cost drivers.
- Better support for capacity planning and demand forecasting using actual effort
  data.
- Increased accountability for labor costs across projects and solutions.
