# Fixed Assets Overview

## Overview

Fixed Assets provides visibility into IT asset investments, including capital cost,
depreciation, utilization, and lifecycle status. It helps organizations track assets across
their useful life, understand how asset costs contribute to IT spend, and ensure assets are
aligned with business needs.

IT assets typically represent a significant portion of IT investment. Without consistent
tracking of capitalization, depreciation, and utilization, organizations risk poor return on
investment, higher operating and maintenance costs, and missed optimization opportunities.
Fixed Assets supports consistent reporting and informed decision-making for asset
acquisition, optimization, and retirement.

## Personas

- **IT Finance** - Reviews IT asset capital spend, depreciation, budget and forecast
  alignment, and overall financial impact of fixed assets.
- **Service Owners** - Monitor assets supporting services or platforms to ensure assets
  are appropriately utilized, aligned to service demand, and managed across their
  lifecycle.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Depreciation Management** - Track capital cost, monthly depreciation, and remaining
  depreciation of IT assets to support accurate financial reporting and cost allocation.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Asset Lifecycle Management**- Track IT assets across acquisition, usage, aging, and
  end-of-life to ensure assets remain aligned with service demand and business priorities.

## Outcomes

- Clear visibility into IT asset lifecycle status and end-of-life risk.
- Accurate and consistent tracking of asset capitalization and depreciation.
- Improved financial transparency for asset-related costs.
- Better alignment of IT asset investments with service demand and business needs.
