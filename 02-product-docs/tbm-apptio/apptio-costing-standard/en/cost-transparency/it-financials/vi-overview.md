# Vendor Insights Overview

## Overview

Vendor Insights provides detailed analytics across the vendor management lifecycle,
extending beyond foundational vendor spend visibility into invoices, purchase orders,
contracts, and service commitments. It brings together accounts payable, procurement, and
contract data to help organizations govern vendor spend, reduce financial risk, and optimize
vendor performance.

Vendor Insights enables deeper oversight into how vendor costs are incurred, validated, and
managed over time, supporting stronger commercial controls, improved forecasting, and
proactive vendor decision-making across IT and the business

## Personas

- **IT Finance**
- Reviews invoice accuracy, committed spend, and budget adherence while strengthening
  financial controls.
- **Vendor Manager / Procurement**
- Manages vendor relationships, contract commitments, renewals, and compliance.
- **Cost Center Owner**
- Monitors vendor-related spend and commitments within their area of responsibility.
- **Service or Solution Owner**
- Understands how vendor contracts, invoices, and services support applications, services,
  and platforms.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Contract Renewals & Expirations**
- Monitor contract timelines to reduce auto-renewal risk, maintain negotiation leverage,
  and avoid unplanned cost increases.
- **Contract Terms Analysis**
- Provide centralized visibility into contract terms, pricing, commitments, and key
  conditions for faster decision-making.
- **Purchase Orders: Budget Controls & Approvals**
- Identify spend occurring without purchase orders to improve governance and enforce
  budget discipline.
- **Purchase Orders: Tracking & Visibility**
- Track purchase orders against vendors and budgets to prevent duplication and unplanned
  spend.
- **Accounts Payable: Invoice Validation**
- Detect billing discrepancies early, validate ARC/RRC charges, and reduce overpayments
  while maintaining vendor trust.
- **Contract Service Analysis**
- Align contracted services with applications, services, products, and platforms to
  reduce overlap and complexity.
- **Track Committed Vendor Spend**
- Monitor actual spend against contractual commitments to avoid penalties and improve
  forecasting accuracy.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Accounts Payable: Invoice Analysis**
- Perform detailed invoice analysis to improve spend allocation accuracy and reduce
  manual reconciliation effort.
- **Purchase Orders: Vendor & Contract Integration**
- Link purchase orders to contracts to strengthen cost alignment and reduce overspend
  risk.
- **Vendor SLA & Performance Tracking**
- Measure vendor performance against SLAs to evaluate service quality and
  cost-effectiveness.

## Outcomes

- Improved accuracy and control over vendor invoices, commitments, and payments.
- Reduced financial risk through proactive contract renewal and expiration
  management.
- Stronger governance of spend through visibility into PO compliance and non-PO
  spend.
- Better alignment between vendor services and supported applications or services.
- Enhanced negotiation leverage through transparency into committed versus actual
  spend.
- Increased confidence in vendor data quality and commercial decision-making.
