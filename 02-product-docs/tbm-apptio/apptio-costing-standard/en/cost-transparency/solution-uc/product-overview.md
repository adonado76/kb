# Products Overview

## Overview

As organizations shift from service-based delivery to product-centric operating models,
understanding Product Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) becomes critical. Traditional
out-of-the-box costing and reporting models have primarily focused on services, creating a
visibility gap for organizations that need clear, defensible insight into product cost
structures and financial performance. Product TCO addresses this gap by enabling consistent,
product-level cost transparency that supports modern product and portfolio management.

## Personas

- IT Finance
- Reviews product-level costs, ensures GL-reconcilable views, and enables accurate cost
  allocation across products and services.
- Product Managers and Portfolio Leaders
- Understand product cost drivers and financial performance to support roadmap, funding,
  and lifecycle decisions.
- Executives and IT Leadership
- Assess portfolio-level cost rollups to compare products, prioritize investments, and
  direct funding to the highest-value initiatives.
- PMO and PPM Leaders
- Link project spend to products to understand delivery cost, investment impact, and
  alignment with product strategy.

## Use Cases

- **Product Cost and Driver Visibility**
- Continuously track product costs and underlying drivers across labor, vendors, tools,
  and initiatives to identify inefficiencies, improve transparency, and support accurate
  budgeting and forecasting.
- **Product Lifecycle and Investment Management**
- Manage resourcing and spend across the full product lifecycle, from ideation and
  development to maintenance and retirement, to rebalance investments toward growth and
  innovation.
- **Customizable Product and Portfolio Hierarchies**
- Create flexible, role-specific product hierarchies that surface relevant insights for
  different stakeholders while maintaining consistency and governance across the
  organization.

## Outcomes

- Achieve defensible, product-level transparency into total cost and cost composition
- Gain end-to-end visibility across products, consumers, applications, and projects
- Support strategic decisions across new, active, and retired products
- Maintain alignment across product-only and hybrid service delivery models
- Enable confident product portfolio funding, prioritization, and roadmap planning
