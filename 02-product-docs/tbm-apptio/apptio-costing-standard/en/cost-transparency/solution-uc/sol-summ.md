# Summary

IBM Apptio Costing Standard provides a comprehensive set of solution use cases that
help organizations understand, allocate, and optimize technology costs across applications,
products, services, and business units. These use cases establish financial transparency, enable
defensible cost allocation, and support data-driven decisions across IT Finance, Technology
Leaders, and Business stakeholders.

**End User Devices**

End User Device use cases address the total cost of workforce devices such as laptops,
desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. They support cost allocation to services, lifecycle and
refresh planning, inventory optimization, compliance tracking, and identification of excess or
underutilized devices.

**Applications**

Application use cases enable full transparency into application total cost of ownership
across labor, infrastructure, software, and services. They support portfolio rationalization,
migration analysis, unit-cost and usage tracking, budget alignment, and lifecycle decisions
such as invest, migrate, tolerate, or retire.

**Services**

Services use cases provide a service-centric view of IT spend by consolidating underlying
technology, labor, and vendor costs into fully burdened service TCO. They support service
portfolio optimization, unit-cost analysis, showback and chargeback, and improved
communication of IT value to the business.

**Products**

Product TCO use cases enable product-centric financial transparency across the portfolio,
supporting modern product operating models. They help organizations understand product cost
drivers, manage lifecycle investments, compare OpEx and CapEx across products, and align
funding decisions with business outcomes.

**Business Units**

Business Unit use cases analyze IT spend by organizational units to understand consumption,
cost drivers, and investment patterns. They support comparisons across business units,
analysis of direct versus indirect spend, and alignment of IT investment with workforce size
and business priorities.
