# Applications Overview

## Overview

Reactive IT strategies focused solely on cost-cutting have proven ineffective as
organizations shift toward value optimization and continuous innovation. Many enterprises
struggle with limited application portfolio discipline, rising technical debt, redundant
applications, and poor cost transparency, often compounded by reliance on spreadsheets and
fragmented data. These challenges make it difficult to understand true application costs,
prioritize investments, and rationalize legacy systems, ultimately slowing innovation and
increasing waste.

Apptio Costing Standard Applications enables organizations to catalog all applications
across the enterprise and establish full transparency into application total cost of
ownership (TCO). By combining labor, infrastructure, software, vendor, and facilities costs,
it provides a clear, defensible view of application economics. This allows IT and business
leaders to identify cost drivers, reduce risk from legacy platforms, rationalize the
application landscape, and make informed investment, modernization, and decommissioning
decisions.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**
- Analyzes application TCO, unit costs, budgets, and variances to support investment
  decisions and financial governance.
- **Solution Owner / Application Owner**
- Understands application cost, usage, and efficiency to optimize delivery, prioritize
  improvements, and manage lifecycle decisions.
- **Business Relationship Manager**
- Uses application cost transparency to support accountable consumption and informed
  discussions with business stakeholders.
- **Enterprise Architecture**
- Evaluates application portfolios to guide standardization, consolidation, and long-term
  technology strategy.

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- **Application TCO, Unit Costs, and Usage**
- Analyze total application costs across labor, software, infrastructure, and platforms
  to guide strategic investment decisions.
- **Cost Drivers and Savings Identification**
- Identify major cost drivers across labor, vendors, infrastructure, and platforms to
  uncover actionable savings opportunities.
- **TCO Showback**
- Provide transparent visibility into each application’s total cost to drive
  accountability and responsible consumption.
- **Usage and Utilization**
- Monitor usage-based metrics such as cost per user to identify inefficiencies and
  optimization opportunities.

**Custom use cases:**

- **Budget and Forecast by Application**
- Align application budgets and forecasts with business priorities to optimize funding
  and investment outcomes.
- **Demand and Capacity Planning**
- Forecast future application demand to maintain predictable costs and ensure right-sized
  capacity.
- **Application Lifecycle Management**
- Assess application costs across lifecycle stages to guide invest, tolerate, migrate, or
  retire decisions and reduce redundancy.
- **Technology Stack Consolidation**
- Standardize applications on preferred platforms to reduce licensing, maintenance, and
  support costs while minimizing risk.

## Outcomes

- Clear, defensible visibility into application total cost of ownership across the
  enterprise.
- Improved investment decisions through understanding of application cost drivers, unit
  costs, and usage trends.
- Reduced technical debt by identifying high-cost, low-value, and redundant
  applications.
- More effective application rationalization and modernization planning based on financial
  and usage insights.
- Better budget alignment and forecasting by linking application spend to business
  priorities.
- Increased accountability and transparency through application-level showback and cost
  communication.
