# End User Insights Overview

## Overview

End User Devices provides consolidated visibility into the **cost, inventory, and
utilization** of end user computing assets across the enterprise. It brings together
device, financial, and lifecycle data to calculate the cost for PCs, laptops, smartphones,
tablets, and other client compute equipment, and allocates those costs to **End User
Services** **and** **business-specific services****.**

This is an **independent solution** that enablesorganizations to understand how end user
device spend is consumed, identify opportunities for **fleet optimization and
consolidation**, and clearly communicate costs to IT and business stakeholders. End User
Devices also standardizes analysis of device inventory, age, compliance, and refresh planning,
enabling consistent evaluation of current state, future demand, and estimated refresh costs
across the end user computing environment.

## Personas

- **IT Finance**
- Monitors end user device spend, budget performance, and key cost drivers.
- **IT Asset Manager**
- Manages device inventory, lifecycle, and identifies excess or unused assets.
- **Desktop Support / EUC Manager**
- Oversees device compliance, refresh planning, and operational readiness.
- **Service Owner (End User Services)**
- Understands cost to deliver client compute services and drives optimization.

## Use Cases

- **Fleet Spend Visibility**
- Consolidate OpEx and CapEx associated with end user devices across cost centers,
  services, and device types to understand total cost of ownership and key cost
  drivers.
- **Device Cost Allocation**
- Allocate end user device costs to End User Services and business-specific services,
  enabling accurate chargeback/showback and cost transparency.
- **Device Age & Refresh Analysis**
- Analyze device lifecycle, end-of-life status, and estimated refresh costs to support
  proactive planning and budgeting.
- **Inventory & Utilization Insights**
- Gain visibility into device assignments, usage patterns, and location to identify
  excess capacity and underutilized assets.
- **Multiple & Orphan Device Identification**
- Identify users with multiple devices and orphaned or inactive devices to enable
  reclamation and reallocation, reducing overall fleet costs.
- **Compliance Monitoring**
- Track device compliance with operating system and hardware standards to identify
  non-compliant devices and plan remediation or replacement.
- **Optimization & Cost Reduction**
- Identify opportunities to standardize, consolidate, and right-size the device fleet to
  lower run-rate costs and improve cost efficiency.

## Outcomes

- Clear understanding of end user device cost drivers, trends, and variances.
- Accurate calculation and allocation of end user device total cost of ownership
  (TCO).
- Improved fleet optimization through visibility into device age, usage, and
  compliance.
- Better-informed planning and forecasting for device refresh and replacement.
- Reduced costs by identifying excess, orphaned, and underutilized devices.
- Stronger support for investment and standardization decisions using lifecycle and TCO
  insights.
