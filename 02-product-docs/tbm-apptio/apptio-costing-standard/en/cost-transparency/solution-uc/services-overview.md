# Services Overview

## Overview

Services provides comprehensive visibility into IT spending on services delivered to the
business, including cost, consumption, and performance against plan. It enables
organizations to understand the true total cost of service delivery by consolidating all
underlying technology costs that support each service.

This capability brings together infrastructure, applications, labor, vendor, and platform
costs to present a complete and transparent view of service TCO. By standardizing how
service costs are reviewed and communicated, Services supports service portfolio
optimization, service-based showback and chargeback, and stronger alignment between IT
investment decisions and business value.

## Personas

- IT Finance

  Reviews service spend, monitors budget performance, and analyzes
  service-level cost drivers to support showback, chargeback, and investment decisions.
- Service or Solution Owner

  Owns the cost, consumption, and performance of services and
  uses service-level insights to optimize efficiency, pricing, and lifecycle decisions.
- Enterprise Architecture

  Evaluates service rationalization opportunities and alignment
  of services with target architectures and investment strategies.
- Business Relationship Manager

  Uses service cost and consumption insights to
  communicate IT value, support demand discussions, and improve transparency with business
  stakeholders.

## Use Cases

- Fully Burdened Solution Offering TCO

  Calculate complete TCO for solution offerings by
  lifecycle phase, including labor, infrastructure, vendors, and platform services, to
  support informed investment decisions.
- Unit Cost Analysis for Efficiency

  Compute per-unit or per-user service costs to
  support rate setting, demand planning, and ongoing efficiency improvement.
- Solution Offering Lifecycle Management

  Assess solution offering costs across
  lifecycle stages to guide upgrade, retirement, or replacement decisions and align
  spending with investment objectives such as Tolerate, Invest, Migrate, or Eliminate.
- Cost Optimization and Rationalization

  Analyze fully burdened service TCO across
  labor, vendors, infrastructure, and platform services to identify actionable cost
  reduction and efficiency opportunities.
- Communicate IT Spend (Showback)

  Provide transparent visibility into the cost and
  usage of solution offerings, including products and services, to drive accountability
  and responsible consumption.
- Peer Benchmarking

  Compare service costs against internal or industry benchmarks to
  identify inefficiencies, optimization opportunities, and areas for continuous
  improvement.

## Outcomes

- Clear and defensible view of service-level total cost of ownership.
- Improved transparency and communication of IT spend to business stakeholders.
- Stronger service portfolio governance and rationalization decisions.
- Actionable insights to reduce service delivery costs and improve efficiency.
- Better alignment of IT services with business value and consumption patterns.
- Data-driven support for showback, chargeback, and service investment planning.
