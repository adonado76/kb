# Report overview - Monthly TCO reports (v12.3.3)

Note: Applies to: [Apptio Costing Standard](../reports/costtransparencyreports.html) or [Apptio Cloud
Cost Management](../reports/costtransparencyreports.html) running on TBM Studio v12.3.3.

## Overview

The Monthly TCO reports provide an understanding your organization's total monthly spending on
public cloud services. You can gain a much deeper understanding of which organizations and
applications are driving the most public cloud spend, which types of cloud services are being
consumed, and what other, non-provider costs (for example, Labor, Software, and others) are
associated with consuming those services. The reporting includes organizational, application, and
service type slicers to enable filtering for specific areas in order to drill down deeper into
public cloud costs.

## KPIs

- ***IaaS & PaaS Spend YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud
  IaaS & PaaS services, including not just public cloud provider costs, but also those
  non-provider costs incurred as a direct result of adopting public cloud services.
- ***IaaS & PaaS Spend Current Month***- The current month, fully-burdened costs of
  public cloud IaaS & PaaS services.
- ***Cloud Compute YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud computer
  services.
- ***Cloud Compute Prev YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud
  computer services for the prior year.
- ***Cloud Network YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud network
  services.
- ***Cloud Network Prev YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud
  network services for the prior year.
- ***Cloud Storage YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud storage
  services.
- ***Cloud Storage Prev YTD***- The year-to-date, fully-burdened costs of public cloud
  storage services for the prior year.
