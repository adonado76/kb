# View and Edit Your User Properties

Your user properties include your full name, email address, password, and current role.
To edit your user properties, click your user name in the upper-right corner of the application
browser window.

- Applies to: Costing Standard 11.8.x running on either TBM Studio v12 or TBM Studio v11.

When your Apptio administrator created your Apptio account, he entered your user properties
including:

Email
:   Your application user name. Use this name to log in to the application.

Full Name
:   Your first and last name. This name is displayed in the user list on the Administration
    tab.

Currency
:   Your preferred currency when viewing reports.

Locale
:   Your preferred locale used to determine the format of the numbers displayed in the reports.

Current Role
:   Your role determines the actions you can perform in the application. If your Apptio
    administrator has enabled Available Roles for you, you can assign yourself to different role.

## Display your user properties

To display your user properties:

1. Click the down arrow next to your user name in the global header.

   The Change User
   Properties dialog is displayed as shown in the following image.

   ![Change User Properties diaglog](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/viewedituserproperties_1.png)

## Change your role

If your Apptio administrator has enabled Available Roles for you, you can change your role by
clicking the down arrow in the Current Role field and selecting a role. For a description of the
roles, see Defining Roles in the Administrator Guide.
