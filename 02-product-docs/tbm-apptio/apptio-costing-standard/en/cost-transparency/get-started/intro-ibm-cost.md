# Introduction to IBM Apptio Costing

IBM Apptio Costing Standard is the core IT Financial Management solution that enables
Technology, Finance, and Business teams to understand, manage, and optimize the full cost of
their technology. Costing Standard transforms raw financial, operational, and consumption data
into trusted financial intelligence.

With Costing Standard, organizations can accurately allocate technology spend, uncover the
drivers of cost, and link those costs to solutions and business outcomes. This enables teams
to make informed investment decisions, identify optimization opportunities, and improve the
financial performance of their technology landscape; all with a consistent and governed model.

IBM Apptio Costing Standard works seamlessly across the broader IBM Apptio platform, powering
higher-level capabilities such as planning, billing and benchmarking. Whether you are
modernizing your technology cost structure, supporting a chargeback/showback model, or
preparing financial insights for executives, Costing Standard serves as the system of record
for translating technology spend into business value.

## Core Capabilities

**Financial Use Cases:** Gain complete visibility into technology financials, including
labor, vendors, projects, fixed assets, and public cloud; to accurately allocate spend and
understand the total cost of your technology.

**Infrastructure Use Cases:** Analyze cost, consumption, and unit economics across core
technology domains such as Data Centers, Compute, Storage, Network, End-User Devices, Telco
and Service Desk.

**Solution Use Cases:** Understand the full cost and performance of Applications,
Services, Products, and Business Units, enabling chargeback/showback, investment decisions,
and value conversations.

**Strategic Technology Domains:** Extended transparency into high-value technology domains
such as Mainframe, AI and Hybrid IT, supported with dedicated models and insightful reporting.

**Cost Modeling & Allocation:** Create a complete, end-to-end view of technology costs
by integrating financial, operational, and consumption data. Allocate and model costs across
the various layers (financial, resource towers, infrastructure, solutions, and business units)
using IBM Apptio’s best-in-class Apptio Technology Business ManagementUnified (ATUM)
methodologies.

**Analytics & Reporting:** Explore standardized dashboards and domain-specific reports
that surface cost trends, drivers, and optimization opportunities across your technology
landscape.

**Integrations:** Connect seamlessly with IBM Apptio Planning, IBM Cloudability, and other
key enterprise data sources to automate ingestion, validation, and continuous updates.

**Administration:** Manage users, roles, permissions, upgrades, and project configuration
to ensure your Costing Standard environment remains accurate, up to date, and aligned with
organizational needs.
