# Storage Overview

## Overview

Many organizations struggle with limited transparency into storage usage and cost across
thousands of devices, applications, and environments. This lack of visibility makes it
difficult to understand allocated versus used capacity, application-level consumption,
reliability versus cost trade-offs, and storage lifecycle status, often resulting in
overprovisioning, inefficient tiering, and higher-than-necessary storage costs. Apptio
Costing Standard addresses these challenges by providing clear visibility into storage
usage, allocation, and cost across on-premises and hybrid environments, enabling
organizations to manage storage more effectively and align spending with operational and
financial objectives.

## Personas

• IT Finance

• Service Owners

• Infrastructure and Storage Owners

• FinOps or Cloud Center of Excellence (CCoE)

## Use Cases

**Supported out-of-the-box:**

- Storage Usage and Efficiency - Analyze storage usage by platform, tier, and device to
  identify underutilized capacity, optimize refresh cycles, and improve operational
  efficiency.
- Optimize Hybrid Infrastructure - Identify cost and performance optimization
  opportunities across on-premises and cloud storage environments, and communicate clear
  cost and performance benefits of right-sizing to application owners.
- Track and Communicate Hybrid Infrastructure Cost Savings - Capture and report projected
  and realized storage cost optimizations to provide transparency and measurable proof of
  value across stakeholders.
- Hybrid Infrastructure TCO - Monitor and compare storage costs across on-premises and
  public cloud environments to understand total cost of ownership and support migration and
  modernization initiatives.

## Outcomes

- Improved visibility into storage usage, allocation, and cost across devices,
  applications, and environments.
- Reduced storage costs through identification of overprovisioned, underutilized, or
  mis-tiered capacity.
- Better alignment between storage spend, reliability requirements, and operational
  strategy.
- Improved storage lifecycle management, reducing end-of-life risk and unplanned refresh
  costs.
- Stronger decision support for storage optimization, consolidation, and cloud migration
  initiatives.

## Available Storage Solutions

IBM Apptio offers two Storage solutions to support cost transparency, capacity analysis,
and optimization across on-premises, public cloud, and hybrid storage environments. Both
solutions address storage cost visibility, but differ in depth, analytical maturity, and
operational insight.

**Storage Cost Overview (CT Apps – Storage)**

The Storage Cost Overview solution provides a foundational view of storage spend and total
cost of ownership across the enterprise. It is designed for organizations seeking quick
visibility into storage costs, addressable versus used capacity, and high-level storage
economics without deep operational complexity. This solution supports early optimization
discussions, budget alignment, and storage lifecycle awareness through a focused set of
reports, including Infrastructure & Cloud Summary and Storage TCO.

It helps organizations understand where storage costs are incurred, how spend is
distributed across on-premises and cloud storage, and how storage usage aligns with business
environments such as Production, Test, and Development.

To learn more about the Storage Cost Overview solution, go [here](storage-reports.html#storrep__sco).

**Storage Insights & Optimization (BI – Storage Insights)**

The Storage Insights & Optimization solution delivers comprehensive, operationally
rich analytics across the entire storage landscape. It enables detailed analysis of cost,
capacity, utilization, unit costs, and application-level storage consumption across devices,
tiers, and environments. This solution supports advanced optimization, tiering strategies,
lifecycle planning, and hybrid cloud decisions by combining financial, operational, and
utilization metrics with actionable KPIs and deep drill-downs.

It helps answer critical questions such as:

- Whether storage tiers are aligned with application criticality
- Which storage assets are approaching end of life
- How much unallocated or underutilized capacity exists
- What the effective unit cost is for total versus allocated storage
- Where opportunities exist to reclaim capacity and reduce storage spend

To explore the full set of detailed Storage reports and advanced analytics available with the
Storage Insights & Optimization solution, go [here](storage-reports.html#storrep__sip).
