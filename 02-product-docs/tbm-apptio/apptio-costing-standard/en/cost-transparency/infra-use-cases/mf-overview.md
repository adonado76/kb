# Mainframe Overview

## Overview

Mainframes continue to play a critical role in enterprise IT, especially for high-volume,
high-availability transaction workloads that demand reliability, security, and consistent
performance. While modern architectures have evolved, mainframes remain a preferred platform
for core business processing due to their scalability and efficiency.

IBM Apptio Mainframe TCO provides a clear, defensible view of mainframe costs and
utilization, enabling organizations to understand true total cost of ownership, track
consumption accurately, and make informed decisions about optimization, modernization, and
funding. The capability addresses long-standing challenges related to limited cost
transparency, unclear unit economics, and inconsistent cost allocation across applications
and business units.

## Personas

**IT Finance**- Owns mainframe financial governance, tracks total cost of ownership, and
ensures accurate allocation of mainframe costs across consuming applications and business
units.

**Application Owners** - Monitor application-level mainframe consumption, evaluate cost
efficiency, and support optimization or modernization decisions based on usage and unit cost
insights.

**Infrastructure and Platform Owners** - Manage mainframe capacity, utilization, and
performance while balancing cost efficiency and service reliability.

## Use Cases

- **Mainframe Total Cost of Ownership**

  Consolidate hardware, software, labor, and external service costs into a single,
  comprehensive mainframe cost view for budgeting and long-term planning.
- **Mainframe Consumption and Unit-Cost Analysis**

  Analyze mainframe resource consumption relative to business transaction volumes to
  identify high unit-cost workloads and optimization opportunities.
- **Mainframe Cost Allocation by Application**

  Allocate mainframe costs to applications using consumption metrics such as MSUs, Allocated/Used
  Storage and CPU seconds, improving cost accountability and enabling accurate P&L
  reporting.
- **Mainframe Showback and Chargeback**

  Generate transparent showback or chargeback views for mainframe usage to encourage
  responsible consumption and support fair cost recovery models.

## Outcomes

- Improved visibility into total mainframe cost of ownership across hardware, software,
  labor, and supporting services.
- Accurate allocation of mainframe costs to applications and business units using
  defensible, consumption-based drivers.
- Clear understanding of unit cost metrics to support workload optimization,
  modernization, and investment decisions.
- Stronger financial accountability through consistent showback or chargeback for
  mainframe usage.
