# Communications Overview

## Overview

Organizations often lack clear visibility into communication costs such as circuits, voice
services, and network usage across data centers, offices, and business services. This makes
it difficult to control spend, allocate costs fairly, and understand the true cost of
applications and services.

Apptio Costing Standard provides foundational transparency into communication
infrastructure costs by allocating circuits, voice, and usage-based charges to the
appropriate consumers. This enables a clear and defensible view of communication costs
across servers, end-user devices, and business services, supporting accurate application and
service TCO.

## Personas

- IT Finance - Reviews communication spend, validates allocation logic, and ensures costs
  are accurately reflected in financial reporting and TCO models.
- Service Owner - Understands how communication costs contribute to service delivery and
  identifies opportunities to optimize usage and contracts.
- IT Operations - Uses cost visibility to manage communication assets, vendors, and usage
  patterns more effectively.

## Use Cases

**Communication Cost Allocation**

Allocate communication infrastructure costs to servers, end-user devices, and business
services to establish accurate application and service TCO.

## Outcomes

- Improved visibility into communication infrastructure costs across the enterprise.
- Accurate and defensible allocation of communication costs to consuming services and
  applications.
