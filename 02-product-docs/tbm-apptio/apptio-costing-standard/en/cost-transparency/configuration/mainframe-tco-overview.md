# IBM Apptio Mainframe TCO Solution

## Overview

Mainframes continue to play a vital role in enterprise operations and are well-suited for modern
workloads. When it comes to high-transaction workloads, mainframes remain the preferred choice due
to its reliability, efficiency, and security.

## Solution

**IBM Apptio Mainframe TCO**

To maximize the business value of your mainframe environments, it's crucial to understand your
total cost of ownership and accurate utilization. This solution provides a clear and defensible view
of mainframe costs, thereby enabling accurate cost allocation, comprehensive utilization tracking,
and data-driven decision making.

- **Cost Transparency & Allocation**Get a full view of mainframe TCO with
  cost per MSU, GCP/zIIP usage, and spend by application or workload to support accurate allocation
  and reporting.
- ****Cost Pool Composition & Peer Comparison****This report provides a
  detailed breakdown of your mainframe TCO across key cost pools, which include, hardware, software,
  labor, facilities, and outside services.
- **Unit Rates & Consumption Insights**Track GCP and zIIP MSU trends to spot
  underutilization, detect anomalies, and identify patterns to optimize mainframe resource
  utilization.
- **Showback & Chargeback ​**Allocate costs by actual usage and provide
  clear, defensible views to business units.
- **Workload Efficiency**See what's running on your mainframe, identify redundant
  workloads, and reallocate or retire them to optimize resources and improve efficiency.

## Cost Transparency & Allocation – TCO

**Key Benefits**  

- Comprehensive view of total cost and consumption by combining costs, GCP/zIIP MSUs, and
  calculated unit costs in one screen.
- Understand cost and usage trends over time and compare to business volumes and transactional
  unit costs to identify inefficiencies and anomalies.
- Break down key cost drivers across labor, vendors, and applications—including spend by labor,
  vendor, and application.

![Cost Transparency & Allocation TCO](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/ct-allocation.png)

## Cost Pool Composition & Peer Comparison

**Key Benefits**  

- Provides a detailed breakdown of your mainframe TCO across key cost pools, which include,
  hardware, software, labor, facilities, and outside services.
- Benchmark percentages are overlaid to highlight how your cost structure compares to industry
  norms.
- These insights help you to identify potential cost optimization areas, validate budget
  assumptions, and support strategic improvement initiatives.

![Benchmark percentage](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/benchmark-costpool.png)

## Cost Transparency & Allocation – Unit Costs & Consumption

**Key Benefits**  

- View mainframe costs and consumption in a flexible table format using filters and optional
  dimensions, metrics, and time.
- View consumption of both GCP and zIIP MSU and compare different types of workload processing.
- Analyze trends across various time periods - monthly, quarterly, or as needed for reporting.
- Export data for further analysis in other tools or dashboards .

![Mainframe Cost Transparency & Allocation Unit Cost](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/mainframe-cost-transparency-unit-cost.png)

## Mainframe Utilization

**Key Benefits**  

- View MSU consumption trends by workload category, helping users track usage across different
  types of processing.
- Track usage by workload type, product line name, product name, and application name.
- Monitor month-over-month MSU consumption with conditional highlights identifying significant
  month-over-month changes.
- Quarter-over-quarter and year-over-year views help understand long-term consumption trends.

![Mainframe Usage](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/mainframe-usage.png)

## Mainframe Showback & Chargeback

**Key Benefits**  

- Connect mainframe usage to costs by application, workload, or business unit.
- Ensure cost allocation reflects true consumption with IBM IntelliMagic Vision data .
- Deliver clear, defensible showback and chargeback views for business units.
- Make cost discussions productive by grounding them in data

![Mainframe Consumption](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/mainframe-consumtion.png)

## Mainframe Utilization & Efficiency Insights

**Key Benefits**  

- Identify mainframe efficiencies through workload-level utilization insights that reveal
  patterns, spikes, and underused capacity to inform smarter planning and usage decisions.
- Track all workloads and applications currently active on the mainframe, along with their MSU
  consumption.
- Track consumption trends by workload type, product line, and application to identify
  inefficiencies.
- Track workload spikes and identify opportunities to improve operational efficiency.

![Mainframe Consumption Metrics](../../../../../03-media/apptio-costing-standard/resources/images/ct_images/mainframe-consumption.png)

## Mainframe TCO – Model View

**Key Benefits**  

- Visualize cost flow from beginning to end in the cost model.
- Trace the underlying cost drivers feeding into the mainframe processor including direct costs
  from the General Ledger (e.g., depreciation) as well as labor and vendor costs.
- Visualize how usage data drives the allocation of mainframe TCO to the business applications.
