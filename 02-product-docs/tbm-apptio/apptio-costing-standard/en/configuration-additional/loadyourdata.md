# Load Your Data

The overall process of how Apptio uses your data includes:

1. Load your data into Apptio.
2. Append and map your data into the corresponding Master Data Set.

![](../../resources/images/ct_images/1424_1.png)

The master data sets are used throughout the Costing Standard application to:

- Back objects in the model
- Support allocation strategies
- Calculate metrics
- Display data on reports
