## Views

Opening the Planning menu lists all the views available for the selected plan section.

- For all plan sections, Expenses captures direct expenses such as labor,
  contracts, assets and other expenses (see [View net expenses](view-net-expense.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
- For departments:
  - Status - Review a plan's status
  - Targets (Departments)- Review and edit OpEx, CapEx, and labor target information
- For projects (Project Financial Planning features must be licensed and enabled in the Company Profile):
  - Labor - Capture labor demand in hours
  - Services - Capture service unit demand by project
  - Ledger - Review all direct and indirect expenses plus revenue (cross
    charges) for departments and projects (see [View net expenses](view-net-expense.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))

- For services (Service Demand Planning features must be licensed and enabled; see [Edit the Company Profile](edit-company-profile.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)")):
  - Incoming Demand - Capture incoming service demand by business units, services, and
    projects (see [Forecast
    indirect service costs](sdp/forecast-indirect-service.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
  - Labor - Request labor for devices (see [Forecast demand for services](sdp/forecast-demand-services.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
  - Component Demand - Capture component demand for services (see [Forecast demand for
    services](sdp/forecast-demand-services.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
  - Service Pricing - Review unit price and calculate the effective unit cost (see [Calculate service unit cost and
    price](sdp/calculate-service-unit.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
  - Ledger - Review all direct and indirect expenses plus revenue (cross-charges) for
    departments, services, and business units (see [View net expenses](view-net-expense.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
- For business units (Service Demand Planning features must be licensed and enabled; see [Edit the Company Profile](edit-company-profile.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)")):
  - Services - Capture service unit demand by business unit (see [Estimate and forecast labor for
    projects and services](pfp/estimate-forecast-labor.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
  - Ledger - Review service costs for business units (see [View net expenses](view-net-expense.dita "(Opens in a new tab or window)"))
