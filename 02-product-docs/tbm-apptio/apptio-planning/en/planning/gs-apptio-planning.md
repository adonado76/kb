# What you can do with Planning

Apptio's Planning and financial management applications include the following:

- Planning - Collaborative budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, and multi-year
  planning purpose-built for IT. Planning includes the following products:
  - Planning
    Foundation - Automate, centralize, and simplify annual budgeting and forecasting.
  - Costing Standard - Build the financial layer of your cost model and perform greater
    in-depth financial and labor analysis.
- Project Financial Planning - An add-on module that provides financial planning at the
  Cost-Object level for people, assets, and vendor spend on projects.
- Service Demand Planning - An add-on module that provides financial planning for “run the
  business” operating costs for services and resources. Service Demand Planning is no longer an
  actively sold module. However, it is documented here for current customers.

## About Planning

Planning helps IT finance professionals align spend with business priorities. Also, Planning
provides granular analysis, the entry of IT financial data, and the transition of data back to your
corporate finance system. Planning automates collaborative budgeting, forecasting, and analysis by
using:

- Variance analysis - Translate cost into IT-relevant categories. Identify and explain IT
  spend and labor variance
- Budget planning - Consolidate OpEx and CapEx budgets, improve budget transparency, and
  increase budget accountability
- Forecasting - Automate the forecast process, perform "what if" analysis, and make
  decisions with real-time cost trends

Planning includes the following applications:

- Planning - Centralize, automate, and track the budget, forecast, and variance analysis
  process
- Costing Standard - Build the financial layer of your cost model and perform greater
  in-depth financial and labor analysis

## About Planning

Planning is used for the following:

- Centralize, automate, and track the annual budget and regular forecasting process
- Conduct regular variance analysis and surface top drivers
- Find opportunities to shift spend to higher priority projects
