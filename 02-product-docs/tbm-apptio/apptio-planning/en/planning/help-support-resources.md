# Help and support resources for Planning

If you are the authorized point of contact in your organization for Apptio support, use
the contact methods provided to you to escalate issues to Apptio Support Services. Otherwise, reach
out to the designated support contact within your organization. This is likely your Budget Process
Owner or Admin.

## Before you begin

All Apptio planning application users have access to the Apptio Community on the TBM Connect
website. There, you can see, ask, and respond to questions about Apptio planning applications and
view instructional and troubleshooting information

## Procedure

- **Here are some starting points :**

  - Visit the [Planning Knowledge Base](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/apptio/communities/community-home?CommunityKey=4100dfb8-fc23-4203-83c7-019253cf7c0b "(Opens in a new tab or window)") for troubleshooting KB articles,
    announcements, discussions, and trainings.
  - Visit [Planning applications video and training resources](https://education.apptio.com/learn/course/it-financial-management-foundation-learning-plan "(Opens in a new tab or window)") to see
    Apptio planning applications in action.
  - Vote on [product feature ideas](https://community.apptio.com/participate/ideation-home "(Opens in a new tab or window)") submitted by other customers or submit your own
- Your organization's designated support contact can also use the following
  resources:

  - Use the Support Help Center (https://support.apptio.com/s/) to submit a support request.
