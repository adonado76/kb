# Delegate Asset Cost

On the Expenses > Summary page, generated expenses have the Asset Purchase amount allocated to
Cost Center selected on the asset line, and the Depreciation amount allocated to the Depreciation
Cost Center selected on the asset line.

## Choose Cost Center for asset depreciation

1. Navigate to Planning > Expenses > Assets tab.
2. Enter an asset expense line.
3. Select the depreciation Cost Center.

This Cost Center is used to generate the asset depreciation expenses.

To learn more about assets, see [Manage assets](../manage-assets.htm "(Opens in a new tab or window)")
