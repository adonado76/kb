# Period picker for line-item tables

## About this task

Period picker allows us to pick/view only certain periods of a plan and hide the rest. It is
useful to view only specific interest of periods or a certain time period on the table. Below are a
few important notes about them.

## Procedure

- The period picker has a vertical Financial Year (FY) selector. On clicking it, two
  drop-downs Start Period and End Period appear. On
  clicking this further, a small window opens a calendar with year and periods/months selector. The
  year drop-down selector shows years up until the chosen FY year starting from start year of the
  plan.
- The custom year can be configured to show FY details between any periods within the plan
  years.
- Time period picker is applicable to Expenses tables and to graphs
  on Summary page.
- If a user wants to know the expenses for the first quarter, they must select the
  available fiscal year from the year drop-down, Start
  Period as January and End Period as March. The table will show
  the first quarter periods from Jan-Mar with Total FY column.
- In a multi-year plan, the period picker has different options to choose present year,
  next year(s) or it can be custom configured to show FY details.
- Time period pickers can be configured to show any periods of the year within the plan
  years.
