# Reports overview

Planning includes several Apptio Costing Standard reports for detailed variance
analysis.

See [Integrate with Cost
Transparency](integrate-ct.html "If your organization runs both Apptio Costing Standard and an Apptio Planning application, you can integrate them to share data.").

Available reports include the following:

- [Cost Pool Analysis report (v104 and later)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_costpoolanalysis104.html "◆ Applies to: Planning and Costing Standard on TBM Studio 12.3 and later, with Template v104 and later")
- [Financial Analysis report (v104 and later)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_financialanalysis104.html "◆ Applies to: Planning and Costing Standard on TBM Studio 12.3 and later, with Template v104 and later")
- [Financial Review (OpEx) report (v107)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_financialreview107.html "◆ Applies to: Planning and Costing Standard on TBM Studio 12.3 and later, with Template v107 and later")
- [Financial Review - CapEx report (v107)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_financialreviewcapexv107.html)
- [Financial Variance Review email report (v104)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_financialvariancereviewemail104.html "◆ Applies to: Planning and Costing Standard on TBM Studio 12.3 and later, with Template v104 and later")
- [ITP - Labor Review report (v104 and later)](../reports-itfmf-ctv104/itfmf-ct_itplaborreview104.html "◆ Applies to: Planning and Costing Standard on TBM Studio 12.3 and later, with Template v104 and later")
