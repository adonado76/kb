# Support Resources

## About this task

Apptio Planning provides a variety of support resources to help you learn, troubleshoot,
and stay informed. These resources connect you with documentation, training, and the broader
Apptio community so you can get the most out of the product.

- [Release Notes:](https://www.ibm.com/docs/en/apptio-commercial/planning-standard/saas?topic=planning-recent-releases "(Opens in a new tab or window)") Stay up to date with the
  latest enhancements. View all *What’s New* posts to track product changes and new
  capabilities.
- [Videos and
  Training:](https://education.apptio.com "(Opens in a new tab or window)")Access self-paced training resources, including video tutorials,
  webinar recordings, and guided walkthroughs to build your skills.
- [Community Q&A:](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/groups/community-home?communitykey=4100dfb8-fc23-4203-83c7-019253cf7c0b "(Opens in a new tab or window)")Browse questions and
  answers from other Apptio users, or post your own to get help from peers and experts.
- [Feature
  Ideas:](https://ideas.ibm.com/ "(Opens in a new tab or window)")Help shape the product by submitting new feature requests or voting
  on ideas shared by the community.
- [Release and Maintenance Schedule:](https://community.ibm.com/community/user/viewdocument/apptio-planning-whats-new-cumula?CommunityKey=4100dfb8-fc23-4203-83c7-019253cf7c0b&tab=librarydocuments "(Opens in a new tab or window)")Stay
  informed about upcoming release dates and planned maintenance windows to prepare your
  teams in advance.
- [IBM Support:](https://www.ibm.com/mysupport/s/?language=en_US "(Opens in a new tab or window)") Use the Support portal to submit a support
  request.
